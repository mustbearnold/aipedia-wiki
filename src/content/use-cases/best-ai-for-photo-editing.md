## Top Picks

### 1. Adobe Firefly
Adobe Firefly, powered by custom models trained on licensed data, specializes in photo editing within Adobe apps like Photoshop and Lightroom. It handles generative fill to remove objects, extend backgrounds, and apply realistic relighting; users select areas and describe changes for instant results. For this use case, it wins with commercial safety (no copyright risks) and precision on high-res photos, outperforming general models in professional consistency. Pricing starts free via web app (50 credits/day); full access requires Creative Cloud at $4.99/month (single apps) or $59.99/month (All Apps). Limitations include credit caps on free tier and less flexibility for non-Adobe workflows. As of 2026-04-15, it integrates GPT-5.4 for advanced prompting.[1][3]

### 2. Midjourney v8 alpha
Midjourney v8 alpha, accessed via Discord or web, excels at photo editing through remix and upscale features; upload a photo, use /blend or variation prompts for style transfers, inpainting, or enhancements. It stands out for creative outputs like turning portraits into stylized art while preserving details, with turbo upscaling to 8K. Best for users needing artistic flair over realism. Pricing: $10/month basic (3.3 GPU hours), $30/month standard (15 hours), up to $120/month mega; no free tier post-2025. Drawbacks: Discord reliance slows pros, and outputs require Discord bot for edits. Current as of 2026-04-15, v8 alpha leads image benchmarks.[1][3][5]

### 3. Gemini 3.1 Pro
Gemini 3.1 Pro from Google supports photo editing via its multimodal interface; upload images for object removal, background swaps, or enhancements using text prompts, with native Google Photos integration. It fits users in Google Workspace for quick edits like auto-enhance or generative expand. Pricing: free for basic use; $19.99/month Gemini Advanced unlocks higher limits and GPT-5.4 rivals. Cons: less specialized than Adobe for pros, occasional artifacts in complex edits. Verified 2026-04-15 for image tasks.[2][3][5]

### 4. ChatGPT (GPT-5.4)
ChatGPT with GPT-5.4 handles photo uploads for edits like inpainting, upscaling, and style application through its canvas interface. It analyzes images, suggests crops, or generates variations; suitable for casual users needing one-tool versatility. Pricing: free (limited); $20/month Plus; $200/month Pro for heavy use. Limits: not as refined for pro photography vs. specialists. Current 2026-04-15.[3]
