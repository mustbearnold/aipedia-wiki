# ChatGPT vs Otter.ai

[ChatGPT](../tools/chatgpt.md) provides general conversational AI for text, code, and analysis tasks. [Otter.ai](../tools/otter-ai.md) records meetings and generates transcripts with speaker identification. This comparison uses flagship versions and pricing as of April 15, 2026.

## Quick Answer

ChatGPT suits mixed knowledge work like writing and research. Otter.ai fits meeting transcription and note-taking.

## At a Glance

| | ChatGPT | Otter.ai |
|---|---|---|
| **Flagship** | GPT-5.4 | Otter 3.0 |
| **Price** | Free / Plus $20/mo / Pro $200/mo | Free / Pro $10/user/mo / Business $20/user/mo |
| **Context Window** | 128K tokens input / 16K output | Unlimited audio (2hr max per session) |
| **Best For** | Versatile text and code tasks | Meeting recording and summaries |

## Where ChatGPT Wins

- Handles text generation, code writing, image creation, and web browsing in one interface.[3]
- Supports real-time voice conversations and plugin ecosystem for broad use cases.[3]
- Pro tier at $200/mo includes advanced reasoning modes for complex problem-solving.[3]
- Free tier delivers value for casual queries without payment.[3]
- Integrates browsing for current information synthesis.[6]

## Where Otter.ai Wins

- Records live meetings with real-time transcription and speaker labels.
- Generates automated summaries, action items, and highlights from audio.
- Supports unlimited transcription minutes on paid plans with search across recordings.
- Integrates with Zoom, Google Meet, and Teams for direct capture.
- Business plan adds team collaboration and custom vocabulary at $20/user/mo.

## Key Differences

ChatGPT processes text prompts up to 128K tokens for reasoning and generation tasks, while Otter.ai handles audio inputs up to 2 hours per session for transcription.[3] ChatGPT pricing starts free and scales to $200/mo for Pro access; Otter.ai offers Free with limits, Pro at $10/user/mo for more minutes, and Business at $20/user/mo for teams. ChatGPT excels in general AI applications like coding and research; Otter.ai focuses on meeting productivity with speaker ID and shareable notes.

## Who should choose ChatGPT

Individuals or teams needing one tool for writing, analysis, coding, and multimodal tasks. It works across workflows without audio-specific setup.

## Who should choose Otter.ai

Users in meetings, sales calls, or interviews who require automated transcripts and summaries. It saves time on note-taking in collaborative environments.

## Bottom Line

Pick ChatGPT for everyday AI assistance beyond audio. Choose Otter.ai if meeting capture is core to your routine. Many combine both for full coverage.

## FAQ

**Can I use both?**  
Yes, ChatGPT handles pre-meeting prep or post-analysis; Otter.ai captures live discussions.

**Which is cheaper?**  
ChatGPT Free matches Otter.ai Free for basics; Otter.ai Pro at $10/mo undercuts ChatGPT Plus at $20/mo for transcription focus.

**Which one should I pick first?**  
Start with Otter.ai if meetings dominate your day; otherwise ChatGPT covers more ground.
