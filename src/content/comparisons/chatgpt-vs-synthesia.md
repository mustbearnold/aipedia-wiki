# ChatGPT vs Synthesia

[ChatGPT](../tools/chatgpt.md) generates text, code, images, and supports voice interactions in a single interface. [Synthesia](../tools/synthesia.md) creates videos with AI avatars from scripts or documents for business use. This comparison covers their flagship versions, pricing, and use cases as of April 2026[2][3].

## Quick Answer

ChatGPT suits general tasks like writing and analysis across text, code, and images. Synthesia fits scripted video production for training or marketing.


## Where ChatGPT Wins

- Handles text generation, document analysis, image creation, voice conversations, web browsing, and code execution in one place[3].
- Free tier provides real value; paid plans scale to Pro at $200/mo for heavy use[3].
- Integrates plugins and real-time features for broad workflows[3].
- Processes mixed inputs like documents or data alongside outputs[2].
- Default choice for users needing one tool for multiple categories[3].

## Where Synthesia Wins

- Produces business videos and video agents directly from scripts or documents[2].
- Focuses on avatar-based video for training, marketing, or presentations[2].
- Listed among top tools people use for video-specific tasks[2].
- Avoids generalist limitations by specializing in video output.
- Serves teams repeating video creation weekly.

## Key Differences

ChatGPT offers broad capabilities across text, code, images, and voice with pricing from free to $200/mo; GPT-5.5 supports 1M-token API context, while ChatGPT tier windows are not fully published[3]. Synthesia targets video generation with AI avatars, converting scripts to polished business videos without general text or code features[2]. ChatGPT fits mixed daily tasks; Synthesia addresses repeated video needs.

## Who should choose ChatGPT

Users handling writing, analysis, coding, or images in varied workflows. Teams wanting one interface for multiple AI functions[3].

## Who should choose Synthesia

Businesses producing training videos, marketing clips, or agent videos from text. Groups focused on avatar-driven video output[2].

## Bottom Line

Pick ChatGPT for versatile AI across text and media at clear pricing tiers. Choose Synthesia for dedicated script-to-video production. Many workflows benefit from both for complementary strengths[2][3].

## FAQ

**Can I use both?**  
Yes, ChatGPT for content creation pairs with Synthesia for video output from those scripts[2].

**Which is cheaper?**  
ChatGPT free tier works for light use; Synthesia requires paid plans for full access[3].

**Which one should I pick first?**  
ChatGPT for general needs; Synthesia if video production is primary[3].
