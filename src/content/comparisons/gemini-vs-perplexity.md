# Gemini vs Perplexity

[Gemini](../tools/gemini.md) and [Perplexity](../tools/perplexity.md) serve different primary functions in the AI assistant landscape. Gemini 3.1 Pro excels as a general-purpose multimodal model with deep Google Workspace integration, while Perplexity specializes in cited research and decision support. This comparison covers flagship versions, current pricing, and which tool aligns with your workflow as of April 2026.

## Quick Answer

Choose **Gemini 3.1 Pro** if you need a versatile general-purpose model with the largest context window and Google ecosystem integration. Choose **Perplexity Enterprise** if your primary need is cited research, fact-checking, and decision support with real-time web access.


|---|---|---|
| **Flagship Model** | Gemini 3.1 Pro | Perplexity Enterprise |
| **Pricing** | $2/$12 per million tokens (API); $19.99/month (Advanced) | Enterprise (custom pricing) |
| **Context Window** | 2 million tokens | Not publicly specified |
| **Input/Output** | Text, image, audio, video | Text with cited web sources |
| **Best For** | Multimodal tasks, Google Workspace users, general-purpose work | Research, fact-checking, cited decision support |

## Where Gemini Wins

- **Largest context window**: 2 million tokens allows processing of entire datasets, lengthy PDFs, and hours of video in a single request[3]
- **Multimodal capabilities**: Accepts and generates text, images, audio, and video; Perplexity focuses primarily on text with web citations[3]
- **Google Workspace integration**: Native integration with Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Drive, and Meet makes it the default for organizations already in Google's ecosystem[3][4]
- **Benchmark performance**: Gemini 3.1 Pro leads on raw benchmark scores across the most tests as of February 2026[1]
- **API pricing**: At $2/$12 per million tokens, it offers the best value of any frontier model for developers and agencies[1]

## Where Perplexity Wins

- **Cited research**: Perplexity Enterprise is one of the strongest choices for cited research and decision support, with real-time web access built into every response[4]
- **Fact-checking focus**: Specialized for verification and source attribution, reducing hallucination risk in research workflows[4]
- **Research-first design**: Purpose-built for users who need sources and citations as a core feature, not an afterthought[4]

## Key Differences

Gemini 3.1 Pro is a general-purpose multimodal model that prioritizes breadth of capability and ecosystem fit. It excels at handling diverse input types (text, images, audio, video) and integrates directly into Google's productivity suite. The 2 million token context window is the largest available in mainstream models, making it exceptional for processing large documents and datasets.

Perplexity Enterprise takes a different approach by specializing in research and decision support. Its core strength is providing answers with real-time web citations, making it ideal for workflows where source verification and attribution are non-negotiable. Perplexity is not positioned as a general-purpose replacement for Gemini; instead, it fills a specific niche in research and fact-checking.

For general knowledge work, content creation, coding assistance, and multimodal tasks, Gemini 3.1 Pro is more versatile. For research-heavy workflows where citations and fact-checking are primary requirements, Perplexity Enterprise is the better fit.

## Who Should Choose Gemini

Organizations and individuals already embedded in Google Workspace should prioritize Gemini 3.1 Pro. Its tight integration with Gmail, Docs, Sheets, and Drive removes friction from daily workflows. Developers and agencies building agentic systems benefit from its benchmark performance and API pricing. Users who need to process large documents, analyze datasets, or work with multiple media types will find the 2 million token context window invaluable.

## Who Should Choose Perplexity

Teams whose primary workflow involves research, fact-checking, and decision support should choose Perplexity Enterprise. If your work requires real-time web access, source attribution, and citation trails for every answer, Perplexity's specialized design delivers more value than a general-purpose model. Perplexity is strongest as a complement to other tools rather than a replacement for them.

## Bottom Line

Gemini 3.1 Pro is the more versatile general-purpose choice with superior multimodal capabilities and the largest context window available. Perplexity Enterprise excels in a narrower but important domain: research and cited decision support. For most users, Gemini 3.1 Pro covers more ground; for research-focused teams, Perplexity Enterprise is worth the investment.

## FAQ

**Can I use both?**
Yes. Many teams use Gemini 3.1 Pro for general work, content creation, and multimodal tasks, then switch to Perplexity Enterprise when they need cited research or fact-checking. They serve different purposes and complement each other well.

**Which is cheaper?**
Gemini's API pricing ($2/$12 per million tokens) is lower than Perplexity Enterprise's custom pricing model. However, Gemini Advanced costs $19.99/month for individual users, while Perplexity's consumer pricing is not directly comparable to its enterprise offering.

**Which one should I pick first?**
If you use Google Workspace or need general-purpose AI capabilities, start with Gemini 3.1 Pro. If your primary need is research with citations, start with Perplexity Enterprise. Most users benefit from having both available for different tasks.
