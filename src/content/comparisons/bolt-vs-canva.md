# Bolt.new vs Canva AI

[Bolt.new](../tools/bolt.md) and [Canva AI](../tools/canva.md) compete in the AI design space as of April 2026, with Bolt.new focusing on code-first prototyping and Canva AI on template-driven graphics via Magic Studio.[5] Bolt.new uses GPT-5.3 Codex for full-stack app generation from prompts, while Canva AI integrates Gemini 3.1 Pro and Flux 2 for image/video design.[1][2][5]

## Quick Answer

Canva AI suits non-technical teams for quick graphics and social media; Bolt.new fits developers prototyping web apps via AI code generation. Choice depends on coding needs versus drag-and-drop ease.

## Decision Snapshot
| | Bolt.new | Canva AI |
|---|---|---|
| **Flagship** | GPT-5.3 Codex | Gemini 3.1 Pro + Flux 2 |
| **Price** | $20/mo Pro | Free / $19.99/mo Advanced |
| **Context Window** | 1M tokens | 2M tokens |
| **Best For** | Web app prototyping | Team graphics, Magic Studio |

## Where Bolt.new Wins

- Generates full-stack apps from prompts using GPT-5.3 Codex, ideal for developers skipping boilerplate.[1]
- Handles agentic tasks like multi-step reasoning for complex prototypes at $20/mo.[1][2]
- Integrates code execution and real-time editing for rapid iteration.[2]

## Where Canva AI Wins

- Magic Studio offers template-based design with AI edits, accessible for non-coders.[5]
- Multimodal input (text, image, video) via Gemini 3.1 Pro processes large files like PDFs or hours of video.[2][5]
- Team collaboration in Google Workspace ecosystem at lower $19.99/mo entry.[2][4]

## Key Differences

Bolt.new centers on AI-driven code generation with GPT-5.3 Codex for building deployable web apps, targeting developers who edit code directly; it excels in agentic workflows but requires technical skills.[1][2] Canva AI, through Magic Studio, prioritizes visual design with Gemini 3.1 Pro's 2M token context and Flux 2 image gen, enabling fast team outputs like presentations without coding.[2][5] Pricing starts free for Canva versus Bolt's $20/mo Pro, though Canva's advanced tier matches at $19.99/mo.[2][4]

## Who should choose Bolt.new

Developers and agencies prototyping full apps choose Bolt.new for its GPT-5.3 Codex code gen and 1M context handling complex logic.

## Who should choose Canva AI

Non-technical teams and marketers pick Canva AI for Magic Studio's drag-and-drop AI tools integrated with Gemini 3.1 Pro.

## Bottom Line

Bolt.new leads for code-heavy design prototyping; Canva AI dominates accessible visual workflows. Most users benefit from Canva's free tier and ecosystem fit unless building interactive apps.

## FAQ

**Which is cheaper?**  
Canva AI starts free, with Advanced at $19.99/mo; Bolt.new requires $20/mo Pro.[2][4]

**Which has better output quality?**  
Bolt.new excels in functional apps via GPT-5.3 Codex; Canva AI in polished graphics via Gemini 3.1 Pro.[1][2][5]

**Can I use both?**  
Yes, combine Bolt.new for prototypes and Canva AI for final visuals.
