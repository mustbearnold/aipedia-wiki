# Apollo.io vs Intercom

[Apollo.io](../tools/apollo.md) and [Intercom](../tools/intercom.md) are sales engagement and customer messaging platforms in the ai-automation category as of April 2026. Apollo.io focuses on lead generation, prospecting, and outbound sequences with AI-driven enrichment. Intercom centers on inbound customer support, product tours, and AI chatbots for user engagement.

## Quick Answer

Apollo.io suits sales teams needing lead data and outbound automation. Intercom fits product and support teams prioritizing customer messaging and self-serve tools.

## At a Glance

| | Apollo.io | Intercom |
|---|---|---|
| **Flagship** | Apollo Sequences AI 3.0 | Fin AI Agent |
| **Price** | Free; Basic $49/user/mo; Professional $99/user/mo (annual)[Apollo.io pricing page](https://www.apollo.io/pricing) | Starter $74/mo; Essential $99/mo; Advanced $158/mo (annual)[Intercom pricing page](https://www.intercom.com/pricing) |
| **Best For** | Outbound sales prospecting | Inbound customer support |

## Where Apollo.io Wins

- Provides 275+ million contact database for lead sourcing and enrichment at scale.[Apollo.io features](https://www.apollo.io/product/sequences)
- AI Sequences automate personalized email, call, and LinkedIn outreach with A/B testing.
- Lower entry pricing for individual sales reps starting at $49/user/mo.
- Built-in dialer and meeting scheduler integrate outbound workflows.
- Compliance tools handle TCPA, CASL, and GDPR for cold outreach.

## Where Intercom Wins

- Fin AI Agent resolves 50% of support queries autonomously with custom skills.[Intercom Fin](https://www.intercom.com/fin)
- Messenger and help center enable self-serve user experiences across web, app, and email.
- Product tours and checklists guide onboarding without sales involvement.
- Advanced segmentation targets users by behavior for targeted messaging.
- Higher scalability for enterprise support volumes with SLA guarantees.

## Key Differences

Apollo.io emphasizes outbound sales with a massive B2B contact database and sequence automation, making it direct for prospecting 65 million companies and 275 million contacts. Intercom focuses on inbound retention through AI chat, help articles, and product adoption tools, processing millions of conversations monthly. Apollo starts cheaper for reps ($49/mo) while Intercom scales to advanced plans ($158/mo) for teams needing segmentation and analytics. Apollo integrates CRMs like Salesforce for outbound; Intercom connects to Slack and Zendesk for support.

## Who should choose Apollo.io

Sales development reps and small teams building pipelines from scratch. Growing startups using outbound to hit revenue targets.

## Who should choose Intercom

Customer success managers and product teams driving retention. Enterprises with high inbound volume seeking AI self-serve.

## Bottom Line

Choose Apollo.io for lead generation and outbound scale. Choose Intercom for customer engagement and support efficiency. Many teams combine both: Apollo for top-of-funnel, Intercom for mid-funnel nurturing.
