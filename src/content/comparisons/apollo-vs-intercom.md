# Apollo.io vs Intercom

[Apollo.io](../tools/apollo.md) and [Intercom](../tools/intercom.md) both touch revenue workflows, but they live on opposite sides of the customer lifecycle. Apollo helps teams create pipeline through B2B data, enrichment, sequencing, calling, and CRM sync. Intercom helps teams support, retain, and activate users through Fin AI Agent, Fin AI Copilot, inbox, messenger, help center, and support analytics.

## Quick Answer

Choose **Apollo.io** for outbound prospecting and sales engagement. Choose **Intercom** for AI-first customer support and in-product customer messaging. If your team owns both acquisition and support, these tools are complements, not substitutes.

## Decision Snapshot

| | Apollo.io | Intercom |
|---|---|---|
| **Best buyer** | SDR leaders, AEs, founders, RevOps, and demand-gen teams building outbound pipeline | Support, CX, success, and product teams reducing ticket volume and improving user assistance |
| **Core job** | Find, enrich, contact, and manage prospects | Resolve support questions, route conversations, assist agents, and message users |
| **June 2026 pricing anchor** | Free tier; paid plans scale up to $149/user/month before add-ons and consumption | Public pricing lists seat plans, Fin AI Agent outcome pricing, Fin Copilot at $29/agent/mo, and Pro add-on at $99/mo |
| **AI center of gravity** | AI-assisted prospecting and sales workflow | Fin AI Agent, Fin AI Copilot, and support automation |
| **Primary data surface** | B2B contacts, accounts, firmographics, enrichment, CRM fields | Customer conversations, help articles, tickets, inbox workflows, user events |
| **Avoid if** | You need post-sale support automation more than prospecting | You need a B2B prospect database and outbound sequencer |

## Where Apollo.io Wins

- **Pipeline creation.** Apollo is the right tool when the team needs to identify accounts, find contacts, enrich records, and launch outbound motions.
- **Sales engagement depth.** Sequences, dialer, meeting workflow, CRM sync, and prospect analytics sit inside Apollo's core product job.
- **B2B database leverage.** Intercom is not a prospecting database; Apollo's database and enrichment layer are the reason to buy it.
- **RevOps fit for acquisition.** Apollo is easier to justify when success metrics are sourced pipeline, meetings booked, reply rates, and CRM hygiene.
- **Clearer fit for SDR teams.** Reps can work from prospect lists and sales tasks instead of support conversations.

## Where Intercom Wins

- **AI support automation.** Fin is built to resolve customer questions and assist support agents, not to send outbound sales sequences.
- **In-product customer experience.** Messenger, help center, inbox, tours, and customer messaging are Intercom's durable strengths.
- **Conversation intelligence.** Intercom's data model is richer for post-sale conversations, handoffs, escalation, and support quality.
- **Support-cost economics.** Fin outcome pricing and Copilot pricing need modeling, but the business case is ticket deflection and agent productivity.
- **Better for retention.** If the business problem is onboarding, activation, and support response time, Intercom wins decisively.

## Pricing reality check

Apollo and Intercom price different jobs, so a raw plan comparison is misleading. Apollo spend scales with seats, credits, exports, and engagement usage. Intercom spend scales with seats, Fin AI Agent outcomes, Copilot seats, the Pro add-on, and support volume. Model the KPI each tool can improve before comparing dollars: Apollo should create pipeline; Intercom should reduce support load and improve customer experience.

## Who should choose Apollo.io

Choose Apollo if your next hire or budget line is outbound sales, prospecting, enrichment, or RevOps tooling for pipeline generation.

## Who should choose Intercom

Choose Intercom if your next budget line is support automation, customer messaging, AI agent resolution, or agent-assist tooling.

## Bottom Line

Apollo is an acquisition tool. Intercom is a support and customer-experience platform. The only reason to compare them head-to-head is budget prioritization: do you need more pipeline or better customer support first? For most growing SaaS teams, Apollo feeds prospects into the revenue system while Intercom handles customers after they arrive.

## FAQ

**Which is cheaper?**  
There is no fair one-line answer because Apollo prices outbound sales workflow while Intercom prices support workflow plus AI outcomes. Compare expected pipeline created versus support tickets resolved.

**Which has better AI?**
Intercom has the stronger AI support agent story through Fin. Apollo's AI value is in sales workflow and prospecting assistance rather than autonomous customer support.

**Can I use both?**  
Yes. Apollo can create and enrich opportunities; Intercom can support, onboard, and message customers. Connect them through CRM and lifecycle-event hygiene rather than treating them as replacements.
