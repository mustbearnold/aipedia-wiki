# ChatGPT vs HeyGen

[ChatGPT](../tools/chatgpt.md) provides general-purpose text generation, image creation, voice conversations, and web browsing in one interface. [HeyGen](../tools/heygen.md) specializes in AI video generation with avatar-based talking heads from text scripts. This comparison covers their flagship versions, pricing, and use cases as of April 2026[2].

## Quick Answer

ChatGPT suits mixed tasks like writing, coding, and research; HeyGen fits scripted video production for business communications.

## At a Glance

| | ChatGPT | HeyGen |
|---|---|---|
| **Flagship** | GPT-5.4 | Avatar Video Generator |
| **Price** | Free / Plus $20/mo / Pro $200/mo | Free / Creator $29/mo / Business $89/mo |
| **Context Window / Output Specs** | 128K tokens / Multimodal (text, image, voice) | Script-to-video / 1080p avatars, 120+ languages |
| **Best For** | Versatile knowledge work | Professional video messaging |

## Where ChatGPT Wins

- Handles text, code, images, and voice in unified interface for broad workflows[2].
- Real-time web browsing and plugin support enable current information tasks[2].
- Pro tier at $200/mo unlocks highest performance for complex reasoning[2].
- Free tier provides functional access without commitment[2].

## Where HeyGen Wins

- Generates talking avatar videos from text scripts in minutes.
- Supports 120+ languages and customizable avatars for global business use.
- Templates for marketing, training, and sales videos streamline production.
- Business plan includes team collaboration and brand kits at $89/mo.

## Key Differences

ChatGPT's GPT-5.4 model processes 128K token contexts for analysis and generation across modalities, priced from free to $200/mo for Pro users needing advanced reasoning[2]. HeyGen focuses on video output, converting scripts to avatar performances in 1080p with voiceovers in 120+ languages; its Creator plan starts at $29/mo for individuals, scaling to Business at $89/mo for teams. ChatGPT excels in text-heavy or multimodal tasks; HeyGen targets video-specific needs without general AI capabilities.

## Who should choose ChatGPT

Individuals or teams handling writing, research, coding, or casual multimedia pick ChatGPT for its all-in-one access from free tier up.

## Who should choose HeyGen

Marketers, trainers, or sales teams producing frequent scripted videos select HeyGen to create professional avatar content efficiently.

## Bottom Line

Choose ChatGPT for general AI tasks across text and media; opt for HeyGen when video production from scripts is the priority. Many workflows combine both via ChatGPT's script generation feeding into HeyGen videos.

## FAQ

**Can I use both?**  
Yes, generate scripts in ChatGPT then import to HeyGen for video output.

**Which is cheaper?**  
ChatGPT's free tier works for basics; HeyGen starts at $29/mo for video features.

**Which one should I pick first?**  
Start with ChatGPT for versatile needs, add HeyGen if videos become frequent.
