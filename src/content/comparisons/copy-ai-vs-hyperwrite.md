# Copy.ai vs HyperWrite

[Copy.ai](../tools/copy-ai.md) and [HyperWrite](../tools/hyperwrite.md) are AI writing assistants that generate text for marketing, blogs, and productivity tasks as of April 2026. Copy.ai focuses on marketing copy with templates, while HyperWrite emphasizes real-time writing assistance across apps.

## Quick Answer

HyperWrite edges out for general writing and productivity due to its browser extension and lower entry price; Copy.ai suits marketing teams needing branded templates.

## At a Glance

| | Copy.ai | HyperWrite |
|---|---|---|
| **Flagship** | GPT-5.3 Codex | Claude Opus 4.6 |
| **Price** | Free; Pro $49/mo; Team $249/mo | Free; Premium $19.99/mo; Ultra $44.99/mo |
| **Context Window** | 128K tokens | 1M tokens |
| **Best For** | Marketing copy, templates | Real-time writing, long docs |

## Where Copy.ai Wins

- Offers 90+ templates for ads, emails, and social posts, speeding up repetitive marketing tasks.[https://www.copy.ai/pricing]
- Brand voice training locks in consistent tone across outputs for teams.[https://www.copy.ai/features]
- Team plans include unlimited words and collaboration for agencies.[https://www.copy.ai/pricing]
- Integrates with Zapier and HubSpot for marketing workflows.[https://www.copy.ai/integrations]

## Where HyperWrite Wins

- Chrome extension provides autocomplete in Gmail, Google Docs, and any web field for seamless use.[https://www.hyperwriteai.com/extension]
- 1M token context handles full documents or datasets without splitting.[1]
- Lower premium tier at $19.99/mo gives Claude Opus 4.6 access for long-form writing.[https://www.hyperwriteai.com/pricing]
- TypeAhead and Personalize features adapt to user style over time.[https://www.hyperwriteai.com/features]

## Key Differences

Copy.ai structures around pre-built workflows for short-form marketing content, using GPT-5.3 Codex for quick generation within template limits. HyperWrite acts as an always-on assistant via browser integration, leveraging Claude Opus 4.6's 1M context for editing long documents or emails in place. Copy.ai's higher pricing targets teams with collaboration needs; HyperWrite's tiers scale for individuals with broader app support.

## Who should choose Copy.ai

Marketing teams or solopreneurs generating high-volume ads and social content benefit from its templates and brand controls. Agencies with multiple users find value in team seats and integrations.

## Who should choose HyperWrite

Writers, researchers, or professionals needing inline suggestions across apps prefer its extension and large context. Solo users save with the $19.99/mo premium plan.

## Bottom Line

Choose HyperWrite for versatile, everyday writing assistance at lower cost. Pick Copy.ai if marketing templates and team features match your needs. Both integrate top models like GPT-5.3 Codex and Claude Opus 4.6, so test free tiers for fit.[1]
