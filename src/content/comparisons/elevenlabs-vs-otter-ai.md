# ElevenLabs vs Otter.ai

[ElevenLabs](/tools/elevenlabs/) and [Otter.ai](/tools/otter-ai/) are both audio tools, but they sit on opposite sides of the workflow. ElevenLabs generates speech from text and powers synthetic voice output. Otter.ai captures live or recorded meetings and turns speech into transcripts, summaries, action items, and searchable team memory.

## Quick Answer

Choose ElevenLabs when you need to create audio: voiceovers, narration, dubbing, cloned voices, sound effects, music, or voice-agent speech. Choose Otter.ai when you need to capture audio: meetings, interviews, sales calls, action items, custom AI workflows, and searchable conversation history.

They are not substitutes. A training company might use Otter to capture expert interviews, then use ElevenLabs to turn edited scripts into polished narration.

## Winner By Use Case

- **Video narration, ads, audiobooks, podcasts:** ElevenLabs.
- **Meeting notes, transcripts, summaries, action items:** Otter.ai.
- **Voice agent spoken output:** ElevenLabs.
- **Meeting bot and recurring team memory:** Otter.ai.
- **Dubbing or multilingual generated speech:** ElevenLabs.
- **Sales or internal meeting workflows:** Otter.ai, especially when Business or Enterprise controls are needed.
- **End-to-end content pipeline:** Use Otter for capture and ElevenLabs for finished audio.

## Where ElevenLabs Wins

ElevenLabs wins when the output is something a listener will hear later. Its current model help identifies Eleven v3 as the advanced expressive speech model and Flash v2.5 as the ultra-low-latency model for speed-sensitive use. Current API pricing lists Flash/Turbo TTS at $0.05 per 1K characters and Multilingual v2/v3 at $0.10 per 1K characters.

Otter does not try to compete there. It does not provide the same creator voice library, voice cloning, dubbing, sound effects, music, or voice-agent output stack. If the job is to make a product, video, ad, course, or app speak, ElevenLabs is the tool to test.

## Where Otter.ai Wins

Otter.ai wins when the audio already exists and needs to become usable knowledge. Current pricing lists Basic, Pro, Business, and Enterprise routes. Business is positioned for medium-sized teams with unlimited meetings and in-app recordings, custom AI workflows, unlimited audio/video imports, up to 4 hours per meeting, admin features, usage analytics, prioritized support, and three concurrent meetings.

Enterprise adds sales notetaker and custom integrations, SSO/SCIM/domain capture, enterprise controls, HIPAA add-on, Otter API and webhooks, and future connectors such as AI Chat with Gmail, Google Drive, Notion, and Jira. That is meeting memory and operations software, not a voice generator.

## Pricing And Procurement

ElevenLabs budgeting is based on generated usage: characters, credits, models, voice clones, dubbing, speech-to-text, agent traffic, concurrency, and commercial rights.

Otter budgeting is based on seats, meeting volume, imports, concurrent meetings, team admin controls, integrations, API/webhooks, retention, consent posture, and whether Enterprise security or HIPAA add-on requirements apply.

The cheaper tool depends on the problem. Otter can be cheaper for meeting memory because it is priced around teams and meetings. ElevenLabs can be cheaper or more expensive for generated speech depending on model, volume, retries, and production needs.

## Risk Checklist

- **ElevenLabs risk:** cloned-voice consent, likeness rights, synthetic disclosure, commercial plan fit, and credit/API usage.
- **Otter risk:** recording consent, workspace sharing defaults, meeting retention, sensitive transcripts, CRM handoff, and admin governance.

Those risks are different enough that procurement should review them separately.

## Who Should Choose ElevenLabs

Choose ElevenLabs if the output is audio people will hear: narration, product voice, voice agents, dubbing, podcasts, audiobooks, game dialogue, training narration, sound effects, or music.

Skip ElevenLabs if the main pain is forgetting what happened in meetings.

## Who Should Choose Otter.ai

Choose Otter.ai if the main pain is capturing meetings, summarizing decisions, assigning follow-ups, searching call history, importing recordings, and turning conversations into team knowledge.

Skip Otter if you need the app to speak with a synthetic voice.

## Bottom Line

ElevenLabs speaks. Otter listens. Buy ElevenLabs for generated voice. Buy Otter for meeting capture and memory. Use both when a workflow needs interviews or calls to become polished audio content later.

## FAQ

**Can Otter.ai replace ElevenLabs?**
No. Otter captures and summarizes speech; ElevenLabs generates speech.

**Can ElevenLabs replace Otter.ai?**
No for meeting workflows. ElevenLabs has speech-to-text capabilities, but Otter is the meeting assistant with summaries, workflows, team controls, and meeting memory.

**Which should I buy first?**
Buy Otter first if meetings dominate the workload. Buy ElevenLabs first if content, products, or agents need to speak.

**Can I use both together?**
Yes. Capture source calls in Otter, edit the transcript into a script, then create polished narration in ElevenLabs.
