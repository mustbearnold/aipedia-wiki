# ChatGPT vs Fireflies.ai

[ChatGPT](../tools/chatgpt.md) provides general-purpose AI for text, code, and multimodal tasks. [Fireflies.ai](../tools/fireflies.md) records meetings, transcribes audio, and generates summaries. This comparison covers their flagship versions, pricing, and use cases as of April 2026[1][2].

## Quick Answer

ChatGPT suits broad tasks like writing and analysis; Fireflies.ai fits meeting transcription and note-taking. Choose based on whether you need a versatile assistant or automated meeting records.


## Where ChatGPT Wins

- Handles text generation, image creation, voice conversations, web browsing, and code execution in one interface[2].
- Supports plugin ecosystem for extended functions like data analysis[2].
- Free tier delivers value for basic use; Pro tier at $200/mo unlocks highest capabilities[2].
- Integrates broadly for knowledge work beyond meetings[5].
- Tops versatility rankings across categories[2].

## Where Fireflies.ai Wins

- Automatically records, transcribes, and summarizes Zoom, Google Meet, and Teams calls.
- Provides searchable transcripts and action items for teams.
- Stores unlimited meeting history with easy collaboration features.
- Costs less per user at $18/mo for Pro plan focused on meetings.
- Reduces manual note-taking in sales, HR, and customer support workflows.

## Key Differences

ChatGPT's GPT-5.5 model processes diverse inputs with a 1M-token API context (ChatGPT tier windows not fully published) for general reasoning and creation, priced from free to $200/mo[2]. Fireflies.ai specializes in audio capture from meetings, offering transcription accuracy over 90% and integration with calendars, billed at $18/user/mo for Pro. ChatGPT excels in standalone tasks; Fireflies.ai embeds into video calls for real-time automation. Output from ChatGPT includes generated content; Fireflies.ai delivers structured notes and highlights.

## Who should choose ChatGPT

Individuals or teams needing one tool for writing, coding, research, and multimedia. Best if your work involves creation over recording[2][5].

## Who should choose Fireflies.ai

Remote teams with frequent calls requiring transcripts and insights. Ideal for sales, management, and support roles focused on meetings.

## Bottom Line

Neither tool replaces the other; ChatGPT handles general AI needs while Fireflies.ai streamlines meeting workflows. Use ChatGPT for daily productivity; add Fireflies.ai if calls dominate your schedule. Many combine both for full coverage.

## FAQ

**Can I use both?**  
Yes, ChatGPT for general tasks pairs with Fireflies.ai for meeting-specific automation without overlap[2].

**Which is cheaper?**  
Fireflies.ai Pro at $18/user/mo undercuts ChatGPT Plus at $20/mo, but ChatGPT free tier costs nothing[2].

**Which one should I pick first?**  
Start with ChatGPT for broad use; switch to Fireflies.ai if you hold 5+ meetings weekly[2].
