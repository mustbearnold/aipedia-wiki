# ElevenLabs vs WellSaid Labs

[ElevenLabs](../tools/elevenlabs.md) and [WellSaid Labs](../tools/wellsaid.md) generate realistic AI voices for text-to-speech, voiceovers, and agents. This comparison covers flagship models, pricing as of April 15, 2026, and workflow fit based on verified data.

## Quick Answer

ElevenLabs leads for broad text-to-speech and voice agent use with more voices and generation speed. WellSaid Labs suits professional voiceovers needing studio-grade narration control.

## At a Glance

| | ElevenLabs | WellSaid Labs |
|---|---|---|
| **Flagship** | Eleven Multilingual v3 | Studio 2.0 |
| **Price** | Free / Starter $5/mo / Creator $22/mo / Pro $99/mo | Creator $49/mo / Pro $199/mo / Enterprise custom |
| **Best For** | Voice agents, multilingual TTS, rapid prototyping | Studio voiceovers, branded narration, enterprise audio |

## Where ElevenLabs Wins

- Supports 70+ languages with consistent quality in flagship Multilingual v3 model.[3]
- Free tier includes 10,000 characters/mo for testing voice cloning and agents.
- Faster generation at scale for podcasts, games, and real-time apps.
- Voice design tools allow custom voices from short audio samples.
- Integrates with apps like ChatGPT for conversational AI voices.

## Where WellSaid Labs Wins

- Studio-focused controls for pitch, pace, and emotion in professional narration.
- Branded voice library with exclusive talent for consistent corporate audio.
- Higher fidelity output for ads, e-learning, and broadcast without post-production.
- Team collaboration features for shared projects and approvals.
- Enterprise plans include dedicated support and SOC 2 compliance.

## Key Differences

ElevenLabs prioritizes volume and versatility, with its Multilingual v3 handling diverse accents and real-time synthesis at lower costs starting from free tiers up to $99/mo Pro. WellSaid Labs emphasizes precision for studio work, using Studio 2.0 for nuanced delivery but at higher entry pricing from $49/mo without a free option. ElevenLabs generates quicker for iterative workflows; WellSaid excels in final polish for client-facing audio.

## Who should choose ElevenLabs

Solo creators, developers, and teams building voice apps or multilingual content. Its low entry price and speed fit prototyping and scaling.

## Who should choose WellSaid Labs

Agencies and enterprises producing high-end voiceovers for marketing or training. Controls and compliance justify the premium for polished results.

## Bottom Line

Pick ElevenLabs for affordable, fast TTS across languages and agents. Choose WellSaid Labs for pro-grade narration with fine controls. Test both free trials to match your audio needs.

## FAQ

**Can I use both?**  
Yes, combine ElevenLabs for drafts and agents with WellSaid for final voiceovers.

**Which is cheaper?**  
ElevenLabs, with free tier and plans from $5/mo versus WellSaid's $49/mo start.

**Which one should I pick first?**  
ElevenLabs for most users due to free access and broader capabilities.
