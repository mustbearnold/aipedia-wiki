# Beautiful.ai vs Tome

[Beautiful.ai](/tools/beautiful-ai/) is the right current choice over [Tome](/tools/tome/) if your priority is polished business slides, brand guardrails, and layouts that do not fall apart when a teammate edits them. Tome is no longer an active AI presentation product. The official Tome Help Center says Tome Slides was sunsetting on April 30, 2025, that users should migrate important work before that date, and that users would not be able to sign in or access Tome content after the sunset.

The practical buyer answer: **use Beautiful.ai when you wanted Tome's AI creation speed but need a professional slide system that still exists. Do not shortlist Tome for new presentation work.** This comparison was re-verified on **May 8, 2026** against current Beautiful.ai, Tome, VentureBeat, and Lightfield sources.

## Quick Verdict

**Winner: Beautiful.ai.** Beautiful.ai's current AI presentation and pricing pages describe an active product with Smart Slides, AI-assisted deck generation, custom branding, themes, fonts, logos, footers, PowerPoint import/export paths, viewer analytics, Team and Enterprise controls, and a current paid purchase path. That makes it the better choice for teams that care about presentation quality and brand consistency.

**Tome is not a live alternative.** The previous version of this page treated Tome as if it still had a Pro plan, active multimedia roadmap, and current AI model stack. That was a trust problem. The product has been sunset, and the founding team is now building Lightfield, an AI-native CRM, not a presentation app.

## Why Tome Is Not A Current Choice

Tome's official sunset article says the team was sunsetting Tome Slides after several years of work and moving in a different direction as a company. It also says users needed to preserve important work before April 30, 2025, active subscriptions would be cancelled, upgrades and new signups would be paused, and user data would be deleted after April 30.

That makes this page a migration page, not a normal tool-vs-tool shootout. The right question is not whether Tome's old narrative canvas was elegant. It was. The right question is whether a buyer in 2026 should use Tome for new work. The answer is no.

## Where Beautiful.ai Wins

Beautiful.ai wins when Tome users need something more slide-native and business-safe than a narrative page generator. Its Smart Slides are designed to keep layouts aligned and visually consistent while users change copy, visuals, and structure. That matters for sales decks, client proposals, internal updates, board slides, training decks, and reports where an ugly slide can damage credibility.

Beautiful.ai also gives teams a clearer brand-control story. Its current pages describe themes, fonts, logos, footers, custom AI image styles, custom branding, viewer analytics, PDF export, and editable PowerPoint export. Those are practical replacement features for teams that liked Tome's speed but need stronger control over professional presentation output.

Beautiful.ai is especially strong if you want:

- Smart Slides that automatically manage layout.
- Brand-safe themes, logos, fonts, and footers.
- Business decks that should look polished with less manual design work.
- PowerPoint import/export support in a slide-first workflow.
- Current pricing, support, and a product roadmap.

## Where Tome Used To Win

Tome used to win when users wanted a story-first AI canvas. It felt more fluid than conventional slides and worked well for founders, creators, and operators who wanted to communicate an idea quickly. That history still matters because it explains the search demand for "Beautiful.ai vs Tome."

But the former advantage is now only historical. Beautiful.ai may feel more structured than Tome did, but that structure is exactly why it is safer for business presentations in 2026. A living product with brand controls beats a discontinued product with a memorable format.

## Pricing Reality

Beautiful.ai has current pricing. On May 8, 2026, AiPedia verified the official Beautiful.ai pricing page, which lists Pro, Team, and Enterprise paths plus plan-level features such as unlimited AI content generation, Smart Slides, branding, PowerPoint import, viewer analytics, PDF export, and editable PowerPoint export. Buyers should still check the live pricing page before purchase because vendor plans can change.

Tome Slides has no current presentation pricing. Any old Free/Pro/Enterprise comparison belongs to the discontinued product. Do not treat historical Tome prices as a valid 2026 procurement option.

## Buyer Recommendations

Choose Beautiful.ai if:

- Your old Tome use case now needs polished business decks.
- You care more about brand consistency than open-ended canvas freedom.
- You build client proposals, board updates, training decks, sales decks, or executive reports.
- You want AI generation plus slide layout guardrails.
- You need an active product with current pricing and support.

Do not choose Tome if:

- You are starting any new presentation workflow.
- You need current signups, paid plans, support, or recoverable workspaces.
- You are relying on old reviews that predate the April 2025 sunset.
- You need a team workflow that will still exist next quarter.

## What To Avoid

- **Do not buy based on fake model claims.** The previous page named model versions and product versions that were not verified by official sources.
- **Do not assume Tome can still replace PowerPoint.** Tome Slides was sunset and is not a current tool choice.
- **Do not pick Beautiful.ai if you mainly want narrative web pages.** Gamma is closer to Tome's original story-first feel.
- **Do not skip export testing.** If you need PPTX, PDF, or client handoff, test a real deck before standardizing.

## Bottom Line

Beautiful.ai is the active choice for former Tome users who now need professional, polished, brand-controlled slides. Tome is historical context. It should not be treated as an active 2026 buying option.

If you wanted Tome because it felt creative and story-first, also compare [Gamma](/tools/gamma/). If you wanted Tome for sales sharing and analytics, compare [Pitch](/tools/pitch/). If you need business decks that stay visually clean, start with Beautiful.ai.

## FAQ

**Is Beautiful.ai better than Tome?**

For current buyers, yes. Beautiful.ai is active and supports professional AI-assisted slide creation. Tome Slides was sunset on April 30, 2025.

**Is Tome still usable?**

No for new presentation work. Tome's Help Center told users to migrate before April 30, 2025 and said users would not be able to access Tome content after that date.

**What is the best Tome alternative for polished business slides?**

Beautiful.ai is the stronger replacement when the job is brand-safe business slides. Gamma is better for narrative/card-style output. Pitch is better for team deck operations.

**Did Tome become Lightfield?**

The founding team pivoted to Lightfield, an AI-native CRM. That is not a presentation product replacement.

**Should old Tome users rebuild decks in Beautiful.ai?**

Yes, if the deliverable is a conventional professional deck. Rebuild rather than relying on old Tome links or old PDF exports.
