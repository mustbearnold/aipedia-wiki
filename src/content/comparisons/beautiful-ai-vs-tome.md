# Beautiful.ai vs Tome

[Beautiful.ai](../tools/beautiful-ai.md) and [Tome](../tools/tome.md) are AI presentation tools that generate slides from text prompts or outlines as of April 2026. Beautiful.ai focuses on design automation with templates, while Tome emphasizes narrative-driven content creation with embedded multimedia.

## Quick Answer

Tome suits users needing fast, story-based presentations with AI-generated text and images; Beautiful.ai fits teams requiring structured, brand-compliant slide decks. Choice depends on whether narrative flexibility or design consistency matters more.

## At a Glance

| | Beautiful.ai | Tome |
|---|---|---|
| **Flagship** | Design Automation Engine v3.2 | Narrative AI v2.1 |
| **Price** | Free; Pro $12/month; Team $40/user/month | Free; Pro $16/month; Enterprise custom |
| **Best For** | Branded corporate decks | Storytelling pitches |

## Where Beautiful.ai Wins

- Smart templates adjust layouts automatically for consistent branding across slides.[Beautiful.ai](https://www.beautiful.ai/pricing)
- Team collaboration features include shared libraries and version history for enterprise use.[Beautiful.ai](https://www.beautiful.ai/features)
- Export options support PowerPoint and PDF without watermarks on paid plans.[Beautiful.ai](https://help.beautiful.ai/article/exports)
- Drag-and-drop editor maintains design rules, reducing manual tweaks.[Beautiful.ai](https://www.beautiful.ai/blog)
- Integrates with Slack and Google Workspace for workflow embedding.[Beautiful.ai](https://www.beautiful.ai/integrations)

## Where Tome Wins

- Generates full narratives with text, images, and embeds from single prompts using multimodal AI.[Tome](https://tome.app/pricing)
- One-click webpage publishing turns decks into interactive sites.[Tome](https://tome.app/features)
- Faster generation for pitch decks, averaging 2 minutes per 10-slide deck.[Tome](https://tome.app/blog)
- Stronger multimedia support, including AI video clips and 3D models.[Tome](https://tome.app/updates)
- Free tier allows unlimited public presentations with basic exports.[Tome](https://tome.app/pricing)

## Key Differences

Beautiful.ai prioritizes slide-level design control through rule-based templates that enforce spacing, colors, and fonts, making it ideal for sales teams producing uniform decks; Tome starts with content generation, using large language models to build cohesive stories with integrated visuals, better for founders crafting investor pitches. Pricing reflects this: Beautiful.ai's Pro at $12/month emphasizes editing tools, while Tome's $16/month Pro unlocks advanced AI generations and private sharing. Context handling differs too, with Tome processing up to 100k tokens per prompt for longer outlines versus Beautiful.ai's focus on modular slide inputs.

## Who should choose Beautiful.ai

Sales and marketing teams at scale who need reusable templates and brand enforcement. Agencies producing client deliverables benefit from its export fidelity.

## Who should choose Tome

Solo creators, startups, and consultants building narrative-driven content like pitches or reports. Users valuing speed over customization prefer its end-to-end generation.

## Bottom Line

Both tools automate presentation creation effectively in 2026, but Tome leads for quick, creative outputs while Beautiful.ai excels in polished, team-oriented production. Test free tiers to match your workflow; neither dominates universally.
