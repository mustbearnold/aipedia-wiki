# Capacities vs Fireflies.ai

[Capacities](../tools/capacities.md) and [Fireflies.ai](../tools/fireflies.md) are note-taking tools with AI features in the ai-notes category as of April 2026. Capacities focuses on object-based knowledge management; Fireflies.ai centers on meeting transcription and summarization.

## Quick Answer

Fireflies.ai suits teams needing automated meeting notes; Capacities fits individuals building personal knowledge bases. Choice depends on whether transcription or structured note organization is primary.

## Where Capacities Wins

- Object-based notes link ideas as customizable objects like tasks or projects for flexible organization.
- Offline access and native apps support individual workflows without internet.
- Daily notes and block-based editing enable quick capture and long-term knowledge building.
- Integrates Claude Sonnet 4.6 for AI-assisted querying across personal notes.
- Export options include Markdown and PDF for data portability.

## Where Fireflies.ai Wins

- Auto-joins Zoom, Google Meet, Teams calls to transcribe and summarize without manual start.
- Speaker identification and topic detection organize meeting notes by conversation flow.
- Searchable transcripts across all recorded calls with AI-generated action items.
- Team sharing and permissions allow collaborative review of meeting insights.
- OpenAI model routing powers advanced summarization for multi-hour sessions.

## Key Differences

Capacities structures notes around user-defined objects and blocks, suiting solo users who build interconnected knowledge systems; it uses Claude Sonnet 4.6 (1M token context) for AI features like search and generation. Fireflies.ai automates meeting capture with transcription, summaries, and analytics powered by OpenAI models (2M token context), targeting teams with frequent calls. Capacities Pro costs $10/month for unlimited AI; Fireflies Pro matches at $10/month but adds Business tiers for teams. Capacities emphasizes privacy with local storage options; Fireflies requires cloud for transcription processing.

## Who should choose Capacities

Individuals or researchers who organize thoughts into linked objects and need AI for personal querying. It works for daily journaling or project tracking without meeting focus.

## Who should choose Fireflies.ai

Sales, support, or executive teams that record multiple calls weekly. It reduces manual note-taking through auto-summaries and searchable archives.

## Bottom Line

Use Capacities for standalone note organization and knowledge management; select Fireflies.ai for meeting-heavy workflows. Many users combine both, with Fireflies feeding transcripts into Capacities for deeper analysis.

## FAQ

**Which is cheaper?**
Both start free, with Pro tiers at $10/month; Fireflies Business at $19/month adds team features.

**Which has better output quality?**
Fireflies excels in transcription accuracy via OpenAI models; Capacities leads in customizable note structuring with Claude Sonnet 4.6.

**Can I use both?**
Yes; export Fireflies summaries as text to import into Capacities objects for combined use.
