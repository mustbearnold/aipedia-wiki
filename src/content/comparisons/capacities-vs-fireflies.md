# Capacities vs Fireflies.ai

[Capacities](/tools/capacities/) and [Fireflies.ai](/tools/fireflies/) both turn information into searchable notes, but they start from different inputs. Capacities is an object-based personal knowledge system. Fireflies.ai is a meeting intelligence platform that joins calls, transcribes conversations, summarizes them, and makes meeting history searchable.

## Quick Answer

Choose **Capacities** if your main problem is organizing long-lived knowledge: projects, people, books, sources, decisions, research notes, and daily notes that need durable structure.

Choose **Fireflies.ai** if your main problem is meetings: Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, Webex, sales calls, customer calls, interviews, action items, CRM handoff, and searchable team call history.

The practical combo is Fireflies for capture and Capacities for synthesis. Fireflies records what happened; Capacities turns the important parts into a working knowledge base.

| **Main watch-out** | Object-first note-taking has a learning curve | Meeting bots, retention settings, and AI credit auto-upgrades need policy review |

## Where Capacities Wins

- It gives notes structure through typed objects, properties, backlinks, collections, tags, and daily notes.
- It is stronger for research, personal knowledge management, writing from accumulated context, and project memory.
- Its free tier is unusually useful for PKM because the official pricing page lists unlimited spaces, objects, blocks, and custom object types.
- Capacities Pro adds the AI assistant, smart queries, calendar integrations, task actions, and richer media handling.
- AI Chat Connectors let Pro users connect a Capacities space to tools such as ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, Le Chat, and other MCP-compatible clients.

## Where Fireflies.ai Wins

- It can join meetings and capture transcripts without assigning a human note taker.
- Paid plans focus on unlimited transcription, AI summaries, meeting search, storage, integrations, analytics, and admin controls.
- AskFred, AI Skills, custom summaries, Sales Assist, voice agents, and CRM autofill make Fireflies more useful for sales, customer success, recruiting, and executive teams.
- Business and Enterprise are better fits when call history must be shared across a workspace with stronger admin controls.
- Fireflies is the clearer choice when the input is live speech, not typed notes.

## Plan Guidance

Start with **Capacities Basic** if you want to test whether object-based notes actually fit your brain. Upgrade to Pro when the AI assistant, AI Chat Connectors, provider choice, smart queries, calendar context, task actions, recurring tasks, and media limits become part of the daily workflow.

Start with **Fireflies Free** only for proof of fit. The free plan has limits and is not the right production answer for meeting-heavy teams. Move to Pro when individuals need unlimited paid transcription and summaries, and move to Business when shared team usage, storage, video, analytics, and admin controls matter.

For Fireflies, model the AI credits separately. Fireflies' own AI Credits guide says credits are shared at the team level and advanced features can consume them even when base transcription is covered by the plan.

## Workflow Fit

| Workflow | Better fit | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Personal knowledge base | Capacities | Objects and backlinks make long-term structure easier. |
| Sales or customer calls | Fireflies.ai | Transcripts, summaries, action items, and CRM handoff are the core job. |
| Research notes | Capacities | Sources, people, projects, and decisions can become typed objects. |
| Weekly team meetings | Fireflies.ai | Meeting capture and shared history matter more than PKM structure. |
| Writing from accumulated notes | Capacities | Linked context is easier to turn into drafts and outlines. |
| Compliance-sensitive calls | Depends | Fireflies needs bot consent and retention review; Capacities needs AI/data-sharing review. |

## Current Fact Check

Verified June 18, 2026 against official Capacities pricing/docs, Capacities Release 64, AI Chat Connector docs, and Fireflies pricing/help pages. Current official sources support the product split above: Capacities is a structured knowledge tool with Pro AI features and MCP-style AI Chat Connectors; Fireflies is a meeting intelligence product with paid transcription/summaries and a separate advanced AI credit system.

## Who Should Choose Capacities

Choose Capacities if the meeting summary is just one input among many. It is better when the real goal is a durable map of people, projects, sources, books, decisions, and future writing.

## Who Should Choose Fireflies.ai

Choose Fireflies if calls are the source of truth. It is better when the team needs transcripts, summaries, searchable call history, action items, integrations, and meeting analytics.

## Bottom Line

Capacities is the better knowledge base. Fireflies.ai is the better meeting capture system. If both problems are real, use Fireflies for raw meeting memory and Capacities for the cleaned-up knowledge layer.

## FAQ

**Which is cheaper?**
Capacities is easier to justify for solo PKM because the Basic tier is broad. Fireflies becomes valuable when meeting time is expensive enough to justify paid transcription, summaries, admin controls, and storage.

**Does Capacities replace Fireflies.ai?**
No. Capacities can store meeting notes, but it is not a meeting bot or transcription platform.

**Does Fireflies.ai replace Capacities?**
No. Fireflies keeps meeting history searchable, but it is not designed as a full object-based PKM system.

**Can I use both?**
Yes. Use Fireflies to capture calls, then move decisions, customer themes, action items, and research context into Capacities objects.
