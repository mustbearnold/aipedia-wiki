# Frase.io vs MarketMuse

[Frase.io](/tools/frase/) and [MarketMuse](/tools/marketmuse/) both help content teams plan and improve SEO content, but they are bought for different jobs. Frase is now the more operational SEO/GEO workflow: AI Agent, content optimization, AI visibility tracking, site audits, publishing, API, and MCP access. MarketMuse is the strategic content-intelligence layer: inventory, topical authority, content briefs, competitive gaps, and prioritized content planning.

## Quick Answer

Choose Frase if the team needs one workflow to research, brief, optimize, monitor AI visibility, audit pages, and publish. Choose MarketMuse if the team already has a content estate and needs to decide which pages to create, refresh, consolidate, or prioritize across a site-wide topical authority map.

## Decision Snapshot

- **Best operational workflow:** Frase. It is stronger when writers and editors need daily briefs, drafts, optimization, AI visibility prompts, and site-audit workflow in one account.
- **Best strategy layer:** MarketMuse. It is stronger when leadership needs inventory, topical authority, competitive advantage, content briefs, and strategy documents.
- **Pricing posture:** Frase exposes a self-serve ladder. MarketMuse exposes Free, Optimize, Research, and Strategy tiers with usage limits, but buyers should verify commercial terms directly.
- **Best for new content production:** Frase, because it moves from SERP research to rank-ready documents faster.
- **Best for mature content estates:** MarketMuse, because inventory and prioritization are the reason to pay.
- **Main watch-out:** Frase can tempt teams into generic optimized pages; MarketMuse can be overkill if no strategist will act on the roadmap.

## Where Frase Wins

- **End-to-end daily workflow.** Frase combines AI Agent access, SEO/GEO content optimization, AI visibility tracking, site audits, SERP research, competitor analysis, brand voice, and API/MCP access across plans.
- **Clearer self-serve purchase path.** Starter, Professional, Scale, and Enterprise separate prompts, AI visibility history, audit pages, articles, seats, and domains.
- **AI visibility inside content ops.** Frase tracks prompts, competitors, visibility score, mentions, share of voice, sentiment, and AI referrals.
- **Better writer handoff.** It is easier for a content manager to turn a target keyword into a brief, outline, draft, and optimization pass.
- **Lower process overhead.** Smaller teams can start with Frase without first designing a full topical-authority operating model.

## Where MarketMuse Wins

- **Content inventory.** MarketMuse keeps a site inventory of published pages, topics, and page-topic combinations.
- **Topical authority modeling.** It is built for deciding where a site has authority, where gaps exist, and where content investment should go next.
- **Strategy documents and brief types.** Research and Strategy tiers unlock more planning depth, while Strategy includes all nine brief types.
- **Portfolio prioritization.** MarketMuse is better at ranking content opportunities by authority lift, personalized difficulty, and competitive advantage.
- **Executive planning.** It gives SEO and content leaders a stronger roadmap than a page-level optimizer alone.

## Pricing and Plan Guidance

For Frase, start with the lowest tier that covers monthly article count, visibility prompts, domains, and seats. Professional is the practical floor for teams because it increases seats, visibility history, and article volume. Scale is for agencies or high-output teams.

For MarketMuse, do not rely on old public dollar figures. The current public page names Free, Optimize, Research, and Strategy with limits for queries, tracked topics, content briefs, strategy documents, users, and brief types. Ask MarketMuse for current commercial terms before procurement.

## Bottom Line

Frase is the better buy when content production and optimization are the everyday bottleneck. MarketMuse is the better buy when the expensive question is what the site should publish or refresh next. Many mature teams can use both: MarketMuse for roadmap decisions, Frase for execution.

## FAQ

**Which is cheaper?**
Frase is easier to price from the public site. MarketMuse is more demo-led on commercial terms, so procurement should verify the actual quote.

**Which is better for AI search visibility?**
Frase is the more direct AI-visibility workflow because it exposes prompt tracking, competitor monitoring, AI referrals, sentiment, and visibility metrics. MarketMuse is more about content authority and planning.

**Which is better for an existing site with hundreds of pages?**
MarketMuse is the better strategic layer. Frase can optimize pages, but MarketMuse is built around inventory and prioritization.
