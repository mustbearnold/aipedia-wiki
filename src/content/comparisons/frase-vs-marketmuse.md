# Frase.io vs MarketMuse

[Frase.io](../tools/frase.md) and [MarketMuse](../tools/marketmuse.md) are AI SEO tools that analyze content, suggest optimizations, and support keyword research as of April 2026. Frase.io focuses on content briefs and outlines powered by OpenAI models, while MarketMuse emphasizes content inventory and semantic analysis with Claude Sonnet 4.6 integration[5].

## Quick Answer

Frase.io suits teams needing full content workflows from research to drafting; MarketMuse fits audits and brief generation for existing sites. Choice depends on whether you prioritize creation or optimization.

## Decision Snapshot
| | Frase.io | MarketMuse |
|---|---|---|
| **Flagship** | OpenAI frontier models | Claude Sonnet 4.6 |
| **Price** | Solo $14.99/mo, Basic $44.99/mo, Team $114.99/mo | Standard $149/mo, Premium $299/mo, Enterprise custom |
| **Context Window/Output Specs** | 128K tokens input, unlimited output via API | 1M tokens context, 5K word briefs |
| **Best For** | Content creation workflows | Content audits, optimization |

## Where Frase.io Wins

- Generates complete outlines and drafts from SERP analysis in one workflow[5].
- Lower entry pricing at $14.99/mo for solo users with core SEO features.
- Integrates real-time web data and ChatGPT-style editing for iterative writing.
- Supports team collaboration with shared briefs and version history.
- Handles multilingual content optimization across 20+ languages.

## Where MarketMuse Wins

- Builds detailed content briefs with topic models and gap analysis[5].
- Scales for enterprise inventories tracking thousands of pages.
- Provides semantic scoring to compare content against competitors.
- Offers API for custom integrations in CMS like WordPress.
- Focuses on long-term site health with optimization recommendations.

## Key Differences

Frase.io centers on end-to-end content production, using OpenAI models for generating briefs, outlines, and full drafts based on keyword research and SERP data; it starts at $14.99/mo and scales to team plans[5]. MarketMuse prioritizes analysis, leveraging Claude Sonnet 4.6's 1M token context for auditing inventories, scoring pages, and suggesting improvements; pricing begins at $149/mo for standard access[1][5]. Frase.io outputs editable documents quickly, while MarketMuse delivers structured insights for refinement.

## Who should choose Frase.io

Content marketers and agencies creating new material from scratch benefit from its drafting tools and lower cost. Solo creators find the $14.99/mo plan sufficient for briefs and publishing.

## Who should choose MarketMuse

Site owners with existing content use it for audits and scaling optimizations across portfolios. Enterprises value inventory management and API flexibility.

## Bottom Line

Pick Frase.io for affordable content generation workflows; select MarketMuse for in-depth audits on mature sites. Many teams combine both: Frase for creation, MarketMuse for refinement.
