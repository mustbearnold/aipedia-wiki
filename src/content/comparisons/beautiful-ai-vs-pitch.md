# Beautiful.ai vs Pitch

[Beautiful.ai](/tools/beautiful-ai/) and [Pitch](/tools/pitch/) are both business presentation platforms with AI features, but they solve different buyer problems. Beautiful.ai is strongest when a team wants slides to stay polished, on-brand, and structurally controlled. Pitch is strongest when the deck is a collaborative workspace asset that needs comments, guests, analytics, advanced links, pitch rooms, and distribution controls.

The practical rule: **choose Beautiful.ai when slide design discipline is the bottleneck. Choose Pitch when collaboration and deck sharing are the bottleneck.** This comparison was re-verified on **May 8, 2026** against current official Beautiful.ai and Pitch sources.

## Quick Verdict

**Pick Beautiful.ai for controlled, professional business decks.** Beautiful.ai's current AI presentation page positions the product around moving from prompt to work-ready decks, then refining those decks with Smart Slides. Smart Slides automatically realign, resize, and animate content, while themes, fonts, logos, footers, AI image styles, and brand controls help keep slides consistent. This makes Beautiful.ai the better pick for consultants, executives, sales enablement teams, and operations teams that know what they want to say but do not want slide formatting to become a second job.

**Pick Pitch for team deck operations.** Pitch's current pricing page is built around workspace roles, members, guests, AI credits, advanced links, pitch rooms, custom domains, analytics, PowerPoint exports, and distribution. Pitch is better when the deck passes through multiple hands and needs to be shared, measured, revised, and reused.

There is no universal winner. Beautiful.ai is a design-control platform with AI assistance. Pitch is a collaboration and distribution platform with AI assistance.

## Winner By Use Case

- **Most polished slide layout:** Beautiful.ai.
- **Smart templates and design guardrails:** Beautiful.ai.
- **Brand-controlled business decks:** Beautiful.ai.
- **PowerPoint import/export inside a guided design system:** Beautiful.ai.
- **Live team editing and comments:** Pitch.
- **Guest access and review loops:** Pitch.
- **Sales links, pitch rooms, email capture, and engagement analytics:** Pitch.
- **Small team workspace with a free entry point:** Pitch.
- **One-off paid presentation project:** Beautiful.ai.

## Where Beautiful.ai Wins

Beautiful.ai wins when the main cost is slide cleanup. Its AI presentation page describes a staged creation workflow: start with a prompt, shape the outline, generate polished slides, then refine with Smart Slides. The important difference from a one-shot AI generator is that Beautiful.ai is built around guardrails. Smart Slides are intelligent layouts that realign, resize, and animate content automatically. Users focus on the story; the system handles the formatting.

That matters for professional decks. A sales review, board update, client proposal, or operating plan can look worse every time someone adds one more bullet, swaps a chart, or changes an image. Beautiful.ai is strongest when the organization wants fewer layout accidents. Its AI page also describes custom AI image styles, theme controls, fonts, logos, footers, stock images, video, icons, and reusable branded themes.

Beautiful.ai pricing also supports this positioning. Its pricing page lists Pro, Team, and Enterprise. The FAQ says Pro is $12/month billed annually for individual users, Team is $40/user/month billed annually or $50/user/month, Enterprise is custom for larger organizations, and a one-off single presentation option is $45. The same pricing page lists plan-level design and distribution features such as unlimited AI content generation, Smart Slides, custom branding, PowerPoint import, viewer analytics, PDF export, and editable PowerPoint export.

Beautiful.ai is not the best fit for every team workflow. It has collaboration and analytics, but the core product advantage is keeping decks visually disciplined. If the problem is "many people need to collaborate and track buyer engagement," Pitch is the cleaner short list.

## Where Pitch Wins

Pitch wins when the deck is a team object, not just a designed file. Its pricing page starts with a Free workspace for up to 5 members, 100 one-time AI credits, unlimited presentations, and custom templates. Plus is for one person creating professional on-brand decks. Team expands to up to 25 members with higher AI credits, external guests, advanced links, pitch rooms, custom domains, interactive embeds, co-presenting, content variables, workspace roles, and version history. Business expands to up to 200 members with more AI credits, unlimited guests, unlimited advanced links, unlimited shared pitch rooms, and batch presentation creation.

Pitch also has distribution details that matter for sales and agency workflows. Its pricing page describes advanced links that track visits, slide views, time spent on slides, optional passcodes, email capture, visit notifications, data privacy controls, and pitch room links. Those are not cosmetic slide features. They are revenue-workflow features for teams that send decks to prospects, investors, clients, partners, or internal stakeholders.

Pitch is weaker if the team primarily wants the slide design engine to prevent formatting mistakes. It offers templates and AI, but Beautiful.ai is more intentionally shaped around Smart Slides and design guardrails. Pitch starts to win when the presentation needs to be worked on together and measured after sharing.

## Pricing And Plan Guidance

Beautiful.ai is easier to understand as design-system pricing:

- Pro: $12/month billed annually for individual users, with a 14-day trial.
- Team: $40/user/month billed annually or $50/user/month, for up to 20 users.
- Enterprise: custom pricing for larger organizations that need security, support, SSO, provisioning, audit logs, permissions, and brand guardrails.
- Single presentation option: $45 for ad hoc projects.

Pitch is easier to understand as workspace pricing:

- Free: $0 forever, up to 5 workspace members, 100 one-time AI credits, unlimited presentations, and custom templates.
- Plus: $15/month monthly or $13/month yearly for a one-person workspace, with 3,000 AI credits per year.
- Team: $23/seat/month monthly or $19/seat/month yearly, up to 25 members, 6,000 AI credits per seat per year, guests, advanced links, pitch rooms, custom domains, embeds, co-presenting, roles, and version history.
- Business: $30/seat/month monthly or $25/seat/month yearly, up to 200 members, 9,000 AI credits per seat per year, unlimited guests, unlimited advanced links, unlimited shared pitch rooms, and batch creation.

Do not compare these plans only by sticker price. Beautiful.ai's Team price can make sense if it prevents slide cleanup and keeps every deck on brand. Pitch's Team or Business price can make sense if the deck is part of a revenue workflow where analytics, guests, links, and pitch rooms help close deals or coordinate stakeholders.

## Buyer Recommendations

**Choose Beautiful.ai if:**

- Your decks need to look polished with minimal manual design cleanup.
- You want Smart Slides and guardrails more than open-canvas freedom.
- Your company cares about branded themes, fonts, logos, footers, and reusable slide systems.
- You create client proposals, board updates, sales decks, reports, training decks, or executive presentations.
- You want AI to help create and refine slides while the design system keeps output tidy.

**Choose Pitch if:**

- Multiple people need to edit, comment, review, present, or manage deck versions.
- You send decks externally and care about visits, slide views, time spent, passcodes, or email capture.
- Sales, marketing, agency, or startup teams need pitch rooms and custom domains.
- You want free commenters or guests involved without turning every reviewer into a paid creator.
- Deck distribution and follow-up matter as much as deck design.

## What To Avoid

- **Do not choose based on claimed model names.** The important difference is not which vendor is rumored to use which model. It is whether you need Smart Slide design control or collaborative deck operations.
- **Do not buy Beautiful.ai just for link analytics.** It has viewer analytics, but Pitch is stronger when tracking, guests, advanced links, pitch rooms, and client-facing sharing are the central workflow.
- **Do not buy Pitch if slide layout discipline is the pain.** Pitch can make good decks, but Beautiful.ai is more focused on preventing formatting mess.
- **Do not ignore PowerPoint handoff.** Both products discuss PowerPoint export or import capabilities, but real-world handoff should be tested with the exact deck style your team uses.

## Bottom Line

Beautiful.ai is the stronger choice for teams that want slides to look consistently good with less formatting work. Pitch is the stronger choice for teams that collaborate on decks, send them to external audiences, and need engagement data.

If you are an executive, consultant, or team lead trying to stop ugly internal decks, start with Beautiful.ai. If you are a sales, marketing, startup, or agency team using decks as a live workflow, start with Pitch.

## FAQ

**Is Beautiful.ai better than Pitch?**

Beautiful.ai is better for Smart Slides, design guardrails, branded business decks, and reducing manual formatting work. Pitch is better for collaboration, guests, advanced links, analytics, pitch rooms, and external deck sharing.

**Which is cheaper?**

Beautiful.ai Pro is $12/month billed annually for individual users, while Pitch Free supports up to 5 members and Pitch Plus starts at $15/month monthly or $13/month yearly. For teams, Beautiful.ai Team is $40/user/month annually or $50/user/month, while Pitch Team is $23/seat/month monthly or $19/seat/month yearly. Check the live pricing pages before purchase.

**Which is better for sales teams?**

Pitch is usually the better sales-team platform because it includes advanced links, pitch rooms, email capture, visitor notifications, engagement analytics, guests, and custom domains. Beautiful.ai is stronger when the sales team needs polished, on-brand deck creation and fewer design mistakes.

**Can I use both?**

Yes. Some teams may create highly polished templates or presentation systems in Beautiful.ai, then use Pitch for collaborative sales sharing and analytics. Test export/import fidelity before building a workflow around both.

**Which is better for one-off presentations?**

Beautiful.ai is stronger for one-off polished business decks, especially because its pricing page lists a single-presentation option. Pitch is better if the one-off still needs review, guests, external sharing, or analytics.
