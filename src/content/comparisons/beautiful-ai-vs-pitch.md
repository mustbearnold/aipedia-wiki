# Beautiful.ai vs Pitch

[Beautiful.ai](../tools/beautiful-ai.md) and [Pitch](../tools/pitch.md) are presentation tools that use AI to assist with slide creation and design as of April 2026. Beautiful.ai focuses on automated layouts and team collaboration, while Pitch emphasizes storytelling and real-time editing.

## Quick Answer

Beautiful.ai suits teams needing structured templates and design automation; Pitch fits sales and marketing users prioritizing narrative flow and speed. Choice depends on workflow needs.

## At a Glance

| | Beautiful.ai | Pitch |
|---|---|---|
| **Flagship** | Smart Slides with Gemini 3.1 Pro integration | AI Deck Builder with Claude Sonnet 4.6 |
| **Price** | Free; Pro $12/mo; Team $40/mo/user | Free; Pro $20/mo; Business $100/mo/user |
| **Best For** | Design automation, enterprise teams | Storytelling, sales pitches |

## Where Beautiful.ai Wins

- Automated slide layouts adjust content dynamically for consistent design.
- Strong template library for business reports and pitches.
- Enterprise features like brand kits and permissions for large teams.
- Integrates with Gemini 3.1 Pro for content generation in slides.
- Exports to PowerPoint and PDF without formatting loss.

## Where Pitch Wins

- Real-time collaboration with live editing and comments.
- Narrative-focused AI that structures decks around stories.
- Fast deck creation from prompts or outlines using Claude Sonnet 4.6.
- Built-in analytics to track viewer engagement.
- Mobile app for on-the-go edits and presentations.

## Key Differences

Beautiful.ai excels in design precision through rule-based smart slides that resize elements automatically, reducing manual tweaks; Pitch prioritizes speed and collaboration, generating full decks from text prompts with less emphasis on pixel-perfect layouts. Pricing starts lower for Beautiful.ai's Pro plan at $12 per month versus Pitch's $20, but Pitch's Business tier offers advanced analytics at $100 per user monthly. Both integrate 2026 flagship AI models: Beautiful.ai uses Gemini 3.1 Pro for multimodal content, while Pitch leverages Claude Sonnet 4.6 for reasoning-heavy outline building.[1][2]

## Who should choose Beautiful.ai

Teams in enterprise settings or design-heavy roles benefit from its automation and templates. It handles repetitive slide creation efficiently.

## Who should choose Pitch

Sales, marketing, or startup teams needing quick, story-driven decks find its collaboration and analytics most useful. It speeds up pitch preparation.

## Bottom Line

Neither tool dominates universally; Beautiful.ai leads for structured design workflows, Pitch for collaborative storytelling. Test free tiers to match your use case, as both deliver reliable AI-assisted presentations in 2026.

## FAQ

**Which is cheaper?**  
Beautiful.ai Pro at $12/mo undercuts Pitch Pro at $20/mo; both offer free plans with core features.

**Which has better output quality?**  
Beautiful.ai produces more polished, template-driven slides; Pitch generates faster, narrative-focused decks.

**Can I use both?**  
Yes, export from one to the other or use Beautiful.ai for design, Pitch for collaboration.
