# Frase.io vs Semrush

[Frase.io](/tools/frase/) and [Semrush](/tools/semrush/) are not substitutes for the same buyer. Frase is a focused SEO/GEO content workflow for briefing, optimization, AI visibility tracking, audits, publishing, API, and MCP access. Semrush is a broad SEO and marketing suite with keyword research, competitive data, audits, content tooling, toolkits, and AI visibility through Semrush One or the AI Visibility Toolkit.

## Quick Answer

Choose Frase if the team primarily needs to create, optimize, refresh, and monitor content. Choose Semrush if the team needs keyword research, technical audits, competitor intelligence, reporting, and AI visibility connected to a broader SEO suite.

## Decision Snapshot

- **Best content workflow:** Frase. It is built for the editor and content manager who need briefs, drafts, optimization, AI-search visibility, and publishing support.
- **Best SEO operating system:** Semrush. It is stronger for keyword research, competitor analysis, site audits, rankings, reporting, PPC adjacency, and multi-client workflows.
- **AI visibility posture:** Frase embeds AI visibility into content ops; Semrush bundles AI visibility with Semrush One or offers the AI Visibility Toolkit.
- **Plan complexity:** Frase is easier to buy from a content-volume lens. Semrush requires mapping SEO Toolkit, Semrush One, AI Visibility Toolkit, Content Toolkit, projects, users, and limits.
- **Best agency fit:** Semrush for broad client reporting; Frase for content production teams inside an agency.
- **Main watch-out:** Frase does not replace Semrush's SEO data; Semrush can be too expensive and wide if the team only needs a content optimizer.

## Where Frase Wins

- **Content-first workflow.** Frase turns topics into research, briefs, optimized articles, and publishing workflow faster than a broad SEO suite.
- **GEO and AI search inside the editor.** AI visibility prompts, competitor monitoring, sentiment, share of voice, and AI referral tracking connect directly to content decisions.
- **Lower operational overhead.** Content teams do not need to configure a large SEO suite before getting value.
- **API and MCP access.** Frase exposes technical workflow hooks even on self-serve plans.
- **Cleaner buying path for writers.** Article volume, seats, domains, audit pages, and visibility prompts are the key plan variables.

## Where Semrush Wins

- **SEO data breadth.** Keyword research, competitor intelligence, site audits, reporting, backlink analysis, and market research are the reasons Semrush exists.
- **Semrush One.** Semrush now positions SEO and AI visibility together for buyers who want Google plus AI-answer visibility in one platform.
- **Technical and agency workflow.** Projects, audits, client reporting, user seats, toolkits, and integrations fit multi-client teams better than Frase.
- **Adjacent marketing tools.** Semrush can support PPC, content, local, social, and advertising workflows beyond organic content optimization.
- **Procurement familiarity.** Larger marketing teams already know how to budget for Semrush as an SEO suite.

## Pricing and Plan Guidance

Buy Frase when most of the spend is justified by content output, AI-optimized articles, visibility prompts, audit pages, and publishing workflow. It should not be the only SEO tool if the buyer needs backlink data or keyword discovery.

Buy Semrush when the team needs the wider SEO and marketing stack. Map whether the buyer needs classic SEO Toolkit, Semrush One, AI Visibility Toolkit, Content Toolkit, additional users, projects, tracked keywords, and audit limits. Do not compare Semrush's headline price with Frase without accounting for those units.

## Bottom Line

Frase is the better content workflow. Semrush is the better SEO platform. A serious content team may use both: Semrush to decide what to target and monitor the broader site, Frase to brief, optimize, and monitor content visibility.

## FAQ

**Which is cheaper?**
Frase is usually cheaper for content-only teams. Semrush costs more because it covers a broader SEO and marketing surface.

**Does Frase replace Semrush?**
No. Frase can replace some content-brief and optimization work, but it does not replace Semrush's keyword research, site audit, competitive intelligence, or reporting suite.

**Which is better for AI visibility?**
Depends on the job. Frase is better when AI visibility is part of content workflow. Semrush is better when AI visibility must sit beside SEO data, audits, and client reporting.
