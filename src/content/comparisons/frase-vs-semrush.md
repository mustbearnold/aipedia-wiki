# Frase.io vs Semrush

[Frase.io](../tools/frase.md) and [Semrush](../tools/semrush.md) are two options in the ai-seo category as of April 2026. This comparison covers their current flagship AI models, pricing, and workflow fit based on available data[6].

## Quick Answer

Semrush suits comprehensive SEO workflows with broader keyword and site audit tools. Frase.io fits content optimization tasks focused on AI-assisted outlines and scoring.

## Decision Snapshot
| | Frase.io | Semrush |
|---|---|---|
| **Flagship** | Frase AI (integrated GPT-5.3) | Semrush AI (Claude Sonnet 4.6, Gemini 3.1 Pro) |
| **Price** | $14.99/mo Solo, $44.99/mo Basic Team | $139.95/mo Pro, $249.95/mo Guru |
| **Best For** | Content brief generation, SERP analysis | Keyword research, site audits, full SEO suite |

## Where Frase.io Wins

- Content optimization scores pages against SERP data for on-page improvements[6].
- Generates outlines and briefs from topic inputs with AI summaries.
- Lower entry pricing starts at $14.99 per month for solo users.
- Focuses on writing workflows with plagiarism checks and readability metrics.
- Simpler interface for quick content tasks without broad site management.

## Where Semrush Wins

- Covers keyword research, backlinks, PPC, and local SEO in one platform[6].
- Site audit tool identifies technical issues across full domains.
- Semrush AI integrates multiple models like Claude Sonnet 4.6 for varied tasks.
- Higher limits on keywords tracked and reports generated in paid plans.
- Established data from billions of keywords for competitive analysis.

## Key Differences

Frase.io centers on AI-driven content creation, using models like GPT-5.3 to produce briefs, outlines, and optimization scores from SERP analysis. Semrush offers a wider SEO toolkit, including keyword discovery, traffic analytics, and audits, powered by Semrush AI with Claude Sonnet 4.6 and Gemini 3.1 Pro for content and research[1][6]. Frase.io pricing begins lower at $14.99 monthly, while Semrush starts at $139.95 for Pro access to full features. Frase.io suits writers needing targeted content tools; Semrush fits agencies handling complete SEO strategies.

## Who should choose Frase.io

Content creators and small teams focused on article optimization and brief generation. Its lower cost and content-specific AI make it direct for writing workflows.

## Who should choose Semrush

Agencies and marketers requiring keyword research, audits, and competitor tracking. The broader suite justifies higher pricing for end-to-end SEO management.

## Bottom Line

Choose Frase.io for affordable content-focused AI tools. Opt for Semrush when needing comprehensive SEO data and features. Many users combine both for content plus research.
