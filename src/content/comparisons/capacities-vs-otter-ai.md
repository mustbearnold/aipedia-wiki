# Capacities vs Otter.ai

[Capacities](../tools/capacities.md) and [Otter.ai](../tools/otter-ai.md) serve the ai-notes category with distinct focuses as of April 2026. Capacities handles structured knowledge management through object-based notes, while Otter.ai centers on real-time transcription and meeting summaries.

## Quick Answer

Capacities suits users building personal knowledge bases with custom objects and AI-assisted linking. Otter.ai fits teams needing automated meeting notes, speaker identification, and collaboration on transcripts.

## At a Glance

## Where Capacities Wins

- Custom objects allow tagging notes as people, projects, or tasks for linked workflows.
- Daily notes auto-link to objects, supporting long-term knowledge building.
- AI queries search across all content with natural language.
- Offline access and local-first storage for privacy-focused users.
- Export to Markdown or PDF for flexibility.

## Where Otter.ai Wins

- Real-time transcription captures live meetings with 92% accuracy across accents.
- Speaker identification tags dialogue in multi-person calls.
- Automated summaries extract action items and key points post-meeting.
- Integrations with Zoom, Google Meet, and Teams for seamless capture.
- Team sharing enables collaborative editing of transcripts.

## Key Differences

Capacities emphasizes a block-based editor where notes connect via relational objects, enabling queries like "show tasks related to project X." This structure excels for solo users organizing research or ideas over time. Otter.ai prioritizes audio input, converting spoken content into searchable, editable text with timestamps. Its strength lies in group settings, where multiple speakers contribute, and outputs focus on recaps rather than interconnected databases. Capacities Pro includes unlimited AI usage at $10/month, matching Otter Pro's individual pricing but adding storage limits on free tier. Otter scales to Business at $20/month for advanced team features like custom vocabulary.

## Who should choose Capacities

Individuals or researchers managing complex personal notes benefit from its object system and AI linking.

## Who should choose Otter.ai

Teams handling frequent meetings gain from automated transcription and shared summaries.

## Bottom Line

Choose Capacities for structured, personal note-taking with relational depth. Pick Otter.ai for reliable meeting capture and team collaboration. Many users combine both: Otter for input, Capacities for long-term organization.

## FAQ

**Which is cheaper?**  
Both start free, with Pro tiers at $10/month; Otter adds Business at $20/month for teams.

**Which has better output quality?**  
Capacities offers precise object-based organization; Otter provides higher transcription accuracy for speech.

**Can I use both?**  
Yes, export Otter transcripts to Capacities for linking into knowledge objects.
