# ChatGPT vs Udio

[ChatGPT](../tools/chatgpt.md) generates text, code, images, and handles conversations. [Udio](../tools/udio.md) creates full songs from text prompts, including vocals, instruments, and structure. This comparison covers their flagship versions, pricing, and use cases as of April 2026.

## Quick Answer

ChatGPT suits general tasks like writing and analysis. Udio fits music creation from prompts.


## Where ChatGPT Wins

- Handles text generation, code writing, document analysis, and web browsing in one interface.[3]
- Supports image creation, voice conversations, and plugin ecosystem for broad tasks.[3]
- Pro tier at $200/mo includes advanced reasoning and high usage limits for heavy users.[3]
- Integrates real-time data and code execution for research or development workflows.[2]
- Free tier provides useful access without payment.[3]

## Where Udio Wins

- Generates complete songs with lyrics, vocals, and instrumentation from text descriptions.
- Exports stems for remixing or professional production in DAWs.
- Pro plan at $10/mo unlocks faster generation and commercial rights.
- Focuses on music-specific outputs like genre blending and custom structures.
- Free tier allows 10 songs per day for testing ideas.

## Key Differences

GPT-5.5 supports 1M-token API context while ChatGPT tier windows are not fully published; Udio outputs audio tracks up to 4 minutes with separate vocal and instrumental stems.[3] ChatGPT pricing scales to $200/mo for Pro with unlimited access; Udio starts at $10/mo for Pro with 500 credits monthly.[3] ChatGPT covers text, code, and images across domains; Udio specializes in music composition from prompts.

## Who should choose ChatGPT

Users needing one tool for writing, coding, analysis, or general queries. Teams handling mixed knowledge work benefit from its range.

## Who should choose Udio

Musicians or creators generating songs quickly from ideas. Producers value stem exports for further editing.

## Bottom Line

Choose ChatGPT for everyday AI tasks beyond music. Pick Udio to create original tracks without instruments or studio time. Many use both for content with audio elements.

## FAQ

**Can I use both?**  
Yes, combine ChatGPT for lyrics or concepts with Udio for music production.

**Which is cheaper?**  
Udio Pro at $10/mo costs less than ChatGPT Plus at $20/mo for basic paid access.[3]

**Which one should I pick first?**  
Start with ChatGPT for general use; add Udio if music creation is key.
