# Capacities vs Notion AI

[Capacities](/tools/capacities/) and [Notion AI](/tools/notion-ai/) both turn notes into a knowledge workspace, but they are built for different buyers. Capacities is a solo-first object-based personal knowledge system. Notion AI is a shared cloud workspace with docs, databases, meeting notes, search, and agents layered into one company operating system.

## Quick Answer

Choose **Capacities** if the job is personal knowledge management: people, projects, books, meetings, sources, custom object types, properties, backlinks, daily notes, and AI connectors for a selected private space.

Choose **Notion AI** if the job is team knowledge work: company docs, project databases, AI Meeting Notes, Enterprise Search, Notion Agent, Custom Agents, connected apps, permissions, and admin controls.

The clean rule is this: **Capacities is better for one person's structured knowledge graph. Notion AI is better when other people need to work in the same system.**

## Decision Snapshot

- **Primary buyer job:** Capacities: solo object-based PKM. Notion AI: team docs, databases, meetings, search, and agents.
- **Data model:** Capacities: typed objects with properties and backlinks. Notion AI: pages, databases, team spaces, permissions, and connected apps.
- **AI path:** Capacities: Pro AI assistant, provider choice, image analysis, web search, property auto-fill, and MCP AI Chat Connectors 2.0. Notion AI: Notion Agent, Ask Notion, AI Autofill, AI Meeting Notes, Enterprise Search, Research Mode, Custom Agents, and Workers beta.
- **June 2026 price signal:** Capacities pricing is region-rendered, so verify Pro and Believer at checkout. Notion production AI sits on Business at $20/seat/month, while Free and Plus include trial AI.
- **Collaboration:** Capacities is solo-first and has no real-time team workspace. Notion is built for team collaboration.
- **Main watch-out:** Capacities has proprietary export-fidelity risk. Notion AI has per-seat cost, credit risk for Custom Agents, and opaque model routing.
- **Winner for most solo PKM:** Capacities.
- **Winner for most teams:** Notion AI.

## Where Capacities Wins

- **Object-first structure.** People, books, projects, meetings, sources, and custom object types are first-class. Notion can mimic some of this with databases, but Capacities makes typed objects the default.
- **Cleaner personal graph.** Backlinks, properties, daily notes, and object pages create a more focused PKM habit than a broad team workspace.
- **Less workspace sprawl.** The app is narrower than Notion, which helps solo users avoid building a database maze before they write anything useful.
- **AI inside the personal system.** Pro adds the AI assistant, property auto-fill, image analysis, provider choice, and MCP AI Chat Connectors that let tools such as ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, and Le Chat work with a selected Capacities space.
- **Better default for private research systems.** Researchers, writers, founders, and students who think in entities usually get value from the object model faster than from a blank Notion workspace.

## Where Notion AI Wins

- **Team collaboration.** Docs, databases, permissions, comments, shared views, and workspace admin are built into the core product.
- **Meeting-to-workflow flow.** AI Meeting Notes can capture calls into the same workspace where projects, decisions, and follow-up tasks live.
- **Database automation.** AI Autofill and Notion Agent are stronger for shared operational databases, CRMs, project trackers, and content calendars.
- **Enterprise Search.** Notion AI can search across the workspace and connected apps such as Slack, Google Drive, and GitHub, depending on the integration setup.
- **Agent governance path.** Business and Enterprise buyers get Notion Agent and production AI access, while Custom Agents and Workers beta extend Notion into recurring workflows with credit controls.

## Plan Guidance

Start with **Capacities Free** if you are unsure whether typed objects will stick. Upgrade to Pro only when AI assistant access, AI Chat Connectors, provider choice, image analysis, richer media workflows, and recurring knowledge work are part of the daily habit. Because the public pricing page is region-rendered, confirm Pro and Believer pricing in your live checkout before budgeting.

Start with **Notion Free or Plus** only to test the workspace and trial AI. For production AI, Notion's current buyer anchor is Business at $20/seat/month for Notion Agent, AI Meeting Notes, Enterprise Search, and Research Mode. Budget separately for Custom Agent credits if recurring agents read large workspaces, run often, use Workers beta after August 11, 2026, or take many actions.

## Workflow Fit

- **Solo personal knowledge management:** Better fit: Capacities. Why: object types and backlinks are native.
- **Team wiki and operating docs:** Better fit: Notion AI. Why: collaboration, permissions, and databases are native.
- **Source and research graph:** Better fit: Capacities. Why: typed source/person/project objects keep research entities clear.
- **Meeting notes for a company:** Better fit: Notion AI. Why: meeting notes land inside the shared workspace.
- **Database automation:** Better fit: Notion AI. Why: AI Autofill and agents operate on shared Notion databases.
- **External AI access to notes:** Better fit: Capacities for a personal space, Notion AI for a team workspace. Why: Capacities uses MCP connectors; Notion uses its managed workspace AI and connected-app search.
- **Governed enterprise rollout:** Better fit: Notion AI. Why: admin, SSO, audit, security, and data-retention controls are part of the Enterprise path.
- **Avoiding per-seat AI cost:** Better fit: Capacities for solo use. Why: Notion production AI is priced per seat on Business.

## Who Should Choose Capacities

Choose Capacities if you are building a personal knowledge graph and want the system to impose useful structure: objects, properties, backlinks, daily notes, and AI that can work with a selected space. It is the better choice for solo researchers, writers, students, founders, and knowledge workers who dislike loose folders but do not need a shared company wiki.

## Who Should Choose Notion AI

Choose Notion AI if the knowledge base is already collaborative or operational. It is the better choice for teams that need docs, project databases, meeting notes, workspace Q&A, connected-app search, and agents in the same permissioned environment.

## Bottom Line

Capacities is the better **object-based PKM**. Notion AI is the better **team workspace with AI**. Do not choose Notion AI just because it has more features; choose it when collaboration, databases, meetings, and governance matter. Do not choose Capacities for a team operating system; choose it when one person's knowledge graph is the asset.

## FAQ

**Is Capacities cheaper than Notion AI?**
For solo use, usually yes, but Capacities plan prices are region-rendered and should be confirmed at checkout. Notion production AI is anchored on Business at $20/seat/month; Free and Plus include trial AI.

**Can Capacities replace Notion for a team?**
No. Capacities is solo-first and lacks real-time shared workspaces. If multiple people need to collaborate in the knowledge base, Notion AI is the safer fit.

**Can Notion AI replace Capacities for personal PKM?**
It can, but it is heavier. Notion databases can model people, projects, sources, and tasks, but Capacities makes typed objects the default instead of a system you have to design.

**Which has better AI?**
Notion AI has the broader production AI suite for teams: Notion Agent, AI Meeting Notes, Enterprise Search, Research Mode, AI Autofill, Custom Agents, and Workers beta. Capacities has the better AI shape for solo PKM because the assistant and MCP connectors work around a selected personal knowledge space.

**What is the biggest Capacities risk?**
Export fidelity and ecosystem size. Capacities can export, but its object relationships do not move as cleanly as local Markdown.

**What is the biggest Notion AI risk?**
Workspace quality, per-seat cost, and credits. Messy workspaces produce weaker answers, Custom Agents can consume Notion credits, and Workers beta starts using credits on August 11, 2026.
