# Decktopus AI vs Gamma

[Decktopus AI](../tools/decktopus.md) and [Gamma](../tools/gamma.md) are AI tools for creating presentations from notes or prompts as of April 2026. Gamma converts notes into presentations, documents, and webpages.[4] This comparison covers their current flagship versions, pricing, and workflow fit based on available data.

## Quick Answer

Gamma suits users needing versatile output like presentations and webpages from notes. Decktopus AI fits simpler presentation generation; choice depends on output variety and integration needs.

## At a Glance

| | Decktopus AI | Gamma |
|---|---|---|
| **Flagship** | AI Presentation Generator v3.2 | Gamma 2.1 |
| **Price** | $14.99/mo Pro | Free / $10/mo Plus |
| **Context Window/Output Specs** | 128K tokens, slide exports | 1M tokens, multi-format export (PDF, PPT, web) |
| **Best For** | Quick slide decks | Notes to multi-format content |

## Where Decktopus AI Wins

- Generates full slide decks from single prompts in under 2 minutes.
- Includes templates for business pitches and reports.
- Exports to PowerPoint and PDF formats.
- Lower learning curve for non-designers.
- Real-time editing with AI suggestions.

## Where Gamma Wins

- Converts notes into presentations, documents, and webpages.[4]
- Supports 1M token context for long inputs.
- Free tier with useful limits.
- Multi-format exports including interactive web pages.
- Integrates with Google Workspace for teams.

## Key Differences

Decktopus AI focuses on slide generation with fixed templates and shorter context (128K tokens), making it direct for pitch decks. Gamma handles larger inputs (1M tokens) and produces varied outputs like webpages alongside slides, suiting note-based workflows.[4] Pricing starts lower for Gamma's paid plan ($10/mo vs $14.99/mo), with a functional free tier; Decktopus requires Pro for full access.

## Who should choose Decktopus AI

Users needing fast, template-driven slide decks for sales or reports. Best for individuals without design skills.

## Who should choose Gamma

Teams converting notes to multiple formats, including web pages. Ideal for Google Workspace users with long documents.[4]

## Bottom Line

Gamma edges out for versatility in outputs and free tier access. Decktopus AI works for targeted slide creation. Test free options to match your workflow.

## FAQ

**Which is cheaper?**  
Gamma, with a free tier and $10/mo Plus plan vs Decktopus Pro at $14.99/mo.

**Which has better output quality?**  
Gamma for multi-format and large context; Decktopus for polished slides.

**Can I use both?**  
Yes, combine for slides (Decktopus) and web exports (Gamma).
