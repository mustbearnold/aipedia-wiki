# Decktopus AI vs Gamma

[Decktopus AI](/tools/decktopus/) and [Gamma](/tools/gamma/) are both useful AI presentation tools, but they have different centers of gravity. Gamma is the stronger default when you want to turn source material into presentations, documents, websites, exports, shareable pages, analytics, custom domains, or API-connected workflows. Decktopus is stronger when the presentation itself needs interactive delivery: custom domain links, slide analytics, webhook support, AI credits, branded corporate templates, or a sales/training funnel.

The practical rule: **choose Gamma for multi-format content creation. Choose Decktopus when the deck needs built-in presentation delivery and business workflow features.** This comparison was re-verified on **May 8, 2026** against current official Decktopus and Gamma sources.

## Quick Verdict

**Gamma wins for most buyers because it is broader.** Its current pricing page lists Free, Plus, Pro, and Ultra tiers. Free supports simple presentations, docs, websites, social, images, PDF/PPTX import, and PDF/PPTX/PNG/Google Slides export. Plus adds more cards per prompt, branding removal, and advanced AI image models. Pro adds custom branding and fonts, detailed analytics, advanced sharing, up to 10 custom domains, API access, and workspace templates. Ultra adds more cards per prompt, advanced model access across text/image/video, up to 100 custom domains, and early access.

**Decktopus wins when the presentation is an interactive business asset.** Its pricing page lists Pro at $14.99/month billed annually with 9,000 AI credits per year, 300 AI presentations per year, AI assistance, AI presentation generation, AI image generation, and PDF export. Business is $34.99/user/month billed annually with 12,000 AI credits per year, 400 AI presentations per year for the organization, custom domain connection, slide analytics, webhook, and team/organization features. Enterprise is custom and adds corporate-branded slides, branded AI setup, custom slide layouts, importing existing documents, and custom corporate templates.

Gamma is the better general-purpose AI presentation workspace. Decktopus is the better interactive presentation funnel.

## Winner By Use Case

- **Best default AI deck generator:** Gamma.
- **Decks plus docs, websites, social, images, imports, and exports:** Gamma.
- **Custom domains, analytics, API access, and workspace templates:** Gamma Pro or Ultra.
- **Interactive sales, proposal, or training presentation:** Decktopus.
- **Slide analytics, webhook, and team/organization delivery features:** Decktopus Business.
- **Corporate-branded AI slide system:** Decktopus Enterprise.
- **Budget testing:** Gamma Free for multi-format creation; Decktopus Pro when the delivery workflow matters.

## Where Gamma Wins

Gamma wins because it is a content system, not just a slide generator. Its AI presentation page says users can create a working presentation quickly, make presentations that look good on any device, turn text into presentations, remake a deck's style in one click, use flexible templates, and publish with analytics. Its pricing page confirms a broader surface: presentations, docs, websites, social, images, imports, exports, advanced sharing, custom domains, API access, and workspace templates.

That makes Gamma a stronger default for founders, consultants, marketers, educators, and teams that repurpose ideas. A pitch can become a deck and a public page. A memo can become a presentation and a document. A campaign brief can become a deck, web page, and social content. Gamma's value is that it reduces format friction.

Gamma also has a smoother testing path. Free is enough to understand the workflow. Plus adds a more practical solo tier. Pro adds the business features that matter for repeated content production: branding, analytics, sharing, custom domains, API, and templates. Ultra is for buyers who need much higher usage and the most advanced Gamma model options.

## Where Decktopus AI Wins

Decktopus wins when the deck is part of a delivery process. Its pricing page describes a credit-based presentation generator with Pro and Business tiers, plus Business features that matter after the deck exists: custom domain connection, slide analytics, webhook, and team/organization features. Enterprise adds branded slides, AI setup for branded slides, custom slide layouts, importing existing documents, and custom corporate templates.

That makes Decktopus useful for sales decks, proposals, webinars, training, founder pitches, client updates, and lightweight lead-capture workflows. If the main question is "Can we create and send a useful presentation quickly, then see what happened?", Decktopus deserves consideration.

Decktopus is weaker if the buyer needs docs, websites, social assets, Google Slides export, API-based content workflows, or a broader publishing surface. Gamma's official product structure is simply more expansive.

## Pricing And Plan Guidance

Start with **Gamma Free** if you want to test whether AI-generated cards fit your deck style. Move to **Gamma Plus** for regular creation and branding removal. Move to **Gamma Pro** when custom branding, fonts, detailed analytics, advanced sharing, custom domains, API access, and workspace templates matter. Use **Gamma Ultra** for much higher usage and the most advanced Gamma model options.

Start with **Decktopus Pro** if you want AI presentation generation with annual credits, AI image generation, and PDF export. Move to **Decktopus Business** when custom domains, slide analytics, webhook, and team/organization features matter. Use **Decktopus Enterprise** when corporate-branded slide generation, custom layouts, existing document imports, and custom templates are the core need.

Do not compare the tools only by entry price. Gamma can replace more content workflows. Decktopus can add more presentation-delivery value. The better plan is the one that matches the job after the AI draft is generated.

## What To Avoid

- **Do not buy Decktopus if you mainly need multi-format publishing.** Gamma is stronger for decks, docs, websites, social, images, imports, exports, and API workflows.
- **Do not buy Gamma if the presentation needs built-in sales/training delivery features.** Decktopus is stronger for custom domains, slide analytics, webhook, and corporate presentation templates.
- **Do not rely on old exact Gamma price claims unless verified at checkout.** This update uses official Gamma tier names and features, but exact prices were not visible in the extracted official pricing text.
- **Do not ignore credit usage.** Decktopus lists annual credits and presentation counts; Gamma tiers differ by cards per prompt and features.
- **Do not skip mobile share testing.** Both tools can look fine on desktop but behave differently when a prospect opens a presentation link on a phone.

## Bottom Line

Gamma is the stronger default because it creates and publishes more than slides: presentations, documents, websites, social content, exports, analytics, custom domains, API workflows, and reusable templates. Decktopus is the better choice when the presentation itself needs to act like an interactive business asset with custom domains, analytics, webhook support, and branded corporate delivery.

If the job starts with content and can end in several formats, choose Gamma. If the job starts with a presentation and needs viewer interaction or follow-up signals, choose Decktopus.

## FAQ

**Is Gamma better than Decktopus AI?**

Gamma is better for most AI presentation buyers because it supports multi-format content creation, exports, publishing, analytics, custom domains, API access, and workspace templates. Decktopus is better when the deck needs interactive delivery, slide analytics, webhook support, and corporate-branded presentation workflows.

**Which is cheaper?**

Decktopus Pro is listed at $14.99/month billed annually, and Business is $34.99/user/month billed annually. Gamma has Free, Plus, Pro, and Ultra tiers; check Gamma's live pricing page during checkout because exact pricing was not visible in the extracted official page text used for this update.

**Which should a solo founder choose?**

Choose Gamma if you want to create decks, docs, and web pages from the same material. Choose Decktopus if the deck is a pitch or proposal that needs analytics, custom domain delivery, or an interactive workflow.

**Can I use both?**

Yes. Gamma can draft and publish broad content; Decktopus can package a presentation into a more interactive delivery workflow. Use both only if the handoff is clearly defined.
