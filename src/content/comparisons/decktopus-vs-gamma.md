# Decktopus AI vs Gamma

[Decktopus AI](../tools/decktopus.md) and [Gamma](../tools/gamma.md) are AI tools for creating presentations from notes or prompts as of April 2026. Gamma converts notes into presentations, documents, and webpages.[4] This comparison covers their current flagship versions, pricing, and workflow fit based on available data.

## Quick Answer

Choose Gamma when you want the fastest polished first draft that can work as a deck, document, or shareable web page. Choose Decktopus AI when the presentation needs more presenter workflow around it: forms, audience responses, Q&A, analytics, custom domains, or lead capture. Gamma is stronger for content-to-polished-output speed. Decktopus is stronger when the deck itself needs to collect data.

## At a Glance

| | Decktopus AI | Gamma |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary job** | Presentation plus audience workflow | AI deck/doc/web-page generation |
| **Output style** | Prompt-to-deck with forms, Q&A, analytics | Card-based deck, doc, and web page |
| **Best For** | Trainers, educators, consultants, sales decks | Founders, consultants, marketers, fast drafts |
| **Avoid If** | You only care about pure deck polish | You need form/Q&A capture built into the deck |

## Where Decktopus AI Wins

- **Audience-response layer.** Forms, Q&A, analytics, and custom domains make Decktopus useful after the deck is shared.
- **Presenter workflows.** Trainers, educators, consultants, and sales teams can collect feedback or leads directly around the presentation.
- **Simple prompt-to-deck path.** Users who need a straightforward deck generator can get moving quickly.
- **Business sharing features.** Custom-domain and webhook features matter when decks are part of a funnel or training process.
- **Export and review loop.** Decktopus works as a first-draft builder when the final deck will be manually polished.

## Where Gamma Wins

- **Better multi-format output.** Gamma's card format can become a presentation, document, or shareable web page from one source.
- **Stronger first-draft polish.** It is usually the better choice when the visual/content draft matters more than audience forms.
- **Fast narrative assembly.** Notes, outlines, and prompts turn into structured card decks quickly.
- **Flexible sharing.** Web-page-style output is useful for proposals, explainers, and async updates.
- **Good solo/team value.** The free and paid tiers suit users who create decks often but do not need Decktopus's data-capture layer.

## Key Differences

Both tools generate decks, but they are optimized for different moments. Gamma is the better content-generation surface: turn a rough prompt, outline, or document into something presentable quickly. Decktopus becomes more interesting after the deck exists: collecting responses, tracking engagement, embedding forms, and routing viewers into follow-up workflows.

For high-stakes executive, investor, or brand decks, neither should be the final design layer without review. Gamma may get closer to a polished narrative draft; Decktopus may add workflow utility. The right choice depends on whether the deck is mostly a communication artifact or part of an interactive process.

## Who should choose Decktopus AI

Choose Decktopus AI if your slides need forms, Q&A, analytics, custom domains, or lead/feedback capture alongside presentation generation.

## Who should choose Gamma

Choose Gamma if you want to turn prompts, notes, or documents into polished decks, docs, or web pages with minimal manual slide design.

## Bottom Line

Gamma is the stronger default for AI-generated decks and multi-format content. Decktopus is the better niche pick when the presentation needs built-in response collection and viewer analytics.
