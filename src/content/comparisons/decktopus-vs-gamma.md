# Decktopus AI vs Gamma

[Decktopus AI](/tools/decktopus/) and [Gamma](/tools/gamma/) both create presentations from AI prompts, but they solve different buyer jobs. Gamma is the stronger default when a brief needs to become a deck, document, website, social asset, or shareable page. Decktopus is stronger when the presentation itself needs to behave like an interactive business asset with forms, custom domains, slide analytics, webhooks, and a hosted delivery layer.

The practical rule: **choose Gamma when the same idea needs multiple formats. Choose Decktopus when the deck needs interaction after it is shared.** This comparison was re-verified on **June 12, 2026** against official Decktopus pricing, Gamma pricing, Gamma API, and Gamma AI presentation sources.

## Quick Verdict

**Gamma wins for most buyers.** Its current pricing page lists Free, Plus, Pro, and Ultra paths. Free supports simple presentations, docs, websites, social posts, images, PDF/PPTX import, and export to PDF, PPTX, PNG, and Google Slides. Plus raises the card-per-prompt limit and removes Gamma branding. Pro adds premium image models, custom branding and fonts, detailed analytics, advanced sharing, up to 10 custom domains, API access, and workspace templates. Ultra raises the card-per-prompt limit again, unlocks advanced text/image/video model access, supports up to 100 custom domains, and includes early feature access.

**Decktopus wins when delivery matters more than format breadth.** Its June 2026 pricing page lists Pro at $14.99/month billed annually with 9,000 AI credits per year, about 300 AI presentations per year, AI assistance, AI presentation generation, AI image generation, and PDF export. Business is $34.99/user/month billed annually with 12,000 AI credits per year, about 400 AI presentations per year for the organization, custom domain connection, slide analytics, webhook, and team/organization features. Decktopus also says AI deck creation costs 30 credits.

For most prompt-to-deck buyers, Gamma is the safer first test. For trainers, consultants, solo sellers, and small teams that want the presentation to collect responses or trigger follow-up, Decktopus stays on the shortlist.

## Winner By Use Case

- **Best default AI presentation generator:** Gamma.
- **Decks plus docs, websites, social posts, and shareable pages:** Gamma.
- **PDF, PPTX, PNG, and Google Slides export from a broad creator workflow:** Gamma.
- **Custom branding, detailed analytics, custom domains, and API access:** Gamma Pro or Ultra.
- **Interactive sales, proposal, training, or webinar asset:** Decktopus.
- **Forms, voice recording, slide analytics, webhook, and custom-domain presenter links:** Decktopus Business.
- **Corporate-branded AI slide system:** Decktopus Enterprise.
- **Budget testing:** Gamma Free for multi-format creation; Decktopus Pro when the interactive presentation workflow is the real job.

## Where Gamma Wins

Gamma wins because it is a content packaging system, not only a slide generator. The official AI presentation page says Gamma can create a working presentation quickly, make presentations that look good on any device, turn text into a presentation, remake deck style, use templates, and publish with analytics. The pricing page confirms the broader surface: presentations, docs, websites, social posts, images, imports, exports, advanced sharing, custom domains, API access, and workspace templates.

That breadth matters. A founder pitch can become a deck and a web page. A marketing brief can become a presentation, a doc, and social-ready content. A consulting memo can become a client-facing page and a PPTX handoff. Gamma reduces format friction, which is the reason it wins this comparison for most buyers.

Gamma also has the stronger automation path. Gamma's Generate API v1.0 is generally available, and the help center says Pro, Ultra, Team, and Business accounts can access it. That makes Gamma the better pick when a team wants generated decks, documents, webpages, or social posts inside Make, Zapier, n8n, or a custom backend workflow.

## Where Decktopus AI Wins

Decktopus wins when the deck needs to do something after the AI draft exists. The official pricing and FAQ pages call out forms, voice recording, analytics, embeds, mobile-responsive documents, collaboration, custom domains, webhooks, and cloud sharing. Those are not cosmetic features. They change the job from "make slides" to "send an interactive presentation and learn what happened."

That makes Decktopus useful for sales proposals, workshops, webinars, training decks, client onboarding, lead capture, and lightweight microsite-style presentations. If the buyer's real question is "Can I share this and collect signal from the audience?", Decktopus has a clearer fit than Gamma.

Decktopus is weaker when the buyer needs one source to become a document, website, social post, Google Slides export, and API-generated content package. Gamma's format surface is broader, and that is the decisive difference for most general-purpose presentation buyers.

## Pricing And Plan Guidance

Start with **Gamma Free** if you want to test prompt-to-card generation, PDF/PPTX import, and basic exports. Move to **Gamma Plus** when branding removal and more cards per prompt matter. Move to **Gamma Pro** when custom branding, fonts, detailed analytics, advanced sharing, custom domains, API access, and workspace templates become real workflow requirements. Use **Gamma Ultra** only when high usage, advanced models, and many custom domains justify the heavier plan.

Start with **Decktopus Pro** if you need AI presentation generation, AI image generation, PDF export, and a clear annual credit pool. Move to **Decktopus Business** when custom domains, slide analytics, webhooks, and organization features matter. Use **Decktopus Enterprise** for corporate-branded slides, branded AI setup, custom layouts, existing document imports, and custom templates.

Do not compare these products only by entry price. Gamma can replace more content workflows. Decktopus can add more value at the presentation-delivery layer. The right plan depends on what happens after the AI draft is generated.

## What To Avoid

- **Do not buy Decktopus if you mainly need multi-format publishing.** Gamma is stronger for decks, docs, websites, social posts, images, imports, exports, and API generation.
- **Do not buy Gamma if the presentation needs built-in audience response collection.** Decktopus is stronger for forms, voice, analytics, webhooks, custom-domain presenter links, and microsite-like delivery.
- **Do not rely on stale exact Gamma prices.** Gamma's public page is reliable for current plan gates, but exact dollar amounts can vary by billing toggle, region, or account state; verify checkout before purchase.
- **Do not ignore usage meters.** Decktopus lists annual credits and a 30-credit AI deck cost. Gamma tiers differ by cards per prompt, model access, domains, API, and workspace features.
- **Do not skip mobile share testing.** Decks that look polished on desktop can behave differently when a prospect opens the shared link on a phone.

## Bottom Line

Gamma is the better default because it turns one idea into more outputs: presentations, documents, websites, social posts, exports, analytics, custom domains, API workflows, and reusable templates. Decktopus is the better choice when a deck needs to become an interactive presenter workflow with forms, analytics, voice, custom domains, webhooks, or lead-capture behavior.

If the job starts with source material and can end in several formats, choose Gamma. If the job starts with a presentation and needs viewer interaction or follow-up signal, choose Decktopus.

## FAQ

**Is Gamma better than Decktopus AI?**

Gamma is better for most AI presentation buyers because it supports multi-format content creation, exports, publishing, analytics, custom domains, API access, and workspace templates. Decktopus is better when the deck needs interactive delivery, forms, slide analytics, webhooks, and corporate-branded presentation workflows.

**Which is cheaper?**

Decktopus Pro is listed at $14.99/month billed annually, and Business is $34.99/user/month billed annually. Gamma has Free, Plus, Pro, and Ultra tiers; check Gamma's live pricing page during checkout because exact dollar amounts were not exposed consistently in the official public page text used for this June 2026 refresh.

**Which should a solo founder choose?**

Choose Gamma if the founder needs decks, docs, and web pages from the same material. Choose Decktopus if the founder needs an interactive pitch or proposal with analytics, custom-domain delivery, forms, or a presenter workflow.

**Can I use both?**

Yes, but define the handoff. Gamma can own drafting and multi-format publishing, while Decktopus can own an interactive sales, training, or proposal presentation. Avoid maintaining the same final deck in two places without a clear owner.
