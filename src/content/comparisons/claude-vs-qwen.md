# Claude vs Qwen

[Claude](../tools/claude.md) and [Qwen](../tools/qwen.md) are two options in the chatbots category as of April 2026. Claude offers proprietary models from Anthropic, while Qwen provides open-weight models from Alibaba. This comparison covers flagship versions, pricing, and workflow fit.[1]
