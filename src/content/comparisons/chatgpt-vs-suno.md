# ChatGPT vs Suno

[ChatGPT](../tools/chatgpt.md) generates text, code, images, and handles conversations. [Suno](../tools/suno.md) creates full songs from text prompts, including lyrics, music, and vocals. This comparison covers their flagship versions, pricing, and use cases as of April 2026.

## Quick Answer

ChatGPT suits general tasks like writing and analysis. Suno fits music creation from prompts.


## Where ChatGPT Wins

- Handles text generation, code writing, image creation, and web browsing in one interface.[3]
- Supports voice conversations and plugin ecosystem for broad workflows.[3]
- Pro tier at $200/mo enables high-volume, complex reasoning tasks.[3]
- Integrates real-time data and document analysis across formats.[2]
- Free tier provides useful access without payment.[3]

## Where Suno Wins

- Generates complete songs with lyrics, vocals, and instrumentation from text.[web:1]
- Outputs up to 4-minute tracks in diverse genres quickly.[web:1]
- Pro plan at $10/mo unlocks commercial rights and more credits.[web:1]
- Focuses on music-specific prompts without general AI overhead.[web:1]
- Free tier allows basic song creation for testing.[web:1]

## Key Differences

GPT-5.5 supports 1M-token API context while ChatGPT tier windows are not fully published; Suno specializes in audio outputs limited to song lengths.[3] ChatGPT pricing scales to $200/mo for Pro users needing unlimited access; Suno tiers top at $30/mo Premier for high-volume music generation.[3] ChatGPT covers text, code, and images multimodally; Suno targets music exclusively with genre and style controls.[2]

## Who should choose ChatGPT

Users needing one tool for writing, coding, research, or images pick ChatGPT. Its interface unifies tasks for daily knowledge work.[3]

## Who should choose Suno

Musicians or content creators generating songs from ideas select Suno. It delivers polished tracks without production skills.[web:1]

## Bottom Line

Choose ChatGPT for general AI needs across text and code. Opt for Suno when music creation is the goal. Many combine both for content with audio elements.

## FAQ

**Can I use both?**  
Yes, ChatGPT handles lyrics or scripts; Suno turns them into songs.

**Which is cheaper?**  
Suno Pro at $10/mo costs less than ChatGPT Plus at $20/mo for core paid access.[3]

**Which one should I pick first?**  
Start with ChatGPT for broad use; add Suno for music-specific output.
