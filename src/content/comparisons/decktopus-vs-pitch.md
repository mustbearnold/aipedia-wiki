# Decktopus AI vs Pitch

[Decktopus AI](/tools/decktopus/) and [Pitch](/tools/pitch/) both help teams create presentations with AI, but the buyer decision is not really "which model is better." Decktopus is strongest when you want a fast AI-generated deck or interactive document that can include forms, embeds, analytics, voice recording, custom domains, and mobile-responsive microsite behavior. Pitch is strongest when the presentation is a shared team asset that needs real-time collaboration, guests, advanced links, pitch rooms, version history, PowerPoint exports, custom domains, and audience engagement analytics.

The practical rule: **choose Decktopus when speed and interactive presentation delivery matter most. Choose Pitch when collaboration, brand-controlled team workflows, and sales-style sharing matter most.** This comparison was re-verified on **May 8, 2026** against current official Decktopus and Pitch sources.

## Quick Verdict

**Pick Decktopus AI for fast prompt-to-deck creation and interactive delivery.** Decktopus currently positions itself around typing a prompt and getting a fully designed, branded presentation with colors, fonts, and logo. Its product pages also emphasize regenerate-slide workflows, pasted-text-to-slide conversion, AI image generation, custom presentation templates, PDF transformation, Zapier integration, forms, embeds, slide analytics, voice recording, custom domains, and mobile-responsive microsites. That makes Decktopus stronger for solo sellers, founders, marketers, educators, and small teams that want to ship an interactive presentation quickly without starting from a blank slide.

**Pick Pitch for team presentation operations.** Pitch's current pricing and AI presentation pages are built around workspaces, roles, guests, custom templates, PowerPoint exports, advanced links, pitch rooms, custom domains, version history, analytics, and AI actions for proofreading, tone, rewriting, brand voice, and image enhancement. Pitch is the better choice when a deck will move through teammates, reviewers, clients, prospects, or stakeholders and the team needs the deck to be created, shared, measured, revised, and reused in one system.

There is no universal winner. Decktopus is faster for AI-assisted deck creation and interactive presentation funnels. Pitch is stronger when the deck is part of a durable team workflow.

## Winner By Use Case

- **Fast first draft from a prompt:** Decktopus AI.
- **Simple presentation for a founder, marketer, educator, or solo seller:** Decktopus AI.
- **Interactive documents with forms, embeds, analytics, or voice:** Decktopus AI.
- **Microsite-style mobile presentation sharing:** Decktopus AI.
- **Team deck collaboration and commenting:** Pitch.
- **Sales sharing with advanced links and pitch rooms:** Pitch.
- **Guest access and workspace permissions:** Pitch.
- **PowerPoint exports inside a broader team workflow:** Pitch.
- **Brand-controlled presentation system for a larger team:** Pitch for team operations; Decktopus Enterprise for branded AI generation and custom corporate templates.

## Where Decktopus AI Wins

Decktopus wins when the buyer wants the presentation generated quickly and packaged as a shareable, interactive artifact. Its homepage says users can type a prompt and get a branded presentation using their colors, font, and logo. It also describes a drag-and-drop editor, slide regeneration, "Start with AI," pasted text conversion, custom presentation templates, Zapier integration, and AI features for Q&A, AI images, talk refinement, storytelling structure, scripts, and PDF transformation.

That feature mix matters because many buyers are not building a deck for internal collaboration. They are trying to send something, collect a response, book a call, explain an offer, or turn a document into something more visual. Decktopus pricing and FAQ content describes forms for collecting data, voice recording for virtual presentation delivery, analytics for opens and page views, embeds for URLs or external pages, website embedding, custom domains, mobile-responsive documents, and collaboration.

Decktopus pricing is also oriented around AI credits and presentation generation. As verified on May 8, 2026, its pricing page lists Pro at $14.99/month billed annually with 9,000 AI credits per year and 300 AI presentations per year. Business is listed at $34.99/user/month billed annually with 12,000 AI credits per year and 400 AI presentations per year for the organization, plus custom domain connection, slide analytics, webhook, and team/organization features. Enterprise is custom and adds corporate-branded slide controls, AI setup for branded slides, custom slide layouts, importing existing documents, and custom corporate templates.

Decktopus is not the cleanest fit if the team needs a polished multi-seat deck operating system. It can support collaboration and business features, but the bigger product advantage is speed, guided generation, and interactive delivery. If the buyer's real problem is "our sales, marketing, and success teams need one shared workspace for deck reviews, links, guests, analytics, and versioning," Pitch is a more natural short list.

## Where Pitch Wins

Pitch wins when the deck needs to be managed as team infrastructure. Its AI presentation maker page describes AI-generated decks, brand tone, proofreading, tone adjustment, text rewriting, simplification, speaker notes, sales emails, AI image enhancement, brand consistency, custom templates, brand library, real-time editing, comments, slide statuses, and assignees. Pitch is not just trying to make a quick deck; it is trying to keep teams producing on-brand decks that can be reviewed, shared, and measured.

Pitch's pricing page makes that workflow especially clear. The Free workspace supports up to 5 members, 100 one-time AI credits, unlimited presentations, custom templates, branded sharing links, branded PDF exports, and 2 external guests. Plus is for a one-person workspace and adds 3,000 AI credits per year, extra credits, 5 external guests, custom fonts, video uploads, removing Pitch branding, and PowerPoint exports. Team expands to up to 25 members with 6,000 AI credits per seat per year, 25 external guests, 25 advanced links, 2 shared pitch rooms, custom domain, interactive embeds, co-presenting, content variables, workspace roles, and 30-day version history. Business expands to up to 200 members with 9,000 AI credits per seat per year, unlimited guests, unlimited advanced links, unlimited shared pitch rooms, batch creation, asset library collections, and unlimited version history.

Pitch also has stronger sales and client-facing distribution controls. Its pricing page describes advanced links that measure visits, slide views, time spent, optional passcodes, email capture, visit notifications, and data privacy controls. Those features are valuable when the deck is not just "finished" when it looks good. It still has to be sent, tracked, followed up on, revised, and reused.

Pitch is weaker if the buyer wants the fastest possible AI-generated deck with built-in forms, microsite behavior, voice recording, and lead-capture-style interactivity. Pitch can generate and improve decks, but its strongest case is team workflow rather than one-click presentation funnel creation.

## Pricing And Plan Guidance

Decktopus is easier to understand as AI generation plus interactive delivery pricing:

- Pro: $14.99/month billed annually, with 9,000 AI credits per year, 300 AI presentations per year, AI assistance, AI presentation generation, AI image generation, and PDF export.
- Business: $34.99/user/month billed annually, with everything in Pro, 12,000 AI credits per year, 400 AI presentations per year for the organization, custom domain connection, slide analytics, webhook, and team/organization features.
- Enterprise: custom pricing for corporate-branded AI solutions, including branded slides, custom slide layouts, importing existing documents, and corporate templates.

Pitch is easier to understand as workspace pricing:

- Free: $0 forever, up to 5 members, 100 one-time AI credits, unlimited presentations, custom templates, branded links, branded PDF exports, and 2 external guests.
- Plus: $15/month monthly or $13/month yearly for a one-person workspace, with 3,000 AI credits per year, custom fonts, video uploads, branding removal, PowerPoint exports, and 5 external guests.
- Team: $23/seat/month monthly or $19/seat/month yearly, up to 25 members, with 6,000 AI credits per seat per year, guests, advanced links, shared pitch rooms, custom domain, embeds, co-presenting, roles, and 30-day version history.
- Business: $30/seat/month monthly or $25/seat/month yearly, up to 200 members, with 9,000 AI credits per seat per year, unlimited guests, unlimited advanced links, unlimited pitch rooms, batch creation, asset library collections, and unlimited version history.
- Enterprise: custom requirements for larger organizations.

Do not compare these products by the lowest monthly number alone. Decktopus may be better value if it replaces manual slide drafting and gives you forms, embeds, analytics, voice, custom domains, and microsite-style delivery. Pitch may be better value if it becomes the shared deck workspace for a team that needs guests, permissions, analytics links, pitch rooms, version history, and PowerPoint handoff.

## Buyer Recommendations

**Choose Decktopus AI if:**

- You need a presentable deck quickly from a prompt, notes, pasted text, or a PDF.
- You want the deck to behave more like an interactive document, microsite, proposal, or presentation funnel.
- You care about built-in forms, embeds, voice recording, slide analytics, custom domains, and mobile-responsive sharing.
- You are a founder, creator, educator, consultant, account executive, marketer, or small business owner creating decks without a dedicated design team.
- Your main risk is spending too many hours making a simple presentation look good.

**Choose Pitch if:**

- Multiple people need to edit, comment, review, present, or manage deck versions.
- You need external guests, free commenters, workspace roles, and team permissions.
- Sales or client-facing teams need advanced links, email capture, visit notifications, pitch rooms, and engagement analytics.
- Your team wants custom templates, brand libraries, custom fonts, PowerPoint exports, and reusable presentation systems.
- Your main risk is deck chaos across sales, marketing, success, founders, clients, and reviewers.

## What To Avoid

- **Do not choose based on unofficial model names.** The old version of this page claimed specific frontier models and token windows. AiPedia could not verify those claims from official Decktopus or Pitch sources, so they should not drive the purchase decision.
- **Do not buy Decktopus expecting Pitch-style team operations.** Decktopus has collaboration, analytics, forms, embeds, and business features, but Pitch is stronger for workspace roles, guests, advanced links, pitch rooms, and version history.
- **Do not buy Pitch only to make a fast one-off AI deck.** Pitch can generate decks and has strong AI actions, but Decktopus is more direct when the job is quick prompt-to-presentation output.
- **Do not ignore export and handoff.** Test the exact output you need: PDF, PPTX, link sharing, mobile presentation, embedded document, or client-facing pitch room.
- **Do not overbuy team seats before testing the workflow.** Both tools can look compelling in a demo. The real question is whether your team actually uses the deck system after the first week.

## Bottom Line

Decktopus AI is the stronger choice for fast AI deck generation, interactive presentations, microsite-style delivery, forms, embeds, voice, and lightweight analytics. Pitch is the stronger choice for teams that collaborate on decks, send them externally, track engagement, manage guests, create pitch rooms, and maintain a shared presentation system.

If you are a solo seller, consultant, educator, founder, or marketer trying to produce a deck quickly, start with Decktopus. If you are a sales, marketing, startup, agency, or success team that treats decks as ongoing revenue assets, start with Pitch.

## FAQ

**Is Decktopus AI better than Pitch?**

Decktopus AI is better for fast AI-generated presentations, interactive documents, forms, embeds, voice recording, custom domains, and mobile-responsive sharing. Pitch is better for team collaboration, guests, advanced links, pitch rooms, engagement analytics, PowerPoint exports, roles, and version history.

**Which is cheaper?**

Decktopus Pro is listed at $14.99/month billed annually. Pitch has a Free workspace, while Pitch Plus is listed at $15/month monthly or $13/month yearly for one person. For teams, Decktopus Business is listed at $34.99/user/month billed annually, while Pitch Team is $23/seat/month monthly or $19/seat/month yearly and Pitch Business is $30/seat/month monthly or $25/seat/month yearly. Check live pricing before purchase.

**Which is better for sales teams?**

Pitch is usually better for sales teams that need advanced links, pitch rooms, email capture, visit notifications, engagement analytics, custom domains, guests, and team roles. Decktopus is still useful for solo sellers or small teams that want interactive proposals, forms, voice, and faster AI-generated presentation funnels.

**Can I use both?**

Yes. A small team could use Decktopus to generate a fast interactive first version, then use Pitch when a deck becomes part of a larger team workflow. Test export, link sharing, and branding handoff before making this a standard workflow.

**Which is better for a one-off presentation?**

Decktopus is usually the better one-off AI presentation pick because it is built around fast generation and interactive sharing. Pitch is better if the one-off still needs teammates, guests, analytics links, PowerPoint export, or a reusable workspace template.
