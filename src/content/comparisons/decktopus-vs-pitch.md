# Decktopus AI vs Pitch

[Decktopus AI](../tools/decktopus.md) and [Pitch](../tools/pitch.md) compete in the AI presentation category as of April 2026. Decktopus focuses on AI-generated slide creation from prompts, while Pitch offers collaborative deck building with AI assistance.

## Quick Answer

Decktopus suits solo creators needing fast AI slide generation; Pitch fits teams requiring collaborative editing and templates. Choice depends on workflow needs.

## At a Glance

| | Decktopus AI | Pitch |
|---|---|---|
| **Flagship** | Decktopus 3.0 (GPT-5.3 integration) | Pitch 8.2 (Gemini 3.1 Pro) |
| **Price** | $14.99/mo Pro, $29.99/mo Business | Free, $12/mo Pro, $25/mo Business |
| **Context Window/Output Specs** | 128K tokens input, 50-slide decks | 2M tokens input, unlimited slides |
| **Best For** | Quick AI decks from prompts | Team collaboration, templates |

## Where Decktopus AI Wins

- Faster slide generation from single prompts using GPT-5.3 Codex for structured outlines.
- Lower entry price at $14.99/mo for unlimited AI generations.
- Simpler interface for non-designers starting from text or ideas.
- Built-in AI designer suggests layouts, colors, icons automatically.
- Export options include PPTX, PDF, video without extra steps.

## Where Pitch Wins

- Larger 2M token context window via Gemini 3.1 Pro handles full documents or datasets.
- Real-time team collaboration with live editing and comments.
- Extensive template library for sales, pitch decks, reports.
- Integrations with Google Workspace, Slack, Figma for workflow fit.
- Free tier supports basic use with 10 AI generations monthly.

## Key Differences

Decktopus emphasizes end-to-end AI creation, generating complete decks from brief inputs via GPT-5.3 Codex, which excels in structured content but limits customization depth. Pitch leverages Gemini 3.1 Pro's multimodal strengths for data-heavy inputs and prioritizes human-AI hybrid editing in team settings. Pricing starts lower for Decktopus Pro, but Pitch's free plan and $12/mo tier offer broader access; both scale to $25-30/mo for business features like custom branding.

## Who should choose Decktopus AI

Freelancers or marketers generating one-off decks quickly benefit from its prompt-to-slide speed. Users avoiding design work find its AI automation most direct.

## Who should choose Pitch

Teams in Google ecosystems or needing collaboration prefer its integrations and real-time features. Knowledge workers processing large inputs value the 2M context.

## Bottom Line

Neither dominates universally; Decktopus accelerates solo AI creation, Pitch enhances team production. Test free tiers to match your use case, as outputs vary by prompt quality and input scale.

## FAQ

**Which is cheaper?**  
Pitch at $12/mo Pro undercuts Decktopus $14.99/mo; both free tiers exist, Pitch with more generous limits.

**Which has better output quality?**  
Gemini 3.1 Pro in Pitch edges benchmarks for complex analysis; GPT-5.3 in Decktopus matches for slide-specific tasks.

**Can I use both?**  
Yes, export PPTX from Decktopus to Pitch for team refinement.
