# Decktopus AI vs Pitch

[Decktopus AI](/tools/decktopus/) and [Pitch](/tools/pitch/) both help people create presentations with AI, but the purchase decision is really workflow-shaped. Decktopus is strongest when a creator wants a fast AI deck or interactive document with forms, embeds, analytics, voice recording, custom domains, and mobile-responsive sharing. Pitch is strongest when presentations are shared team assets that need real-time editing, comments, guests, roles, advanced links, pitch rooms, version history, PowerPoint exports, and engagement analytics.

The practical rule: **choose Decktopus when speed and interactive presentation delivery matter most. Choose Pitch when collaboration, governance, and sales-style sharing matter most.** This comparison was re-verified on **June 12, 2026** against official Decktopus pricing, Decktopus FAQ, Pitch pricing, and Pitch AI-credit help sources.

## Quick Answer
**Pick Decktopus AI for fast prompt-to-deck creation and interactive delivery.** Decktopus Pro is $14.99/month billed annually with 9,000 AI credits per year, about 300 AI presentations per year, AI assistance, AI presentation generation, AI image generation, and PDF export. Business is $34.99/user/month billed annually with 12,000 AI credits per year, about 400 AI presentations per year for the organization, custom domain connection, slide analytics, webhook, and team/organization features. Decktopus says AI deck creation costs 30 credits.

**Pick Pitch for team presentation operations.** Pitch's June 2026 pricing page lists Free, Plus, Team, Business, and Enterprise paths. Free supports up to 5 members, unlimited presentations, 100 one-time AI credits, custom templates, branded sharing links, branded PDF exports, and 2 guests. Plus is a one-person paid workspace at $15/month monthly or $13/month yearly. Team is $23/seat/month monthly or $19/seat/month yearly with 6,000 AI credits per seat per year, up to 25 members, advanced links, 2 shared pitch rooms, custom domain, embeds, roles, and 30-day version history. Business is $30/seat/month monthly or $25/seat/month yearly with 9,000 AI credits per seat per year, up to 200 members, unlimited guests, unlimited advanced links, unlimited pitch rooms, batch creation, asset library collections, and unlimited version history.

There is no universal winner. Decktopus is better for fast interactive presenter workflows. Pitch is better when the deck is part of a durable team operating system.

## Winner By Use Case

- **Fast first draft from a prompt:** Decktopus AI.
- **Solo pitch, proposal, lesson, or client update:** Decktopus AI.
- **Interactive documents with forms, embeds, analytics, or voice:** Decktopus AI.
- **Mobile-responsive microsite-style presentation sharing:** Decktopus AI.
- **Team deck collaboration and comments:** Pitch.
- **Sales sharing with advanced links and pitch rooms:** Pitch.
- **Guest access, workspace roles, and permissions:** Pitch.
- **PowerPoint exports inside a reusable team workflow:** Pitch.
- **Brand-controlled presentation system for a larger team:** Pitch for workspace operations; Decktopus Enterprise for custom corporate AI slide generation.

## Where Decktopus AI Wins

Decktopus wins when the buyer wants a presentation generated quickly and packaged as an interactive artifact. The official pricing and FAQ pages describe forms for collecting data, voice recording for virtual presentation delivery, analytics for opens and page views, URL embeds, website embedding, custom domains, mobile-responsive documents, collaboration, and webhooks.

That matters because many buyers are not trying to manage a shared deck library. They are trying to send a proposal, collect a response, book a call, support a webinar, explain a service, or turn a PDF into a visual presentation. Decktopus is a cleaner fit for that motion.

Decktopus is not the best fit if the team needs a polished multi-seat deck operating system. It has collaboration and business features, but the larger product advantage is speed, guided generation, and interactive delivery.

## Where Pitch Wins

Pitch wins when the deck needs to move through people. Its pricing page and help center make the workflow clear: workspaces, member limits, guests, custom templates, PowerPoint exports, advanced links, shared pitch rooms, custom domains, embeds, co-presenting, content variables, workspace roles, version history, batch creation, and asset library collections.

Pitch also has the stronger sales and client-distribution layer. Advanced links and pitch rooms help teams measure who viewed a deck, how they engaged, and which materials belong together. That is valuable when the deck is not "done" when it looks good. It still has to be reviewed, shared, tracked, revised, and reused.

Pitch is weaker if the buyer wants the most direct path from prompt to an interactive hosted presentation. Pitch AI can help create and refine slides, but Pitch's strongest case is team workflow rather than one-click presentation funnel creation.

## Pricing And Plan Guidance

## Buyer Recommendations

**Choose Decktopus AI if:**

- You need a presentable deck quickly from a prompt, notes, pasted text, or a PDF.
- You want the deck to behave like an interactive proposal, microsite, lesson, or presenter link.
- You care about forms, embeds, voice recording, slide analytics, custom domains, and mobile-responsive sharing.
- You are a founder, educator, consultant, account executive, marketer, or small business owner creating decks without a design team.
- Your main risk is spending too many hours making a useful presentation look good enough to send.

**Choose Pitch if:**

- Multiple people need to edit, comment, review, present, or manage versions.
- You need external guests, free commenters, workspace roles, and team permissions.
- Sales or client-facing teams need advanced links, pitch rooms, visit analytics, custom domains, and reusable resources.
- Your team wants custom templates, custom fonts, PowerPoint exports, variables, asset collections, and governed presentation systems.
- Your main risk is deck chaos across sales, marketing, success, founders, clients, and reviewers.

## What To Avoid

- **Do not buy Decktopus expecting Pitch-style team operations.** Decktopus has business features, but Pitch is stronger for roles, guests, pitch rooms, version history, and team-scale deck governance.
- **Do not buy Pitch only to make a fast one-off AI deck.** Pitch can generate and improve decks, but Decktopus is more direct when the job is quick prompt-to-presentation output.
- **Do not ignore AI credit overages.** Pitch charges $0.004 per extra AI credit on paid plans; Decktopus uses annual AI credits and a 30-credit AI deck cost.
- **Do not ignore export and handoff.** Test PDF, PPTX, links, mobile presentation behavior, embedded content, and client-facing analytics before standardizing.
- **Do not overbuy team seats before proving adoption.** The value comes from the team actually using the workflow after the first week.

## Bottom Line

Decktopus AI is the stronger choice for fast AI deck generation, interactive presentations, microsite-style sharing, forms, embeds, voice, and lightweight analytics. Pitch is the stronger choice for teams that collaborate on decks, send them externally, track engagement, manage guests, create pitch rooms, and maintain a reusable presentation system.

If you are a solo seller, consultant, educator, founder, or marketer trying to produce a useful interactive deck quickly, start with Decktopus. If you are a sales, marketing, startup, agency, or success team that treats decks as ongoing revenue assets, start with Pitch.

## FAQ

**Is Decktopus AI better than Pitch?**

Decktopus AI is better for fast AI-generated presentations, interactive documents, forms, embeds, voice recording, custom domains, and mobile-responsive sharing. Pitch is better for team collaboration, guests, advanced links, pitch rooms, engagement analytics, PowerPoint exports, roles, and version history.

**Which is cheaper?**

Decktopus Pro is $14.99/month billed annually. Pitch has a Free workspace, while Pitch Plus is $15/month monthly or $13/month yearly for one person. For teams, Decktopus Business is $34.99/user/month billed annually, while Pitch Team is $23/seat/month monthly or $19/seat/month yearly and Pitch Business is $30/seat/month monthly or $25/seat/month yearly. Verify live checkout before buying.

**Which is better for sales teams?**

Pitch is usually better for sales teams that need advanced links, pitch rooms, engagement analytics, custom domains, guests, roles, and reusable resources. Decktopus is better for solo sellers or small teams that want interactive proposals, forms, voice, and faster AI-generated presentation funnels.

**Can I use both?**

Yes, but most teams should avoid two deck systems. If you test both, decide whether Decktopus owns interactive delivery or Pitch owns the final team deck before sharing externally.
