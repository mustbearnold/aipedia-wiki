# Sudowrite vs Wordtune

[Sudowrite](../tools/sudowrite.md) and [Wordtune](../tools/wordtune.md) are both writing tools, but they live on opposite sides of the draft. Sudowrite helps create fiction. Wordtune helps polish existing sentences.

## Quick Answer

Choose **Sudowrite** if you are writing fiction, scripts, scenes, genre prose, or a manuscript. Choose **Wordtune** if you already wrote the sentence and want it clearer, shorter, more formal, more casual, or more fluent without losing your voice. Sudowrite generates story. Wordtune edits prose.

## Decision Snapshot

| | Sudowrite | Wordtune |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary job** | Fiction drafting and story development | Voice-preserving sentence rewrites |
| **Best paid tier** | Professional for most working novelists | Unlimited for heavy daily editing |
| **Pricing shape** | Paid credit tiers | Freemium simplified consumer ladder |
| **Best for** | Novels, scenes, characters, worldbuilding | Emails, docs, essays, professional writing |
| **Avoid if** | You need factual or business editing | You need blank-page fiction generation |

## Where Sudowrite Wins

- **Fiction-specific generation.** Sudowrite is built around scenes, chapters, description, dialogue, genre, and narrative pacing.
- **Story Engine and Canvas.** Long-form projects get structure that a sentence-level rewriter cannot provide.
- **Muse positioning.** Sudowrite markets Muse as its fiction-focused model layer, with model routing for different creative tasks.
- **Creative unblock.** Brainstorm, Describe, Expand, Rewrite, and Feedback help writers move through stuck scenes.
- **Genre tolerance.** It is much stronger for romance, horror, fantasy, thriller, and emotionally intense fiction workflows.

## Where Wordtune Wins

- **Everyday polish.** Wordtune is faster for improving an email, report sentence, LinkedIn post, essay paragraph, or browser draft.
- **Voice preservation.** It generally stays closer to the writer's original intent than a story-generation system.
- **Lower-cost utility.** Wordtune's Basic, Advanced, and Unlimited ladder is easier to justify for non-fiction daily editing.
- **Inline workflow.** It works best where writing already happens: web editors, Gmail, Google Docs, LinkedIn, and browser surfaces.
- **No story setup.** Highlight text, pick a rewrite, and move on.

## Key Differences

Sudowrite is a creative-writing workspace. It is valuable when the writer needs to invent, expand, or reshape story material. The credit model makes sense if those generations help produce fiction, but it is inefficient for ordinary email and business writing.

Wordtune is an editing layer. It is valuable when the writer already has a draft and wants a cleaner version. It is not designed to invent a novel, track character arcs, or manage a manuscript.

The old comparison leaned on unsupported flagship-model claims. The real choice is workflow: fiction generation versus sentence-level polish.

## Who Should Choose Sudowrite

Choose Sudowrite if you are a novelist, screenwriter, roleplay writer, game writer, or genre-fiction author who wants AI help with scenes, beats, descriptions, and story continuity.

## Who Should Choose Wordtune

Choose Wordtune if you are a professional, student, freelancer, non-native English writer, or team member who needs fast rewrites across everyday documents and messages.

## Bottom Line

Sudowrite is the right tool before the draft exists. Wordtune is the right tool after the sentence exists. Buy Sudowrite for fiction; buy Wordtune for daily editing.

## FAQ

**Which is cheaper?**
Wordtune is cheaper for everyday editing. Sudowrite costs more because it is a specialized fiction workspace with generation credits.

**Which has better output quality?**
Sudowrite is better for fiction prose and story expansion. Wordtune is better for preserving voice in emails, docs, and non-fiction sentences.

**Can I use both?**
Yes. Draft fiction in Sudowrite, then use Wordtune only for specific clarity edits. For full manuscript editing, a general assistant or human editor may still be more useful.
