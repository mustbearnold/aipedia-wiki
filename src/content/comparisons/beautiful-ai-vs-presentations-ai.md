# Beautiful.ai vs Presentations.AI

[Beautiful.ai](/tools/beautiful-ai/) and [Presentations.AI](/tools/presentations-ai/) both promise faster business presentations, but they are not equivalent buying decisions. Beautiful.ai is the safer default when the deck needs brand control, Smart Slides, collaboration, PowerPoint export, viewer analytics, and a mature team workflow. Presentations.AI is the budget-friendly annual option when you mainly need AI-generated deck drafts, brand themes, PDF export, and lightweight sharing.

The practical rule: **choose Beautiful.ai if the presentation will represent a company, team, client, or sales motion. Choose Presentations.AI if you want a lower-cost annual AI deck generator and can tolerate more manual review.** This page was re-verified on **May 8, 2026** against current official Beautiful.ai and Presentations.AI sources.

## Quick Verdict

**Beautiful.ai wins for most business buyers.** Its current pricing page lists Pro at $12/month billed annually, Team at $40/user/month billed annually or $50/user/month billed monthly, and Enterprise at custom pricing. The same page confirms a 14-day free trial for Pro or Team, unlimited AI content generation, custom brand styling, extra context from files or links, AI image generation, an AI writing assistant, translation, Smart Slide auto-design, more than 300 Smart Slide layouts, PowerPoint import/export, viewer analytics, live collaboration, workspace libraries, shared assets, version control, permissions, SSO, audit logs, and security certifications on Enterprise.

**Presentations.AI is still worth considering for annual-budget buyers.** Its pricing page currently lists Starter at $0 with limited AI credits, Public beta at $40/year for up to 10 members, Pro at $198/year under early-bird pricing, and Enterprise as custom. Its product page emphasizes effortless creation, personalized design, anti-fragile templates, PowerPoint compatibility, Brand Sync, sharing, access control, analytics, multi-device editing, and multilingual support.

That makes Beautiful.ai the better recommendation when brand reliability and team workflow matter. Presentations.AI is the cheaper experiment when the buyer wants AI-generated slides first and can polish the result elsewhere.

## Winner By Use Case

- **Company-branded decks:** Beautiful.ai.
- **Marketing, sales, and executive presentations:** Beautiful.ai.
- **Teams that need permissions, shared libraries, and analytics:** Beautiful.ai.
- **One-off AI deck experiments:** Presentations.AI Starter or Public beta.
- **Lowest annual entry price:** Presentations.AI Public beta, if the current early-bird offer still appears at checkout.
- **PowerPoint handoff:** Beautiful.ai for mature import/export workflow; Presentations.AI if basic PPT compatibility is enough.
- **Strict governance or enterprise controls:** Beautiful.ai Enterprise.

## Where Beautiful.ai Wins

Beautiful.ai wins because it is built around controlled presentation production, not just prompt-to-slide output. Its AI presentation page describes a guided workflow that moves from prompt to outline to final designs, while Smart Slides handle formatting, resizing, alignment, and animation. That matters for teams because the first AI draft is rarely the final artifact. The deck still needs brand fit, spacing discipline, reusable layouts, sharing, analytics, and export reliability.

Beautiful.ai's pricing page is also more mature for business use. Pro includes unlimited AI content generation, custom brand styling, context from files or links, AI image generation, translation, more than 300 Smart Slide layouts, dynamic data visualizations, embedded video/audio, custom themes, and PowerPoint import/export. Team adds live collaborative editing, centralized slide and template libraries, version control, locked slides, shared assets, live data linking, engagement tracking, viewer analytics, permissions, and multilingual workflows. Enterprise adds SSO, priority support, audit/change logs, SCIM, configurable brand guardrails, and security certifications listed by the vendor.

The buyer value is simple: Beautiful.ai reduces the risk that AI-generated slides become off-brand, inconsistent, or hard to manage. If a sales team, agency, consultant, founder, or marketing team sends decks to real prospects, this reliability is worth more than the lowest headline price.

## Where Presentations.AI Wins

Presentations.AI wins when price and fast AI drafting matter more than governance. Its current pricing page is annual-first and visibly cheaper at the entry level: Starter is listed at $0 with limited AI credits, while Public beta is listed at $40/year for up to 10 members under an early-bird offer. Pro is listed at $198/year, and Enterprise is custom.

Its product page covers the right basics: AI-driven slide creation, personalized design, templates that adapt to content changes, PowerPoint export, Brand Sync, sharing, access control, analytics, multi-device access, and multilingual support. For a founder, student, early consultant, or small team that mainly wants AI to create a first draft from rough ideas, Presentations.AI can be a useful low-cost testing ground.

The limitation is confidence. Presentations.AI's public pages do not justify older claims about specific frontier models, giant input capacity, or unlimited slide generation. AiPedia removed those claims because they were not verifiable from official sources on May 8, 2026. Buy Presentations.AI for the visible product and pricing, not for unsupported model promises.

## Pricing And Plan Guidance

For most business buyers, start with **Beautiful.ai Pro** if one person creates decks, or **Beautiful.ai Team** if multiple people need collaboration, shared assets, templates, locked slides, analytics, and permissions. Enterprise is the right path for SSO, audit logs, SCIM, and stronger brand guardrails.

Use **Presentations.AI Starter** only for testing. Consider **Public beta** if the $40/year early-bird offer is still live and you want a low-cost annual AI deck tool for a small group. Consider **Pro** if you need more credits, Pro templates, custom fonts/colors, analytics, PDF export, and advanced features. Use Enterprise only if you have a clear requirement for custom support, unlimited credits, admin capabilities, company templates, and enterprise security.

Do not compare $12/month Beautiful.ai Pro with $198/year Presentations.AI Pro as a pure price race. Beautiful.ai is charging for a more mature presentation production system. Presentations.AI is selling lower-cost AI deck creation. The right purchase depends on whether the deck must be trusted externally or only drafted quickly.

## What To Avoid

- **Do not buy based on old model claims.** The previous page mentioned specific models and input-capacity numbers that AiPedia could not verify from official sources.
- **Do not assume Presentations.AI is monthly.** Its current pricing page presents annual/early-bird pricing, and prices may vary by country.
- **Do not use Presentations.AI as the final layer for high-stakes brand decks without review.** Treat it as a draft engine unless your team has tested output quality.
- **Do not overbuy Beautiful.ai Team if one person owns decks.** Pro may be enough until collaboration, libraries, permissions, or analytics become necessary.
- **Do not skip export testing.** Test PowerPoint, PDF, sharing links, analytics, and brand fidelity before standardizing either tool.

## Bottom Line

Beautiful.ai is the stronger buyer decision for companies, agencies, sales teams, marketers, consultants, and founders whose decks need to look controlled and survive collaboration. Presentations.AI is the lower-cost annual AI deck generator for people who want to test prompt-to-presentation workflows and can tolerate extra editing.

If the deck represents your business, start with Beautiful.ai. If the deck is a draft, internal brainstorm, class project, or low-budget experiment, test Presentations.AI first.

## FAQ

**Is Beautiful.ai better than Presentations.AI?**

Beautiful.ai is better for brand-controlled business decks, team workflows, Smart Slides, collaboration, analytics, and enterprise controls. Presentations.AI is better for low-cost AI deck experiments and annual-budget buyers.

**Which is cheaper?**

Presentations.AI has the lower annual entry point if its Public beta early-bird pricing remains available. Beautiful.ai Pro is $12/month billed annually, while Team is $40/user/month billed annually or $50/user/month billed monthly. Check both live pricing pages before buying.

**Which plan should most people buy?**

Buy Beautiful.ai Pro for solo business deck creation. Buy Beautiful.ai Team when collaboration and shared assets matter. Use Presentations.AI Public beta or Starter only if you want a lower-cost AI drafting tool.

**Can I use both?**

Yes, but avoid version sprawl. A practical workflow is Presentations.AI for rough AI drafts and Beautiful.ai for final brand polish, if export/import quality is acceptable.
