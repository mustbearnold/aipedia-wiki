# Perplexity vs Poe

[Perplexity](/tools/perplexity/) and [Poe](/tools/poe/) both let you reach many AI models in one place, but they package that very differently in June 2026. Perplexity is a cited AI search and answer engine: it routes questions to frontier models and returns source-linked answers, with Deep Research and the Comet browser. Poe is a model aggregator and chat platform: raw access to GPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, DeepSeek, and thousands of bots in one chat UI, billed by points.

## Quick Answer

Choose **Perplexity** if you want a curated, cited research experience: sourced answers from the live web with model switching handled for you. Choose **Poe** if you want raw multi-model chat: pick any model or bot yourself, compare outputs, and build custom bots, all under one subscription.

The split is **cited answer engine versus raw multi-model chat**. Perplexity optimizes the answer (sources, recency, research). Poe optimizes access (breadth of models and bots, comparison, customization).

## Where Perplexity Wins

- **Citations by default.** Every answer links its sources, the biggest trust advantage for research.
- **Live web research.** Built to answer current questions from the web, not just model knowledge.
- **Deep Research.** Longer, structured reports with exports for serious investigation.
- **Comet browser.** An AI browser (free) plus Comet Plus included in Pro and Max extends search into browsing.
- **Developer API.** The Sonar search and answer API lets you build cited search into your own products.

## Where Poe Wins

- **Raw model breadth.** Direct access to GPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, DeepSeek, open-weight models, and media bots, chosen by you.
- **Model comparison.** Run the same prompt across bots to see which wins on your task.
- **Custom and creator bots.** Build, share, and monetize bots through Poe's creator program.
- **One bill for everything.** A single points-based subscription instead of several provider plans.
- **Media and specialist bots.** Image, video, and niche bots aggregate alongside chat models.

## Plan Guidance

Perplexity has a usable **Free** answer engine, **Pro at $20/month** ($17/month annual, including a small monthly Sonar API credit), and **Max at $200/month** (unlimited Labs, every frontier model, Perplexity Computer, and the Comet browser). Teams use **Enterprise Pro ($40/seat) or Enterprise Max ($325/seat)**.

Poe is **points-based**: a free tier for casual use, a low-cost entry tier, a first serious monthly bucket roughly in the $20/month range, and premium tiers up to very heavy use. Each bot charges a per-message point cost, so expensive frontier and media bots draw points faster.

Do not buy either on the headline price alone. Perplexity is the better value when you want sourced answers and research depth without managing model choice. Poe is the better value when you want to drive model selection yourself, compare outputs, or build bots. They are different products more than different prices.

## Watch-Outs

Perplexity curates the experience around cited answers, so you have less direct control over exactly which model handles a query and it is less of a freeform multi-bot playground. Poe gives you that raw control but does not focus on citations or live-web sourcing, so it is weaker when you need verifiable, source-linked research. Poe's points accounting can also surprise users, since expensive bots burn points quickly.

Both are aggregator-style products rather than first-party model makers, so provider-specific native features (Artifacts, Canvas, Deep Research from the source app) are not always replicated at full fidelity in either.

## Who Should Choose Perplexity

Choose Perplexity if you want cited, source-linked answers, current web research, Deep Research for serious investigation, or a search API to build cited answers into your own product.

## Who Should Choose Poe

Choose Poe if you want raw access to many models and bots under one subscription, you compare model outputs yourself, or you build and share custom bots.

## Bottom Line

Pick Perplexity for a cited research experience where the answer and its sources matter most. Pick Poe for raw multi-model chat, comparison, and custom bots. Some people use both: Poe to explore and compare models, Perplexity when they need sourced, verifiable answers.

## FAQ

**What is the core difference?**

Perplexity is a cited answer engine that picks models for you and links sources. Poe is a model aggregator where you pick the model or bot yourself, without a citation focus.

**Which is better for research?**

Perplexity, because every answer attaches sources and it searches the live web. Poe is better for comparing model outputs and building bots than for cited research.

**Which gives more model control?**

Poe. You choose any model or bot directly. Perplexity manages model selection around its answer experience, though paid tiers expose model choice.

**Can I use both together?**

Yes. Many people use Poe to compare models and prototype bots, and Perplexity when they need cited, verifiable answers.
