# Presentations.AI vs Tome

[Presentations.AI](/tools/presentations-ai/) is the active choice over [Tome](/tools/tome/) for buyers who want AI-generated presentations with team collaboration, brand themes, templates, analytics, export paths, and enterprise controls. Tome Slides was sunset on April 30, 2025 according to Tome's official Help Center, and the founding team later pivoted into Lightfield, an AI-native CRM.

The practical buyer answer: **use Presentations.AI if you wanted an AI-first presentation generator but need a current product with team and brand controls. Do not shortlist Tome for new presentation work.** This comparison was re-verified on **June 8, 2026** against current Presentations.AI, Tome, VentureBeat, and Lightfield sources.

## Quick Verdict

**Winner: Presentations.AI.** Presentations.AI remains an active AI presentation product with a current pricing page. As verified on June 8, 2026, its English pricing page lists Starter at $0 with 100 credits, Pro at $20/month billed annually with 5,000 credits, and Gold at $100/month billed annually with 50,000 credits. The page also lists PowerPoint/PDF export, unbranded public links, password-protected links on paid tiers, guest collaboration, brand customization, analytics, shared knowledge base on Gold, and agent features marked as coming soon or tier-gated.

**Tome is not a current buyer option.** The older page treated Tome as a live solo/storytelling tool with active pricing and model claims. That is outdated. Tome Slides was sunset, and old Tome presentation data and pricing should be treated as historical.

## Why Tome Is Not A Current Choice

Tome's Help Center says Tome Slides was sunsetting on April 30, 2025. It told users to migrate important work before that date, said users would not be able to sign in or access Tome content afterward, and said all user data, including Tome Slides files, would be deleted after April 30, 2025.

That changes the purpose of this route. It should not pretend Presentations.AI and Tome are two equal current choices. It should answer the real 2026 question: if someone once considered Tome for AI-generated decks, when should they choose Presentations.AI instead?

## Where Presentations.AI Wins

Presentations.AI wins for teams that want AI deck generation with brand and collaboration controls. Its current pricing page emphasizes brand themes, team collaboration, seamless sharing, pro templates, custom themes, analytics, PDF export, additional AI credits, custom fonts and colors, company templates, admin capabilities, dedicated support, and enterprise security. That is a better fit for teams than chasing a discontinued product with no current presentation roadmap.

Presentations.AI is especially strong if you want:

- AI-generated presentations with current signups and pricing.
- Brand themes, custom fonts/colors, and pro templates.
- Team collaboration and publishing/sharing.
- Analytics and PDF export.
- Enterprise controls such as company templates, admin capabilities, support, and security.

## Where Tome Used To Win

Tome used to win on story-first creation. It felt lightweight and creative, especially for founders and solo users who wanted to communicate an idea quickly. That history explains why people still search for Tome alternatives.

But Presentations.AI is stronger for a current team procurement decision. It is active, has live pricing, and exposes team and enterprise features. Tome's former creative appeal does not overcome the shutdown.

## Pricing Reality

Presentations.AI has current pricing. On June 8, 2026, the English pricing page showed Starter at $0 with 100 credits, Pro at $20/month billed annually with 5,000 credits, and Gold at $100/month billed annually with 50,000 credits. It also listed AI credits, model tiers, presentation refresh, project knowledge, agent features, PowerPoint/PDF export, unbranded links, password-protected links, guest collaboration, brand kit features, and analytics. Buyers in non-US regions should still verify the checkout because Presentations.AI says prices may vary by country.

Tome Slides has no current presentation pricing. Do not rely on old Tome Free/Pro/Enterprise tiers for current buying decisions.

Choose Presentations.AI if:

- You need an active AI presentation generator with team collaboration.
- Your team cares about brand themes, templates, fonts, colors, and publishing.

Presentations.AI is the active option for buyers who want AI-generated presentations with brand themes, collaboration, analytics, PDF export, and team or enterprise controls. Tome is a discontinued presentation product and should not be used for new work.
