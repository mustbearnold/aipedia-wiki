# Presentations.AI vs Tome

[Presentations.AI](../tools/presentations-ai.md) and [Tome](../tools/tome.md) are AI presentation tools that generate slides from text prompts or documents as of April 2026. Presentations.AI focuses on enterprise teams with custom branding, while Tome targets individual creators with narrative-driven outputs.

## Quick Answer

Tome edges out for solo users and quick storytelling due to its free tier and intuitive flow; Presentations.AI suits teams needing collaboration and brand controls at similar paid rates.

## At a Glance

| | Presentations.AI | Tome |
|---|---|---|
| **Flagship** | Presentations.AI Enterprise (GPT-5.3 integration) | Tome Pro (Claude Sonnet 4.6) |
| **Price** | Free; Pro $15/user/mo; Enterprise $25/user/mo | Free; Pro $16/mo; Enterprise $40/user/mo |
| **Context Window** | 128K tokens | 200K tokens |
| **Best For** | Team collaboration, brand kits | Narrative slides, individual use |

## Where Presentations.AI Wins

- Real-time multiplayer editing supports team workflows better than Tome's async comments.[1]
- Brand kits lock colors, fonts, and logos across decks for consistent enterprise outputs.
- Export options include PPTX, PDF, and video with higher fidelity than Tome's basics.
- Usage analytics track deck views and engagement for sales teams.
- Custom node library lets users build reusable slide templates.

## Where Tome Wins

- Narrative mode auto-arranges content into story arcs with smoother transitions.
- Free tier allows unlimited public decks, more generous than Presentations.AI's 3-deck limit.
- Embed interactive elements like charts and videos directly in slides.
- Faster generation times average 20% quicker for 10-slide decks.
- Mobile app supports on-the-go editing absent in Presentations.AI.

## Key Differences

Presentations.AI prioritizes structured team use with tools like shared workspaces and admin controls, making it fit for sales and marketing groups; Tome emphasizes creative flow with AI-driven layouts and embeds, appealing to designers and executives. Pricing overlaps on pro tiers around $15/mo, but Tome's free plan offers more decks while Presentations.AI caps at three. Context handling favors Tome's 200K tokens for long docs versus Presentations.AI's 128K, though both integrate recent models like GPT-5.3 and Claude Sonnet 4.6 for generation.[1][2]

## Who should choose Presentations.AI

Sales and marketing teams benefit from its collaboration and branding features for consistent client pitches.

## Who should choose Tome

Freelancers and executives prefer its narrative tools and free access for rapid personal decks.

## Bottom Line

Choice depends on use: pick Presentations.AI for team scale and controls, Tome for solo speed and stories. Test free tiers to match your workflow, as both deliver solid AI slides in 2026.

## FAQ

**Which is cheaper?**  
Tome's free tier supports unlimited public decks; Presentations.AI limits free to three. Paid pro plans sit at $15-16/mo.[1]

**Which has better output quality?**  
Tome leads in narrative flow and embeds; Presentations.AI excels in branded, editable exports. Quality varies by prompt style.[2]

**Can I use both?**  
Yes, export PPTX from either and import to the other for hybrid workflows.
