# Apollo.io vs Clay

[Apollo.io](../tools/apollo.md) and [Clay](../tools/clay.md) are sales automation platforms that combine data enrichment, lead scoring, and workflow tools as of April 2026. Apollo.io focuses on B2B prospecting with integrated email outreach, while Clay emphasizes customizable data pipelines for research and enrichment.

## Quick Answer

Clay suits teams building complex data workflows and custom automations; Apollo.io fits sales teams needing all-in-one prospecting and outreach.

## Where Apollo.io Wins

- Larger database covers 275+ million contacts for direct B2B prospecting.[Apollo.io pricing page](https://www.apollo.io/pricing)
- Built-in email outreach and sequences send personalized campaigns at scale.[Apollo.io features](https://www.apollo.io/product/sequences)
- Lower entry pricing starts at $49/user/month for core sales tools.[Apollo.io pricing page](https://www.apollo.io/pricing)
- Dialer and meeting scheduler integrate calling with CRM workflows.[Apollo.io dialer docs](https://knowledge.apollo.io/hc/en-us/articles/4411234194701-Apollo-Dialer-Overview)
- Compliance tools handle GDPR, CCPA for enterprise sales teams.[Apollo.io trust center](https://www.apollo.io/trust)

## Where Clay Wins

- Flexible table-based interface builds custom enrichment pipelines from 100+ sources.[Clay docs](https://www.clay.com/docs)
- AI agents automate multi-step research like competitor analysis or persona building.[Clay AI features](https://www.clay.com/ai)
- Higher customization connects to any API or database without code.[Clay integrations](https://www.clay.com/integrations)
- Collaborative tables allow team editing and real-time updates.[Clay collaboration guide](https://www.clay.com/features/collaboration)
- Export to CRM or spreadsheets in one click for downstream workflows.[Clay export options](https://www.clay.com/docs/exporting-data)

## Key Differences

Apollo.io provides a complete sales stack with prospecting, enrichment, and outreach in one platform, priced from $49/user/month, ideal for revenue teams executing campaigns. Clay offers modular data tables for building research workflows, starting at $149/user/month, better for operations or marketing teams needing tailored pipelines. Apollo.io's 275 million contact database exceeds Clay's reliance on external providers, but Clay's 1 million+ integrations enable broader data sourcing.[Apollo.io knowledge base](https://knowledge.apollo.io); [Clay integration directory](https://www.clay.com/integrations).

## Who should choose Apollo.io

Sales development reps and account executives who send outbound emails daily. Small to mid-size sales teams consolidating tools into one dashboard.

## Who should choose Clay

Growth operators, analysts, or agencies running custom research at scale. Teams integrating data across CRMs, APIs, and spreadsheets.

## Bottom Line

Choose Apollo.io for straightforward B2B sales execution with emailing included. Pick Clay for advanced data workflows where customization drives value. Many teams use both: Apollo.io for outreach, Clay for enrichment.

## FAQ

**Which is cheaper?**  
Apollo.io starts at $49/user/month; Clay at $149/user/month. Apollo.io costs less for basic sales needs.[Apollo.io pricing](https://www.apollo.io/pricing); [Clay pricing](https://www.clay.com/pricing).

**Which has better output quality?**  
Clay delivers higher customization for complex research; Apollo.io provides reliable B2B contact data. Depends on use case.[Clay case studies](https://www.clay.com/customers); [Apollo.io accuracy reports](https://www.apollo.io/data-accuracy).

**Can I use both?**  
Yes, integrate via API or CSV exports. Use Clay to enrich leads, then Apollo.io for outreach.[Clay API docs](https://www.clay.com/docs/api); [Apollo.io imports](https://knowledge.apollo.io/hc/en-us/articles/4409614236051-Import-Contacts).
