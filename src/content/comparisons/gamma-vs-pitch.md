# Gamma vs Pitch

[Gamma](/tools/gamma/) and [Pitch](/tools/pitch/) solve different presentation jobs. Gamma is the faster AI creation workspace for turning a prompt, outline, PDF, or PPTX into a deck, doc, website, social post, image, or shareable page. Pitch is the stronger team presentation workspace for comments, guests, roles, PowerPoint exports, advanced links, pitch rooms, custom domains, co-presenting, version history, AI credits, and engagement analytics.

## Quick Answer

Choose Gamma if the bottleneck is "make a useful deck exist." Choose Pitch if the bottleneck is "help a team review, share, measure, and reuse decks." Solo founders, consultants, educators, and marketers should usually test Gamma first. Sales, marketing, agency, startup, and internal-communications teams should shortlist Pitch when the deck keeps moving through people after the first draft.

## Decision Snapshot

- **Best first draft:** Gamma. Its pricing page confirms PDF/PPTX import plus PDF, PPTX, PNG, and Google Slides export on Free, with higher card-per-prompt limits and API access on paid tiers.
- **Best team workflow:** Pitch. Its pricing page is built around members, guests, AI credits, advanced links, pitch rooms, domains, embeds, roles, and version history.
- **Best output breadth:** Gamma. Presentations, docs, websites, social, images, graphics, and API access are part of the current product surface.
- **Best sales/deck ops:** Pitch. Advanced links, engagement analytics, shared pitch rooms, and external guests are closer to revenue workflow than Gamma's solo-generation lane.
- **Main watch-out:** Gamma can require export and brand-system testing; Pitch can be too much process when one person only needs a fast AI draft.

## Recent Changes

- **June 26, 2026:** Rechecked Gamma pricing and API help. Free still supports simple projects, imports, and exports; Pro is the first self-serve tier with API access; Ultra remains the high-usage tier with 75 cards per prompt, advanced model access, and up to 100 custom domains.
- **June 26, 2026:** Rechecked Pitch pricing and AI-credit help. Free still supports up to 5 members, unlimited presentations, and 100 one-time AI credits. Plus, Team, and Business still scale credits, guests, advanced links, pitch rooms, custom domains, roles, and version history.

## Where Gamma Wins

- **Prompt-to-deck speed.** Gamma is the cleaner answer when the user starts from a rough idea, notes, a PDF, or an imported deck.
- **Multi-format content.** Gamma can produce presentations, documents, websites, social posts, and images from the same workspace.
- **Flexible publishing.** Pro and Ultra add detailed analytics, advanced sharing, custom domains, API access, workspace templates, and more AI model access.
- **Low-friction testing.** Free supports simple projects, imports, and exports without forcing a team workspace decision.
- **Creator fit.** Consultants, founders, educators, and marketers get more value when the output might become a deck, leave-behind, page, or social asset.

## Where Pitch Wins

- **Collaboration.** Pitch is built for shared editing, comments, guests, roles, co-presenting, templates, and version history.
- **Distribution.** Advanced links, pitch rooms, custom domains, and analytics help teams understand how a deck is used after it is sent.
- **Workspace pricing clarity.** Free supports up to 5 members; Plus is for one user; Team supports up to 25 members; Business supports up to 200; Enterprise is custom.
- **AI-credit transparency.** Pitch documents 100 lifetime Free credits, 3,000 annual Plus credits, 6,000 annual credits per Team seat, and 9,000 annual credits per Business seat.
- **Repeatable deck operations.** Pitch is stronger for teams that maintain deck libraries instead of creating one-off AI outputs.

## Pricing and Plan Guidance

For Gamma, start with Free when testing prompt-to-deck output. Move to Plus for more cards per prompt and branding removal. Move to Pro when custom branding, analytics, advanced sharing, custom domains, API access, and workspace templates matter. Use Ultra for heavier usage, 75 cards per prompt, and the most advanced Gamma models.

For Pitch, Free is enough to test a small workspace. Plus is a one-person professional lane at $15/month monthly or $13/month yearly. Team is the practical starting point when shared pitch rooms, advanced links, external guests, roles, and version history matter. Business is for larger teams that need unlimited guests, advanced links, shared pitch rooms, batch creation, asset collections, and unlimited version history.

## Bottom Line

Gamma is the better AI deck generator for most individuals. Pitch is the better presentation workspace for teams. If the work begins with messy source material and one owner, start with Gamma. If the deck must move through reviewers, prospects, links, rooms, analytics, and version history, start with Pitch.

## FAQ

**Is Gamma better than Pitch?**
Gamma is better for fast AI deck creation and flexible outputs. Pitch is better for collaborative deck operations.

**Is Pitch an AI presentation generator?**
Yes, but its durable value is the team workflow around presentations, not only the initial AI generation.

**Can I use both?**
Yes. Draft in Gamma, then move final team-facing decks into Pitch for comments, sharing, tracking, and distribution. Test export fidelity before relying on that process.
