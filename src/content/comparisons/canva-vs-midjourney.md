# Canva AI vs Midjourney

[Canva AI](../tools/canva.md) and [Midjourney](../tools/midjourney.md) generate images and designs for overlapping use cases. Canva AI integrates AI into a full design platform for teams and beginners, while Midjourney focuses on high-detail images via Discord prompts as of April 2026[2][5].

## Quick Answer

Midjourney produces higher-detail artistic images for professionals; Canva AI suits quick team designs and editing.


|---|---|---|
| **Flagship** | Magic Studio with Dream Lab (powered by Leonardo.ai Phoenix 1.0, Imagen 4) | V8 |
| **Price** | Free; Pro $15/mo (billed annually) or $20/mo | Basic $10/mo; Standard $30/mo; Pro $60/mo; Mega $120/mo |
| **Output Specs** | Up to 4K images; video up to 1080p/30s; 100+ templates | Up to 8K images; 60s/5min video (Pro+); aspect ratios 1:10 to 16:9 |
| **Best For** | Team collaboration, templates, editing | Artistic images, upscaling, remixing |

## Where Canva AI Wins

- Built-in editor handles resizing, text, animations without exports[5].
- Thousands of templates speed social media and marketing graphics.
- Team features allow real-time collaboration and brand kits.
- Free tier generates unlimited images with basic edits.
- Integrates video, docs, presentations in one app.

## Where Midjourney Wins

- V8 model delivers superior detail, coherence in complex scenes[2].
- Community prompts and styles enable precise artistic control.
- Upscaling to 8K and remixing refine outputs iteratively.
- Video generation (Pro plans) creates 60s clips from images.
- Discord bot supports batch jobs and private modes.

## Key Differences

Canva AI emphasizes ease with its drag-and-drop editor, template library, and multi-format outputs (images, videos, presentations), making it faster for non-designers to produce polished work[5]. Midjourney prioritizes image quality through text prompts alone, with V8 improving hands, text, and realism over prior versions, but requires Discord and lacks native editing[2]. Canva Pro costs less for broad access ($15/mo annual), while Midjourney scales pricing with GPU hours (200-30k images/mo).

## Who should choose Canva AI

Teams needing quick visuals, edits, and collaboration pick Canva AI for its all-in-one platform.

## Who should choose Midjourney

Artists and pros seeking maximum image detail use Midjourney for prompt-driven generation.

## Bottom Line

Choose Canva AI for workflow speed and versatility in teams. Select Midjourney for top image quality in creative projects. Many users combine both via exports.

## FAQ

**Can I use both?**  
Yes, export Midjourney images to Canva for editing and templates.

**Which is cheaper?**  
Canva Pro at $15/mo annual beats Midjourney Basic ($10/mo) for editing; Midjourney suits image-only needs.

**Which one should I pick first?**  
Start with Canva free tier for broad design; add Midjourney if images lack detail.
