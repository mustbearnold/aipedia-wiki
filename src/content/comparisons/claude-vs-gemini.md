# Claude vs Gemini

[Claude](/tools/claude/) is Anthropic's premium reasoning, writing, long-document, and Claude Code assistant. [Gemini](/tools/gemini/) is Google's AI assistant and model ecosystem across Gemini app, Search, Workspace, Android, Google One subscriptions, Gemini API, AI Studio, Nano Banana image generation, Veo video generation, Jules, Antigravity, and NotebookLM.

**Quick answer:** choose Claude when the buyer needs careful writing, critique, long-form reasoning, Claude Code, or a cleaner high-trust assistant. Choose Gemini when the buyer lives in Google, wants AI bundled with Search/Docs/Gmail/Drive/storage, or needs native image/video and Google developer-agent surfaces in the same ecosystem.

## Decision Table

| Need | Better choice | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Long-form writing and critique | Claude | Claude remains stronger for careful prose, pushback, and complex document reasoning. |
| Google Workspace and Android | Gemini | Gemini sits closer to Gmail, Docs, Drive, Search, Chrome, Android, NotebookLM, and Google One benefits. |
| Terminal-native coding agent | Claude | Claude Code is the tighter Anthropic-backed repo agent. |
| Google-native coding and agents | Gemini | Google AI Pro/Ultra expose Jules, Antigravity, and higher agent/developer limits by plan. |
| Native image generation | Gemini | Gemini API docs list Nano Banana, Nano Banana 2, and Nano Banana Pro surfaces. Claude has no native image generation. |
| Native video generation | Gemini | Veo 3.1 is a Google/Gemini API video-generation surface. Claude has no native video generation. |
| Direct enterprise assistant procurement | Depends | Claude is cleaner for a focused assistant; Gemini is stronger when Workspace/Google Cloud already govern the org. |

## Where Claude Wins

Claude wins on judgment-heavy work. It is the safer first pick for long memos, policy review, legal-style analysis, complex editing, architecture tradeoffs, and code review that needs explanation as much as implementation.

Claude's pricing is also easier to understand when the buyer wants a focused assistant. Anthropic lists Free, Pro at $20/month or $17/month annually, Max from $100/month, Team seats, and Enterprise at $20/seat plus API-rate usage. Opus 4.8 and Sonnet 4.6 list 1M context; Opus 4.8 API pricing is $5 input and $25 output per million tokens.

Claude also wins for developers who want Anthropic's coding lane. Claude Code is included in Pro and higher plans, while Claude's model lineup is strongly associated with careful code reasoning, repo investigation, and terminal-agent workflows.

## Where Gemini Wins

Gemini wins on ecosystem gravity. The official US subscription page lists Free, Google AI Plus at $7.99/month, Google AI Pro at $19.99/month, and Google AI Ultra starting at $99.99/month with a $199.99/month 20x-usage tier. Google AI Pro includes 5TB storage, Gemini in Gmail/Docs/Vids, NotebookLM, Google Flow credits, Gemini in Chrome early access, Google Home Premium Standard, YouTube Premium Lite, and higher access to Gemini 3 Pro/Deep Search/agentic capabilities.

Gemini also wins for native media. Google API docs list Gemini 3.5 Flash as stable, Gemini 3.1 Pro Preview for advanced multimodal/agentic/vibe-coding tasks, Nano Banana 2 and Nano Banana Pro for image generation/editing, Gemini 3.1 Flash Live and TTS, and Veo 3.1 for video generation. Claude can analyze images but does not generate images or video natively.

For developers already in Google's stack, Gemini has a wider platform story: AI Studio, Gemini API, Jules, Antigravity, Google Search grounding, Google Maps grounding, Workspace, Vertex AI, and Google Cloud governance. That is less elegant as a writing assistant than Claude, but it can be more useful if the work already lives in Google.

Do not pick only by sticker price. Claude Pro and Google AI Pro are similar monthly buys, but Gemini includes storage and Google app benefits while Claude buys a more focused reasoning assistant. API buyers should price exact model, prompt size, tier, grounding, cache, media, and region.

## Best Combined Workflow

Use Claude for final reasoning, writing, critique, and code review. Use Gemini for Google-native workflows: searching, working with Drive/Docs/Gmail, generating images/video, using NotebookLM, or building on Gemini API/AI Studio.

The best trial is not a benchmark prompt. Give both products one week of real documents, code, email, source material, and media tasks, then compare saved time, review burden, and where the work naturally lives.

## Watch-Outs

- **Gemini plan names and limits change quickly.** Check your region, account type, and plan page before buying.
- **Claude has no native image or video generation.** Pair it with a media tool if that is part of the workflow.
- **Gemini's product surface is wider but more complex.** Plus, Pro, Ultra, Search, Workspace, Android, AI Studio, Flow, and Vertex all have different limits.
- **Claude is less useful inside Google files.** If Docs, Drive, Gmail, and Search are the daily workspace, Gemini's placement matters.
- **Consumer and enterprise privacy differ.** Sensitive Google work belongs in managed Workspace/Cloud contexts, not casual consumer accounts.

## Who Should Choose Claude

Choose Claude for writers, analysts, researchers, consultants, lawyers, founders, and developers who need careful reasoning, critique, long-form editing, and Claude Code. It is the stronger focused assistant when quality of answer matters more than app integration.

Choose Claude if the buyer wants a simpler assistant procurement story without buying into Google's whole subscription and app bundle.

## Who Should Choose Gemini

Choose Gemini if the buyer already lives in Gmail, Docs, Drive, Sheets, Search, Android, Chrome, NotebookLM, or Google Cloud. Gemini is more valuable when the assistant is close to the actual files and apps.

Choose Gemini if the workflow needs native image generation, video generation, Google Search grounding, AI Studio, Jules, Antigravity, or Google One storage value in one account.

## Bottom Line

Claude is the better deep-work assistant. Gemini is the better Google-native multimodal bundle. Pick Claude for writing, critique, and Claude Code. Pick Gemini when Google ecosystem integration, media generation, storage, Search, and Workspace access are part of the purchase reason.

## FAQ

**Is Gemini cheaper than Claude?**
For US subscriptions, Claude Pro is $20/month and Google AI Pro is $19.99/month. Gemini also has Plus at $7.99 and Ultra at $99.99/$199.99. API pricing depends on exact model and usage tier.

**Which is better for coding?**
Claude is better for terminal-native Claude Code. Gemini is better if the buyer wants Google AI Studio, Gemini API, Jules, Antigravity, or Google-native agent/developer tooling.

**Which is better for image and video?**
Gemini. Nano Banana covers image generation/editing and Veo 3.1 covers video. Claude does not generate images or video natively.
