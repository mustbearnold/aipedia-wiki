# ChatGPT vs Runway

[ChatGPT](../tools/chatgpt.md) handles text, image, voice, browsing, and code tasks in one interface. [Runway](../tools/runway.md) focuses on video generation from text or images with tools for editing and effects. This comparison uses flagship versions and pricing as of April 2026.

## Quick Answer

ChatGPT suits general tasks like writing and analysis. Runway fits video creation workflows.


## Where ChatGPT Wins

- Processes text, images, voice, and code in one place.[2]
- Supports real-time web browsing and plugin ecosystem.[2]
- Free tier delivers value for basic use.[2]
- Pro plan at $200/mo includes advanced features for heavy users.[2]
- Handles document analysis and content generation broadly.[4]

## Where Runway Wins

- Generates videos from text or image prompts with high fidelity.[5]
- Includes editing tools for motion effects and style transfer.
- Outputs 1080p clips up to 10 seconds in base plans.
- Standard plan at $15/mo unlocks more credits than ChatGPT free tier.
- Focuses on creative video workflows without general task distractions.[5]

## Key Differences

ChatGPT's GPT-5.5 model processes 1M-token API context (ChatGPT tier windows not fully published) for long documents or conversations, while Runway's Gen-4.5 emphasizes video synthesis with GWM-1 for motion control.[1][2] ChatGPT pricing scales to $200/mo for Pro access; Runway starts at $15/mo for Standard with video credits.[2][5] ChatGPT covers text, code, and multimodal inputs broadly; Runway specializes in video output and lacks general reasoning.[4][5]

## Who should choose ChatGPT

Users needing one tool for writing, analysis, coding, and basic media. Teams handling mixed knowledge work benefit from its interface.[2][5]

## Who should choose Runway

Video creators requiring text-to-video or image-to-video generation. Filmmakers and marketers focused on short clips find it direct.[5]

## Bottom Line

Pick ChatGPT for everyday versatility across tasks. Choose Runway for dedicated video production. Many workflows use both for complementary strengths.[2][5]

## FAQ

**Can I use both?**  
Yes, ChatGPT handles planning and scripting; Runway generates videos from outputs.

**Which is cheaper?**  
Runway Standard at $15/mo costs less than ChatGPT Plus at $20/mo for specialized video use.[2]

**Which one should I pick first?**  
Start with ChatGPT for broad needs; add Runway if video is core to your work.[2][5]
