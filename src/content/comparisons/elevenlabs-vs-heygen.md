# ElevenLabs vs HeyGen

[ElevenLabs](../tools/elevenlabs.md) generates realistic voices, text-to-speech output, and voice agents. [HeyGen](../tools/heygen.md) creates videos with AI avatars, lip-sync, and voice integration from scripts or documents. This comparison covers their flagship offerings, pricing, and workflow fit as of April 2026[5].

## Quick Answer

ElevenLabs suits audio-only needs like voiceovers and agents; HeyGen fits full video production with avatars. Choice depends on whether voice or video is primary.


|---|---|---|
| **Flagship** | Eleven Multilingual v3 | Avatar v4 |
| **Price** | Free / Starter $5/mo / Creator $22/mo / Pro $99/mo | Free / Creator $29/mo / Business $89/mo / Enterprise custom |
| **Best For** | Voice generation, TTS, voice agents | Avatar videos, lip-sync, script-to-video |

## Where ElevenLabs Wins

- Generates voices for text-to-speech and agents with high realism[5].
- Supports multilingual output for broader language coverage.
- Lower entry pricing starts at $5 per month for paid plans.
- Focuses on audio production without video overhead.
- Integrates into apps for real-time voice features.

## Where HeyGen Wins

- Produces complete videos from scripts with AI avatars and lip-sync.
- Handles document-to-video workflows for business content.
- Offers templates for faster video creation.
- Includes voice cloning tied to avatar movements.
- Scales for team use with business plans at $89 per month.

## Key Differences

ElevenLabs centers on standalone audio: text-to-speech, voice cloning, and agents, with flagship Eleven Multilingual v3 delivering natural intonation across languages. HeyGen combines voice with video, using Avatar v4 for lip-synced talking heads from text or uploads; its strength lies in end-to-end video output rather than isolated audio. Pricing reflects scope: ElevenLabs tiers from $5 monthly for basics, up to $99 for pro audio limits; HeyGen starts free but charges $29 monthly for creator video credits, rising to $89 for business features. ElevenLabs fits lean audio pipelines; HeyGen supports visual storytelling.

## Who should choose ElevenLabs

Users needing voiceovers, podcasts, or app-integrated speech pick ElevenLabs for its audio focus and lower cost.

## Who should choose HeyGen

Teams producing explainer videos, marketing clips, or avatar demos select HeyGen for integrated video and voice delivery.

## Bottom Line

Both tools overlap in voice but diverge on output: ElevenLabs for pure audio efficiency, HeyGen for video completeness. Test free tiers to match your primary need; neither dominates universally.

## FAQ

**Can I use both?**  
Yes, pair ElevenLabs voices with HeyGen videos via export for hybrid workflows.

**Which is cheaper?**  
ElevenLabs has lower starter pricing at $5 monthly versus HeyGen's $29; compare credits per use case.

**Which one should I pick first?**  
Start with ElevenLabs for audio tests, HeyGen for video prototypes.
