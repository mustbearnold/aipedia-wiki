# ElevenLabs vs HeyGen

[ElevenLabs](../tools/elevenlabs.md) and [HeyGen](../tools/heygen.md) overlap around synthetic media, but they should not be bought for the same reason. ElevenLabs is the stronger voice, TTS, dubbing, audio, and voice-agent platform. HeyGen is the stronger avatar-led business video platform for presenters, digital twins, localization, sales, training, and video agents.

The current buyer answer on May 8, 2026: **choose ElevenLabs when the voice is the product. Choose HeyGen when the video needs a presenter on screen.**

## Quick Answer

ElevenLabs wins for audio quality, voice cloning, narration, dubbing, speech-to-text, sound effects, music, and conversational voice agents. Its model docs list Eleven v3, Multilingual v2, Flash v2.5, Scribe v2, and music models. ElevenLabs also now exposes Image to Video inside ElevenCreative, but its main buyer value is still voice and audio.

HeyGen wins when the output must be a finished avatar video. Its current pricing lists app plans for creators and businesses, 500+ stock photo avatars on Free, 700+ stock video avatars on Creator, 175+ languages and dialects, 4K on Pro/Business, SCORM/LMS support on Business, and API usage as a separate surface.

## Winner By Use Case

| Use case | Better pick | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Premium narration or voiceover | ElevenLabs | Eleven v3 and Multilingual v2 are built for expressive speech. |
| Real-time voice agent | ElevenLabs | Flash v2.5 and ElevenAgents are the more direct voice-agent stack. |
| Digital twin sales video | HeyGen | The presenter, face, avatar, and video export are central. |
| Localized talking-head clip | HeyGen | Lip sync, avatar video, scripts, and templates stay together. |
| Audio-only podcast or audiobook | ElevenLabs | HeyGen would add unnecessary video overhead. |
| Presenter video with externally refined voice | Use both | ElevenLabs can produce/refine the voice; HeyGen can package the avatar video. |

## Where ElevenLabs Wins

ElevenLabs wins when audio quality and flexibility matter more than a visual presenter. Its public pricing lists Free, Starter, Creator, Pro, Scale, Business, and Enterprise. Starter adds a commercial license and Instant Voice Cloning. Creator adds Professional Voice Cloning. Pro adds higher-quality API audio output. Business and Enterprise cover team and compliance-heavy needs.

The model docs make the fit clear. Eleven v3 is positioned for emotionally rich speech across 70+ languages. Flash v2.5 is the fast model for real-time use across 32 languages. Scribe v2 and Scribe v2 Realtime cover transcription. If the buyer is building a product, podcast, audiobook, voice agent, narration pipeline, or game/dialogue workflow, ElevenLabs is the more direct tool.

The new Image to Video surface does not change that main decision. It means ElevenLabs can participate in multimedia creation, but it still does not replace HeyGen's avatar-video workflow when the deliverable is a digital twin or structured talking-head business video.

## Where HeyGen Wins

HeyGen wins when the final asset is a video with a person-like presenter. Current HeyGen pricing lists Free, Creator, Pro, Business, and Enterprise. Creator includes avatar-video features such as Digital Twin, stock video avatars, voice cloning, 175+ languages and dialects, 1080p export, brand kit, watermark removal, Avatar IV, and video translation. Business adds longer videos, 4K, SAML/SSO, collaboration, SCORM export, LMS integrations, and workflow integrations.

That is the buying reason. HeyGen gives a marketing, sales, or training team a direct path from script to avatar-led video. ElevenLabs can generate a better standalone voice, but it will not automatically give you a polished presenter clip, digital-twin workflow, business-video template, review surface, or video localization path.

HeyGen's watch-out is pricing surface confusion. App plans, API pricing, LiveAvatar, and enterprise terms are not the same budget.

## Pricing And Procurement

ElevenLabs pricing is credit and character oriented. Voice generation, speech-to-text, agents, music, image/video, and API usage can consume credits differently. Buyers should calculate monthly minutes, characters, voice clones, agent traffic, and API concurrency before choosing a plan.

HeyGen pricing is video-workflow oriented. Buyers should calculate app plan needs, video length, avatar access, translation, digital twins, seats, API generation minutes, LiveAvatar needs, and enterprise controls. For API usage, HeyGen help lists Avatar IV API generation from $3-$5 per generated minute depending on avatar type and resolution.

The cheaper tool depends on the deliverable. Audio-only work usually belongs in ElevenLabs. Presenter-led video usually belongs in HeyGen.

## Who Should Choose ElevenLabs

Choose ElevenLabs for TTS, voice cloning, dubbing, narration, audiobooks, podcasts, game dialogue, speech-to-text, sound effects, music, voice agents, and API voice generation.

Skip ElevenLabs as the default if the end user expects a person on screen. You may still use it for the voice layer, but HeyGen should own the avatar-video workflow.

## Who Should Choose HeyGen

Choose HeyGen for avatar-led sales videos, digital twins, localized talking-head clips, training videos, onboarding, customer education, and script-to-video business workflows.

Skip HeyGen if the output is audio-only, embedded speech, an agent voice, or a high-volume narration pipeline.

## Bottom Line

**ElevenLabs is the voice pick. HeyGen is the avatar-video pick.** If the audience only hears the asset, start with ElevenLabs. If the audience watches a presenter, start with HeyGen. If quality voice plus avatar video both matter, test a combined workflow before buying volume.

## FAQ

**Is ElevenLabs better than HeyGen?**
For voice quality, narration, dubbing, and voice agents, yes. For avatar-led presenter videos, HeyGen is the better first test.

**Does ElevenLabs now make video?**
Yes, ElevenLabs exposes Image to Video in ElevenCreative, including model selection and voice integration. That does not make it the same kind of avatar-video platform as HeyGen.

**Can HeyGen replace ElevenLabs for voice?**
Not for voice-first production. HeyGen includes voice and cloning features, but ElevenLabs is stronger when voice quality and API audio are the product.

**Can I use ElevenLabs voices in HeyGen videos?**
Yes, but test sync, licensing, timing, and export workflow before relying on it for production.

**Which one should a sales team choose?**
HeyGen if the output is a personalized presenter video. ElevenLabs if the output is voice-only calling, narration, or an embedded agent voice.
