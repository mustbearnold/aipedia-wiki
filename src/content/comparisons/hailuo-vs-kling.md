# Hailuo AI vs Kling 3.0

[Hailuo AI](../tools/hailuo.md) and [Kling 3.0](../tools/kling.md) generate videos from text or image prompts. This comparison covers flagship versions, pricing, and workflow fit as of April 2026.

## Quick Answer

Kling 3.0 leads in video length and motion quality for complex scenes; Hailuo AI matches it on price with faster generation for shorter clips.

## At a Glance

| | Hailuo AI | Kling 3.0 |
|---|---|---|
| **Flagship** | Hailuo 2.3 | Kling 3.0 |
| **Price** | Free tier (10s clips); Standard $9.99/mo (120s max) | Free tier (5s clips); Pro $19/mo (2min max) |
| **Output Specs** | 1080p, 10-120s, text/image to video | 1080p, 5s-2min, text/image/video to video |
| **Best For** | Quick social media clips | Longer narrative videos |

## Where Hailuo AI Wins

- Generates 10-second clips in under 30 seconds on free tier, faster than Kling's free plan.[5]
- Lower entry price at $9.99 per month for 120-second videos versus Kling Pro.[4]
- Consistent character consistency in multi-shot scenes due to improved temporal modeling in 2.3.[1]
- Simpler prompt adherence for casual users creating short promotional content.[7]

## Where Kling 3.0 Wins

- Supports up to 2-minute videos, double Hailuo's standard limit, for full scene narratives.[2]
- Superior physics simulation and lip-sync in dialogue-heavy clips.[5]
- Extends inputs with video-to-video mode for iterative editing workflows.[4]
- Higher benchmark scores on VBench for motion dynamics (86.4% vs Hailuo 2.3's 82.1%).[1]

## Key Differences

Hailuo AI 2.3 focuses on speed and affordability, delivering 1080p clips up to 120 seconds with strong prompt fidelity for static or simple motion scenes; its free tier suits testing ideas quickly. Kling 3.0 emphasizes length and realism, handling 2-minute outputs with advanced camera controls and multi-modal inputs (text, image, video), though at higher Pro pricing and slower free generation.[1][2][5] Hailuo edges out on cost per second generated; Kling leads in professional-grade complexity like crowd simulations or extended actions.

## Who should choose Hailuo AI

Social media creators and marketers needing fast, low-cost short videos (under 2 minutes) benefit from Hailuo's free tier and quick turnaround.

## Who should choose Kling 3.0

Filmmakers and advertisers producing longer, dynamic videos with precise motion or extensions from existing footage find Kling's capabilities match complex needs.

## Bottom Line

Choose Hailuo AI for budget-conscious short-form content; select Kling 3.0 for extended, high-fidelity videos. Both offer free tiers to test fit before committing.

## FAQ

**Can I use both?**  
Yes, start with free tiers; many alternate based on clip length or motion needs.[4]

**Which is cheaper?**  
Hailuo AI at $9.99 monthly for standard access versus Kling's $19 Pro plan.[2][5]

**Which one should I pick first?**  
Hailuo for quick tests due to faster free generation; Kling if targeting over 1-minute videos.[1][7]
