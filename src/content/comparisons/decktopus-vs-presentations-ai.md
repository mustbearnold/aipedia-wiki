# Decktopus AI vs Presentations.AI

[Decktopus AI](/tools/decktopus/) and [Presentations.AI](/tools/presentations-ai/) both create decks from prompts, but the buyer decision changed materially in June 2026. Presentations.AI is no longer best described as a cheap Public Beta annual plan. Its current pricing page shows Starter at $0 with 100 credits, Pro at $20/month billed annually with 5,000 credits, and Gold at $100/month billed annually with 50,000 credits. Decktopus remains the clearer interactive presenter workflow.

The practical rule: **choose Decktopus when the presentation needs forms, analytics, voice, webhooks, custom domains, or sales/training delivery. Choose Presentations.AI when you want a business-focused prompt/document-to-PowerPoint workflow with more brand, credit, knowledge-base, and enterprise story depth.** This comparison was re-verified on **June 5, 2026** against official Decktopus and Presentations.AI sources.

## Quick Verdict

**Decktopus AI wins interactive delivery.** Its current pricing page lists Pro at $14.99/month billed annually with 9,000 AI credits per year, about 300 AI presentations per year, AI assistance, AI presentation generation, AI image generation, and PDF export. Business is $34.99/user/month billed annually with 12,000 AI credits per year, about 400 AI presentations per year for the organization, custom domain connection, slide analytics, webhook, and team/organization features. The FAQ says AI deck creation costs 30 credits and highlights forms, voice recording, analytics, embeds, mobile-responsive documents, and collaboration.

**Presentations.AI wins when the buyer wants a more business-presentations-first generator.** Its current pricing page lists Starter at $0 with 100 credits, a starter AI model/agent, and up to 20 slides. Pro is $20/month billed annually with 5,000 credits, high-quality PowerPoint export, presentation tracking and analytics, and brand customization. Gold is $100/month billed annually with 50,000 credits, frontier AI models and agents, advanced brand customization, and a shared knowledge base. The feature table also lists annual-credit columns of 1,500, 5,000, and 50,000 credits across the paid comparison area, plus PDF/PowerPoint export, embeds, unbranded public links, password-protected links on Pro/Gold, guests, live presenting, brand colors, brand fonts, and analytics.

There is no honest single winner. Decktopus is better when the deck is an interactive business link. Presentations.AI is better when the deck is a business presentation output that needs richer PowerPoint, brand, knowledge-base, and enterprise positioning.

## Winner By Use Case

- **Interactive sales proposal or training asset:** Decktopus AI.
- **Forms, voice recording, analytics, embeds, webhooks, and custom domains:** Decktopus Business.
- **Clear credit math for AI deck generation:** Decktopus.
- **Prompt/document/URL-to-business presentation workflow:** Presentations.AI.
- **High-quality PowerPoint export and brand customization:** Presentations.AI Pro.
- **Frontier model access, shared knowledge base, and advanced branding:** Presentations.AI Gold.
- **Enterprise-scale API, white-label, or scheduled generation story:** Presentations.AI deserves a sales-led evaluation; Decktopus Enterprise deserves one when branded slide templates are the core need.
- **Lowest-risk trial:** Presentations.AI Starter for a basic free test; Decktopus Pro when interactive delivery is already the requirement.

## Where Decktopus AI Wins

Decktopus wins because it treats a presentation as a live delivery object. The official pricing and FAQ pages surface features that matter after the deck is generated: forms for collecting data, voice recording for virtual presentation delivery, analytics for opens and page views, embeds, website embedding, custom domains, collaboration, webhooks, and cloud access.

That is the right fit for consultants, trainers, educators, founders, and sales teams whose problem is not only "make slides." The real problem is "send this to an audience, capture signal, and follow up." Decktopus has the more direct workflow for that job.

Decktopus also has simpler self-serve plan math. Pro and Business list annual AI credits, approximate annual AI presentation counts, and a 30-credit deck-generation cost. Presentations.AI has a higher ceiling, but buyers need to understand which pricing table and workspace path they are entering before comparing usage.

## Where Presentations.AI Wins

Presentations.AI wins when the buyer wants a business presentation factory rather than an interactive presenter link. Its official site says it turns ideas, documents, data, PDFs, URLs, and existing presentations into professional decks, applies brand, exports editable PowerPoint, supports presentation refresh, and supports scale through API/webhook and white-label paths. It also claims SOC 2 Type II certification and GDPR compliance on the official product page.

The current pricing model is no longer the old low-cost Public Beta story. The current public page shows Starter, Pro, and Gold. Pro is the practical self-serve plan for serious buyers because it unlocks high-quality PowerPoint export, tracking/analytics, brand customization, and 5,000 credits. Gold is for professional workflows where quality and consistency matter enough to justify frontier models, 50,000 credits, advanced branding, and a shared knowledge base.

Presentations.AI is weaker when the deck has to collect responses, embed a form, route viewer behavior, or function as a lightweight microsite/funnel. That is where Decktopus is clearer.

## Pricing And Plan Guidance

Use **Decktopus Pro** if you are a solo creator, founder, educator, consultant, or seller making regular AI-assisted presentations and want predictable credit math. Move to **Decktopus Business** if custom domains, slide analytics, webhooks, team features, and organization controls matter. Use **Decktopus Enterprise** when corporate-branded slides, branded AI setup, custom layouts, and custom templates are central.

Use **Presentations.AI Starter** to test whether the output style fits. Move to **Presentations.AI Pro** when high-quality PowerPoint export, presentation tracking, analytics, brand customization, and 5,000 credits justify $20/month billed annually. Move to **Presentations.AI Gold** when the shared knowledge base, frontier model access, 50,000 credits, advanced branding, and unlimited-guest path matter enough to justify $100/month billed annually.

Do not rely on the old Presentations.AI Public Beta or $198/year Pro claims. AiPedia found those claims stale during the June 5, 2026 refresh and replaced them with the current official pricing-page ladder.

## What To Avoid

- **Do not buy Presentations.AI because an older list says it is a $40/year Public Beta deal.** The current official page now shows Starter, Pro, and Gold self-serve pricing.
- **Do not buy Decktopus if all you need is PowerPoint-ready business deck generation.** Presentations.AI has the stronger current story for editable PPTX, business inputs, brand customization, and knowledge-base workflows.
- **Do not buy Presentations.AI for analytics-heavy sales delivery without testing.** It has tracking and analytics, but Decktopus is clearer for forms, voice, embeds, custom domains, webhooks, and presenter-funnel workflows.
- **Do not ignore credits.** Decktopus uses annual credits and a 30-credit deck cost. Presentations.AI Starter, Pro, and Gold expose 100, 5,000, and 50,000 credit paths, and the feature table also lists paid annual credit buckets.
- **Do not publish AI-generated decks without review.** Presentations.AI itself warns that important facts, numbers, and claims should be reviewed before external sharing; that warning applies to both tools.

## Bottom Line

Decktopus AI is the better choice when a deck has to support selling, teaching, presenting, collecting responses, tracking engagement, or running as a branded interactive link. Presentations.AI is the better choice when the buyer wants a business-focused AI presentation platform with PowerPoint export, brand customization, higher credit ceilings, and knowledge-base or enterprise generation paths.

For interactive presenter workflows, start with Decktopus. For business deck production from prompts, documents, URLs, and existing presentations, start with Presentations.AI Pro and compare it against Gamma before standardizing.

## FAQ

**Is Decktopus better than Presentations.AI?**

Decktopus is better for interactive presentation delivery, forms, voice recording, custom domains, slide analytics, webhooks, and presenter workflows. Presentations.AI is better for business-ready prompt/document-to-deck generation, high-quality PowerPoint export, brand customization, knowledge-base workflows, and higher credit ceilings.

**Which is cheaper?**

Presentations.AI has a free Starter path with 100 credits. Its Pro plan is $20/month billed annually, and Gold is $100/month billed annually. Decktopus Pro is $14.99/month billed annually, while Business is $34.99/user/month billed annually. Compare credits, output quality, and workflow requirements before choosing.

**Which should a sales team buy?**

Choose Decktopus if the sales workflow needs interactive links, forms, custom domains, analytics, voice, embeds, or follow-up signals. Choose Presentations.AI if the team needs to generate polished, brand-aligned PowerPoint-ready sales decks from source material, refresh decks, or explore API/white-label scale.

**Can I use both?**

Yes, but define the owner of the final deck. Presentations.AI can generate a business-ready PPTX draft; Decktopus can host an interactive sales or training presentation. Maintaining the same final deck in two systems adds version risk.
