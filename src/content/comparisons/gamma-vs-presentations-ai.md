# Gamma vs Presentations.AI

[Gamma](/tools/gamma/) and [Presentations.AI](/tools/presentations-ai/) both create AI presentations, but they are moving in different directions. Gamma is a flexible creator workspace for presentations, docs, websites, social posts, images, graphics, publishing, analytics, custom domains, and API workflows. Presentations.AI is a business presentation platform for turning ideas, documents, data, URLs, and templates into PowerPoint-ready decks with analytics, brand controls, credits, project knowledge, and enterprise-scale refresh/generation paths.

## Quick Answer

Choose Gamma for most prompt-to-deck and multi-format content workflows. Choose Presentations.AI when PowerPoint-ready business decks, brand sync, analytics, project knowledge, refresh workflows, or higher-credit self-serve plans matter more than broad creator output.

## Decision Snapshot

- **Best default AI deck tool:** Gamma, because it is faster and broader for decks, docs, websites, social, and shareable content.
- **Best PowerPoint-ready business deck lane:** Presentations.AI, especially for buyers who care about native PPTX export and brand-ready business presentations.
- **Best flexible publishing:** Gamma Pro or Ultra, with analytics, advanced sharing, domains, API access, and workspace templates.
- **Best high-credit self-serve plan:** Presentations.AI Gold, if 50,000 annual credits, frontier models, advanced branding, project knowledge, and shared knowledge base justify $100/month billed annually.
- **Main watch-out:** Presentations.AI's old Public Beta and $198/year Pro claims are stale; use the current Starter, Pro, and Gold ladder unless checkout shows otherwise. Gamma dollar amounts can vary by billing toggle, account state, or region, so verify live pricing before purchase.

## Recent Changes

- **June 26, 2026:** Rechecked Gamma pricing and API help. Free, Plus, Pro, and Ultra plan gates remain visible; Pro is the first self-serve API tier, and Ultra adds 75 cards per prompt, advanced models, and up to 100 custom domains.
- **June 26, 2026:** Rechecked Presentations.AI pricing and enterprise pages. Starter remains $0 with 100 credits, Pro remains $20/month billed annually with 5,000 credits, and Gold remains $100/month billed annually with 50,000 credits, frontier models, advanced branding, and shared knowledge base.

## Where Gamma Wins

- **Broader content surface.** Gamma supports presentations, documents, websites, social posts, images, graphics, and API use cases.
- **Fast deck creation.** Its AI presentation page positions the product around creating a working presentation quickly from prompts, outlines, or source material.
- **Easy import/export.** Free includes PDF/PPTX import and export to PDF, PPTX, PNG, and Google Slides.
- **Creator-friendly upgrades.** Plus removes Gamma branding; Pro adds custom branding, analytics, advanced sharing, custom domains, API access, and workspace templates; Ultra adds heavier usage, 75 cards per prompt, up to 100 custom domains, and advanced model access.
- **Best solo workflow.** It is easier to recommend when one person owns the narrative and wants a useful deck quickly.

## Where Presentations.AI Wins

- **Business deck framing.** The current homepage positions it as a platform for turning ideas, documents, data, and URLs into presentation-ready decks.
- **PowerPoint export emphasis.** The product page stresses editable PowerPoint output, and pricing includes PowerPoint/PDF export across self-serve plans.
- **Brand and analytics controls.** Pro adds high-quality PowerPoint export, presentation tracking, analytics, and brand customization; Gold adds frontier models, advanced brand customization, shared knowledge base, and project knowledge.
- **Refresh and scale story.** The homepage describes data-driven updates, audience customization, REST API/webhooks, white-label, and generated decks at scale.
- **Enterprise seriousness.** Compliance, SSO, data controls, and enterprise-grade positioning are clearer than a simple slide generator.

## Pricing and Plan Guidance

For Gamma, test Free first. Upgrade to Plus for branding removal, Pro for business publishing/API/domain needs, and Ultra for heavier usage and advanced models.

For Presentations.AI, Starter is a free test with 100 credits and up to 20 slides. Pro is $20/month billed annually with 5,000 credits, PowerPoint export, analytics, and brand customization. Gold is $100/month billed annually with 50,000 credits, frontier models, advanced branding, project knowledge, and shared knowledge base. Regional pricing can vary, so verify the live checkout before quoting.

## Bottom Line

Gamma is still the better default for most AI presentation buyers because it can become a deck, doc, website, social asset, or shareable page. Presentations.AI is the better shortlist when the buyer specifically wants business PowerPoint output, analytics, brand controls, and higher-credit presentation generation.

## FAQ

**Is Gamma better than Presentations.AI?**
For most solo and creator workflows, yes. For business PowerPoint-ready generation and high-credit plans, Presentations.AI is more competitive.

**Which is cheaper?**
Gamma has a Free tier and paid upgrades whose visible dollar amounts can vary by account/billing state. Presentations.AI currently lists Starter free, Pro at $20/month annual, and Gold at $100/month annual for US pricing.

**Which should a sales or customer-success team choose?**
Test Presentations.AI if dynamic business decks, brand controls, analytics, and refresh workflows matter. Test Gamma if the team mainly needs fast narrative drafts and flexible output formats.
