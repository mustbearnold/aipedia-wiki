# Instantly vs Zapier

[Instantly](/tools/instantly/) is a cold-email outreach platform. [Zapier](/tools/zapier/) is a no-code automation platform across 9,000+ app integrations. They work well together, but they should not be asked to do the same job.

## Quick Answer

Choose **Instantly** when the workflow is outbound sales: prospect lists, sending accounts, warmup, sequences, replies, deliverability, and meeting handoff.

Choose **Zapier** when the workflow spans many SaaS apps: CRMs, forms, spreadsheets, Slack, email, databases, Tables, Forms, Agents, MCP, and AI-assisted routing.

Use both when Instantly owns campaign execution and Zapier moves campaign events into the rest of the business.

## Buyer Snapshot

- **Primary job:** Instantly runs cold outreach. Zapier connects apps and automates follow-on work.
- **Pricing anchor:** Instantly Outreach starts at $47/mo monthly. Zapier has a free tier, Professional from $19.99/mo annually, Team from $69/mo annually, and Enterprise custom.
- **Best owner:** Instantly belongs to outbound sales. Zapier belongs to non-technical operators and teams that need broad app coverage.
- **Main risk:** Instantly risk is deliverability and compliance. Zapier risk is task spend, workflow sprawl, and brittle app connections.

## Where Instantly Wins

Instantly wins when the team needs a purpose-built outbound system. It handles sending-account workflows, warmup, uploaded contacts, email volume, reply handling, and deliverability signals in a way a generic automation platform does not.

If your success metric is replies, meetings, and safe domain reputation, Instantly is the better center of gravity.

## Where Zapier Wins

Zapier wins when the workflow is broad and business-facing. It connects 9,000+ apps and now frames its AI platform around Zaps, Tables, Forms, Canvas, Agents, Chatbots, MCP, SDK, and AI orchestration.

If your success metric is fewer manual app-to-app tasks, Zapier is the better first stop.

## Which Should You Buy?

Buy **Instantly** when outbound email is already a defined channel and someone owns list quality, compliance, DNS, and sender reputation.

Buy **Zapier Professional** when you need multi-step Zaps, premium apps, and AI-assisted automations. Move to **Team** when shared governance, SAML SSO, and collaboration matter.

## Bottom Line

Instantly is the sales outreach engine. Zapier is the automation layer around the rest of the stack. Pick Instantly for sending health. Pick Zapier for app coverage and downstream automation.

## FAQ

**Can Zapier send cold email instead of Instantly?**
It can connect email tools, but it should not replace a deliverability-focused cold-email platform for serious outbound.

**Can Instantly automate the rest of my company?**
No. Use Zapier, Make, n8n, or another workflow platform for broad operations automation.

**Can I use both?**
Yes. A common setup is Instantly for campaigns, then Zapier for CRM updates, Slack alerts, lead routing, reporting, and follow-up workflows.
