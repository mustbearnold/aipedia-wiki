# HeyGen vs Synthesia

[HeyGen](../tools/heygen.md) and [Synthesia](../tools/synthesia.md) are leading AI avatar video platforms in 2026; both generate talking-head videos from scripts without cameras, actors, or studios. HeyGen focuses on custom avatar creation and multilingual dubbing. Synthesia targets enterprise users with stock avatars, compliance, and LMS integrations. HeyGen suits most users; Synthesia fits regulated organizations.

## Quick Answer

Choose [HeyGen](../tools/heygen.md) for photorealistic custom avatars, pricing value, and output quality in marketing or demos. Choose [Synthesia](../tools/synthesia.md) for SOC2 compliance, stock avatar libraries, or LMS integrations in enterprise L&D.

## Where HeyGen Wins

- Custom avatars on entry plan produce realistic lip-sync and expressions.
- AI dubbing supports 40+ languages for uploaded videos.
- Cleaner API and priority rendering for business users.
- Stronger output for product demos and marketing content.
- Better value for solo creators needing personal avatars.

## Where Synthesia Wins

- 230+ stock avatars available immediately.
- SOC2 Type II compliance for regulated enterprises.
- SCORM/LMS export for training modules.
- 120+ languages for high-volume localization.
- Team collaboration and extensive templates.

## Key Differences

HeyGen prioritizes custom avatar realism, with Instant and Studio options that match source likeness closely; this appeals to creators and marketers producing spokesperson videos. Synthesia emphasizes enterprise features like compliance certifications, large stock libraries, and integrations; it handles corporate training across large teams better. HeyGen's Creator plan includes custom avatars at $24/mo, while Synthesia reserves them for $67/mo; dubbing depth favors Synthesia at scale, but HeyGen leads on per-video quality.

## Who should choose HeyGen

Solo creators, agencies, and marketing teams benefit from realistic custom avatars and dubbing without enterprise costs. Businesses scaling localized spokesperson videos find HeyGen's API and rendering efficient.

## Who should choose Synthesia

L&D and HR teams in enterprises need SOC2, SCORM, and stock avatars for compliance training. Organizations with 100+ language needs or LMS workflows match Synthesia's infrastructure.

## Bottom Line

HeyGen wins for most users due to custom avatar access at entry pricing and superior realism in demos or marketing. Synthesia serves enterprises requiring compliance or integrations, but HeyGen delivers more features for general video needs. Check plans for current credits and limits.

## FAQ

**Can I create a custom avatar on the cheapest plan?**  
HeyGen's $24/mo Creator includes one instant avatar. Synthesia's $22/mo Starter does not; custom starts at $67/mo Creator.

**Which has more realistic custom avatars?**  
HeyGen ranks higher on realism for facial expressions and lip-sync in custom avatars, per 2026 benchmarks.

**Does either integrate with LMS systems?**  
Synthesia offers SCORM export and LMS connectors. HeyGen does not.
