# Descript vs Resemble AI

[Descript](../tools/descript.md) and [Resemble AI](../tools/resemble-ai.md) are two options in the AI voice category as of April 2026. This comparison covers flagship versions, pricing, and workflow fit based on current data.

## Quick Answer

Descript suits video and podcast editors needing text-based editing with voice generation; Resemble AI fits developers requiring custom voice cloning and API integration for apps.

## At a Glance

| | Descript | Resemble AI |
|---|---|---|
| **Flagship** | Overdub 4.0 | Resemble 3.5 |
| **Price** | Free / Creator $15/user/mo / Pro $30/user/mo | Free / Starter $0.006/sec / Pro $0.030/sec |
| **Best For** | Podcast/video editing | Voice cloning/API use |

## Where Descript Wins

- Text-based audio editing lets users edit transcripts to change voiceovers automatically.
- Built-in tools for screen recording, captions, and filler word removal streamline podcast production.
- Per-user pricing scales for teams without per-second costs.
- Studio Sound feature enhances audio quality in post-production.
- Collaboration features support shared project workflows.

## Where Resemble AI Wins

- API-first design integrates into apps for real-time voice synthesis.
- Multilingual voice cloning supports 149 languages with emotion controls.
- Per-second pricing benefits low-volume or testing use.
- Custom voice creation from short samples enables branded voices.
- Enterprise options include SOC 2 compliance for secure deployments.

## Key Differences

Descript focuses on an all-in-one editor for content creators, where voice generation (Overdub 4.0) integrates with transcription and video tools; users edit text to update audio without re-recording. Resemble AI emphasizes API-driven voice synthesis (Resemble 3.5), with strengths in cloning unique voices for applications like games or virtual agents; pricing scales by usage seconds rather than subscriptions. Descript's interface suits non-technical users; Resemble AI requires development setup but offers more granular control over voice parameters like pitch and speed.

## Who should choose Descript

Podcasters and video editors who edit audio via text and need bundled tools like captions.

## Who should choose Resemble AI

Developers building voice features into apps or sites, needing custom clones and API access.

## Bottom Line

Choose Descript for editing workflows in podcasts or videos. Pick Resemble AI for programmatic voice needs. Many users combine both: Descript for production, Resemble for custom voices.

## FAQ

**Which is cheaper?**  
Resemble AI for low usage (free tier, $0.006/sec starter); Descript for heavy monthly editing ($15/mo Creator).

**Which has better output quality?**  
Resemble AI leads in cloning realism and multilingual support; Descript matches for natural podcast voices.

**Can I use both?**  
Yes; export Descript audio to Resemble for cloning, or use Resemble voices in Descript projects.
