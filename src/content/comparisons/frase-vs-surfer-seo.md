# Frase.io vs Surfer SEO

[Frase.io](/tools/frase/) and [Surfer SEO](/tools/surfer-seo/) are the closest match in this Frase cluster because both are bought by content teams. The distinction is workflow breadth versus scoring depth. Frase is the broader AI SEO/GEO workflow with AI Agent, briefs, AI visibility, audits, publishing, API, and MCP access. Surfer SEO is the stronger page-level content optimizer with Content Editor, AI Tracker, tracked pages, topical maps, content audits, and deeper SERP scoring.

## Quick Answer

Choose Frase if the buyer wants research, brief creation, AI-assisted article production, GEO optimization, visibility tracking, audits, and publishing in one workflow. Choose Surfer SEO if the buyer already has drafts or writers and wants stronger on-page scoring, AI-search tracking, tracked pages, and refresh workflow.

## Decision Snapshot

- **Best workflow breadth:** Frase. It covers more of the content operating loop.
- **Best optimization depth:** Surfer SEO. Its editor and SERP analysis are stronger for turning a draft into a competitive page.
- **Best AI visibility angle:** Depends. Frase connects AI visibility to content creation; Surfer connects AI Tracker to content refresh and tracked pages.
- **Pricing posture:** Frase plans scale by articles, visibility prompts, audit pages, seats, and domains. Surfer plans scale by documents, tracked pages, AI prompts, seats, workspaces, API access, and enterprise support.
- **Best for agencies:** Surfer when the agency sells optimization and refresh; Frase when it sells research-to-publish workflows.
- **Main watch-out:** Both tools can encourage same-looking content if teams chase scores without original expertise.

## Where Frase Wins

- **Brief-to-publish path.** Frase is better when the team wants to move from target keyword to brief, draft, optimization, and publishing without changing tools.
- **GEO workflow language.** Frase explicitly packages SEO and GEO content optimization, AI visibility tracking, and AI Agent workflow together.
- **API and MCP access.** Technical teams can wire Frase into broader content operations.
- **Site audits and AI crawler monitoring.** Frase includes auditing and crawler-monitoring capabilities by tier, which matters for AI-search readiness.
- **Lower friction for content managers.** It is more approachable when the buyer needs structure before writers start drafting.

## Where Surfer SEO Wins

- **Content Editor depth.** Surfer is stronger for SERP-grounded scoring, NLP terms, headings, structure, content audits, and refresh work.
- **AI Tracker.** Current Surfer pricing ties AI prompt tracking to the plan ladder, from weekly prompts on Standard through daily prompts on higher tiers.
- **Tracked pages.** Discovery, Standard, Pro, and Peace of Mind scale tracked pages from small tests to larger content estates.
- **Topical and refresh workflow.** Surfer is better when the buyer already has published pages and wants repeatable optimization cycles.
- **Enterprise ceiling.** Peace of Mind and Enterprise are clearer fits for high-output optimization, API, and advisory support.

## Pricing and Plan Guidance

For Frase, model the number of AI-optimized articles, visibility prompts, domains, audit pages, seats, and brand voice needs. Starter is for evaluation and small teams; Professional and Scale are the practical team tiers.

For Surfer, model document volume, tracked pages, AI prompt tracking, seats, workspaces, and whether API/advisory support matters. Standard is the practical floor for serious content teams; Pro and Peace of Mind are for daily AI visibility and multi-brand workflows.

## Bottom Line

Frase is the better workflow platform. Surfer SEO is the better optimization specialist. Pick Frase before the draft exists; pick Surfer when the draft or page needs to compete harder in Google and AI-search surfaces.

## FAQ

**Which is cheaper?**
It depends on billing period and volume. Frase may be cheaper when it replaces several workflow tools; Surfer can be better value when content optimization depth is the only job.

**Which has better content scoring?**
Surfer SEO. Frase is useful, but Surfer's editor and SERP analysis remain the stronger page-level optimization workflow.

**Can I use both?**
Yes. Use Frase for research and brief creation, then Surfer for final optimization, refresh audits, and AI Tracker monitoring.
