# Frase.io vs Surfer SEO

[Frase.io](../tools/frase.md) and [Surfer SEO](../tools/surfer-seo.md) lead AI SEO tools as of April 2026, using models like Claude Opus 4.6 and Gemini 3.1 Pro for content optimization and SERP analysis.[5] This comparison details their flagship versions, pricing, and workflow fits based on current data.

## Quick Answer

Surfer SEO suits on-page optimization and quick audits; Frase.io fits full content workflows with research and outlining. Choice depends on whether you prioritize speed or end-to-end production.

## At a Glance

| | Frase.io | Surfer SEO |
|---|---|---|
| **Flagship** | Claude Opus 4.6 integration | Gemini 3.1 Pro + Surfer Content Editor |
| **Price** | $45/mo (Solo), $115/mo (Team), $225/mo (Enterprise) | $99/mo (Essential), $219/mo (Scale), custom Enterprise |
| **Context Window** | 1M tokens via Claude | 2M tokens via Gemini |
| **Best For** | Content briefs, outlines, full articles | On-page audits, SERP analysis, real-time editing |

## Where Frase.io Wins

- Handles end-to-end workflows from research to publishing with AI outlines and briefs.[5]
- Integrates Claude Opus 4.6 for long-form analysis up to 1M tokens.[1]
- Team plans support collaboration on content calendars and revisions.
- Lower entry price at $45/mo for solo users.
- Built-in plagiarism checker and publishing tools streamline production.

## Where Surfer SEO Wins

- Real-time content editor scores pages against top SERPs during writing.[5]
- Uses Gemini 3.1 Pro's 2M token window for large dataset analysis.[3]
- Grow Flow feature automates audits and recommendations across sites.
- Stronger for technical audits like schema and entity optimization.
- Higher benchmark performance in semantic modeling for rankings.[5]

## Key Differences

Frase.io focuses on content creation pipelines, generating briefs, outlines, and full drafts powered by Claude models with reliable long-form output.[1] Surfer SEO emphasizes optimization precision, with its editor providing live NLP scores and SERP comparisons using Gemini's multimodal strengths for data-heavy tasks.[3] Frase starts cheaper for individuals, while Surfer's higher tiers include site-wide automation absent in Frase's plans.

## Who should choose Frase.io

Content teams or solo creators needing research-to-publish tools. Its Claude integration excels in structured workflows for blogs and long-form guides.

## Who should choose Surfer SEO

Agencies or site managers focused on audits and on-page tweaks. Gemini-powered analysis handles complex SERPs and scales to multiple sites.

## Bottom Line

Both tools use 2026 frontier models like Claude Opus 4.6 and Gemini 3.1 Pro for SEO tasks.[1][3] Pick Frase for content production; Surfer for optimization speed. Many users combine them for full coverage.
