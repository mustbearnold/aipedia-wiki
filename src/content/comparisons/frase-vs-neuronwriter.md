# Frase.io vs NeuronWriter

[Frase.io](/tools/frase/) and [NeuronWriter](/tools/neuronwriter/) both sit in the SEO content workflow, but the buying logic is not close. Frase is the broader SEO/GEO platform with AI Agent, optimization, AI visibility tracking, audits, publishing, API, and MCP access. NeuronWriter is the budget-focused NLP content optimizer for writers who mainly need SERP analysis, semantic term guidance, and draft scoring.

## Quick Answer

Choose Frase if the buyer wants a single workflow for briefs, AI-assisted content production, GEO optimization, AI-search visibility tracking, audits, and integrations. Choose NeuronWriter if the buyer has a tighter budget and mainly needs an affordable content editor that scores drafts against SERP competitors.

## Decision Snapshot

- **Best broad workflow:** Frase. It is stronger when the work spans research, briefs, drafts, optimization, AI visibility, audits, and publishing.
- **Best budget optimizer:** NeuronWriter. It is stronger when the work is "make this article match the SERP better" at low monthly cost.
- **Pricing posture:** Frase starts higher but includes broader capabilities. NeuronWriter has a five-tier Bronze-to-Diamond ladder with lower annual prices.
- **Best for agencies:** Depends. Frase is better for workflow consolidation; NeuronWriter is better for low-cost high-volume scoring.
- **Best for GEO/AI visibility:** Frase. NeuronWriter is mostly page optimization, not AI-answer visibility monitoring.
- **Main watch-out:** Frase is too much if you only need scoring; NeuronWriter is too narrow if the team needs audits, visibility tracking, or a broader SEO operating system.

## Where Frase Wins

- **AI visibility tracking.** Frase tracks brand and competitor presence across AI search platforms, with visibility score, share of voice, sentiment, and referral signals.
- **Workflow breadth.** AI Agent, SEO/GEO optimization, site audits, publishing, API, and MCP access are part of the platform story.
- **Brief and draft workflow.** It is better for teams that want to go from target keyword to brief to optimized document without stitching together many tools.
- **Team features.** Seats, domains, brand voice profiles, visibility history, and audit pages scale by tier.
- **Strategic fit.** It belongs in stacks that treat AI-search citations and search visibility as part of the same content workflow.

## Where NeuronWriter Wins

- **Lower cost.** Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, and Diamond pricing makes NeuronWriter one of the lowest serious content-optimization ladders in the category.
- **Focused content scoring.** SERP analysis, NLP recommendations, Content Designer, and editor scoring are the core job.
- **Simple buyer promise.** It does not pretend to replace keyword suites, backlink tools, or AI visibility monitors.
- **Small-site fit.** Freelancers, affiliate operators, and small teams can get meaningful optimization guidance without a $99+ content-ops platform.
- **Affiliate economics disclosed.** AiPedia has an approved NeuronWriter affiliate link, but the tool still has to win on value and fit.

## Pricing and Plan Guidance

Buy Frase when the extra cost replaces multiple workflows: content briefs, AI visibility prompts, audit pages, API/MCP access, and team collaboration. The plan decision should be based on monthly AI-optimized article volume, domains, seats, and visibility prompts.

Buy NeuronWriter when the buyer only needs article scoring and can live with project/query limits. Silver is the practical floor for regular SEO writers; Gold and above make more sense when plagiarism checks, integrations, and higher allowances matter.

## Bottom Line

Frase is the better platform. NeuronWriter is the better bargain. Pick Frase when the content team needs SEO plus GEO workflow consolidation. Pick NeuronWriter when the buyer needs low-cost semantic optimization and already owns keyword research, audits, and publishing elsewhere.

## FAQ

**Which is cheaper?**
NeuronWriter. Its annual Bronze and Silver plans sit well below Frase's current self-serve ladder.

**Which has better AI visibility tracking?**
Frase. NeuronWriter focuses on content optimization, not AI-answer visibility reporting.

**Can I use both?**
Yes. A team can brief and monitor in Frase, then use NeuronWriter as a second-pass scoring tool when budget or client preference requires it.
