# Frase.io vs NeuronWriter

[Frase.io](/tools/frase/) and [NeuronWriter](/tools/neuronwriter/) both sit in the SEO content workflow, but the buying logic is not close. Frase is the broader SEO/GEO platform with AI Agent, optimization, AI visibility tracking, audits, publishing, Content Guard, API, and MCP access. NeuronWriter is the budget-focused NLP content optimizer for writers who mainly need SERP analysis, semantic term guidance, AI credits, and draft scoring.

## Quick Answer

Choose Frase if the buyer wants one system for briefs, AI-assisted content production, GEO optimization, AI-search visibility tracking, audits, publishing, and integrations. Choose NeuronWriter if the buyer has a tighter budget and mainly needs an affordable content editor that scores drafts against SERP competitors.

## Decision Snapshot

- **Best broad workflow:** Frase. It is stronger when the work spans research, briefs, drafts, optimization, AI visibility, audits, and publishing.
- **Best budget optimizer:** NeuronWriter. It is stronger when the work is "make this article match the SERP better" at low monthly cost.
- **Pricing posture:** Frase starts at $49/month month to month or $39/month billed yearly. NeuronWriter runs from $23 to $117/month monthly, or $19 to $97/month equivalents on yearly billing.
- **Best for agencies:** Depends. Frase is better for workflow consolidation; NeuronWriter is better for low-cost high-volume scoring.
- **Best for GEO/AI visibility:** Frase. NeuronWriter has annual AI-monitored question allowances, but Frase has the broader visibility dashboard and workflow.
- **Main watch-out:** Frase is too much if you only need scoring. NeuronWriter is too narrow if the team needs audits, publishing, Content Guard, API/MCP access, or broader AI-search visibility reporting.

## Recent Changes

- **June 26, 2026:** Rechecked Frase pricing and AI Visibility help. Starter, Professional, Scale, and Enterprise still differ mainly by volume, seats, domains, AI visibility engines, prompts, API requests, and Content Guard coverage.
- **June 26, 2026:** Rechecked NeuronWriter pricing. Monthly Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, and Diamond remain $23, $45, $69, $93, and $117. Yearly billing shows $19, $37, $57, $77, and $97 monthly equivalents, with annual AI-monitored question allowances.

## Where Frase Wins

- **AI visibility tracking.** Frase tracks brand and competitor presence across AI search platforms, with visibility score, share of voice, sentiment, AI referrals, and plan-gated prompt history.
- **Workflow breadth.** AI Agent, SEO/GEO optimization, site audits, publishing, API, and MCP access are part of the platform story.
- **Brief and draft workflow.** It is better for teams that want to go from target keyword to brief to optimized document without stitching together many tools.
- **Team features.** Seats, domains, brand voice profiles, visibility history, AI visibility prompts, API requests, and audit pages scale by tier.
- **Strategic fit.** It belongs in stacks that treat AI-search citations and search visibility as part of the same content workflow.

## Where NeuronWriter Wins

- **Lower cost.** Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, and Diamond pricing makes NeuronWriter one of the lowest serious content-optimization ladders in the category.
- **Focused content scoring.** SERP analysis, NLP recommendations, Content Designer, and editor scoring are the core job.
- **Simple buyer promise.** It does not pretend to replace keyword suites, backlink tools, or full content-ops platforms.
- **Small-site fit.** Freelancers, affiliate operators, and small teams can get meaningful optimization guidance without buying a higher-priced content-ops platform.
- **Annual AI monitoring allowances.** Annual plans include standard AI-monitored question allowances, which helps budget buyers test AI-answer visibility without buying a heavier platform.

## Pricing and Plan Guidance

Buy Frase when the extra cost replaces multiple workflows: content briefs, AI visibility prompts, audit pages, publishing, Content Guard, API/MCP access, and team collaboration. Starter is the solo test lane; Professional is the practical content-team tier; Scale is for agencies or multi-site teams.

Buy NeuronWriter when the buyer only needs article scoring and can live with project/query limits. Silver is the practical floor for regular SEO writers; Gold and above make more sense when plagiarism checks, integrations, own-key support, API access, and higher allowances matter.

## Bottom Line

Frase is the better platform. NeuronWriter is the better bargain. Pick Frase when the content team needs SEO plus GEO workflow consolidation. Pick NeuronWriter when the buyer needs low-cost semantic optimization and already owns keyword research, audits, and publishing elsewhere.

## FAQ

**Which is cheaper?**
NeuronWriter. Its annual Bronze and Silver plans sit well below Frase's current self-serve ladder.

**Which has better AI visibility tracking?**
Frase. NeuronWriter now has annual AI-monitored question allowances, but Frase has the broader AI Visibility dashboard, prompt history, competitor tracking, and referral signal workflow.

**Can I use both?**
Yes. A team can brief and monitor in Frase, then use NeuronWriter as a second-pass scoring tool when budget or client preference requires it.
