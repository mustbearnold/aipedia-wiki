# Runway vs Synthesia

[Runway](../tools/runway.md) and [Synthesia](../tools/synthesia.md) lead AI video generation as of April 2026. Runway focuses on text-to-video and image-to-video with cinematic controls, while Synthesia specializes in avatar-based videos from scripts and documents.

## Quick Answer

Runway suits creative video production needing motion and effects; Synthesia fits scripted business videos with avatars. Choice depends on use case, with no overall winner.


|---|---|---|
| **Flagship** | Gen-4.5 + GWM-1 | Avatar Video Engine (2026) |
| **Price** | Free / Standard $15/mo / Pro $76/mo | Free / Starter $29/mo / Creator $89/mo |
| **Best For** | Cinematic text-to-video, editing | Talking-head business videos, agents |

## Where Runway Wins

- Higher motion quality in text-to-video clips up to 16 seconds at 1080p.[5]
- GWM-1 adds precise camera and scene controls for professional outputs.
- Supports image-to-video and video editing beyond avatars.[5]
- Free tier allows 125 credits for testing Gen-4.5 features.
- Integrates with Adobe tools for post-production workflows.

## Where Synthesia Wins

- 230+ avatars in 140+ languages for localized business content.[4]
- Converts scripts or documents to full videos with voiceovers in minutes.
- Enterprise features like SOC 2 compliance and custom avatars.
- Video agents handle interactive customer support scenarios.
- Higher output consistency for training, marketing, and explainer videos.

## Key Differences

Runway excels in generative video from prompts, producing dynamic scenes with advanced motion (Gen-4.5 scores 85% on VBench motion benchmarks), but requires more prompt engineering.[5] Synthesia prioritizes avatar-driven narrative videos, supporting 120+ languages and integrations with PowerPoint or PDFs, with less variability in results.[4] Runway's pricing scales by credits (e.g., Pro gives 2250 credits/mo at 4s/credit), while Synthesia uses flat tiers with unlimited minutes on higher plans.

## Who should choose Runway

Filmmakers and creators needing realistic motion, effects, or custom scenes from text/images. Best for artistic projects where control over style and camera matters.

## Who should choose Synthesia

Marketers, trainers, and businesses producing scripted avatar videos at scale. Ideal for consistent, multilingual content without video production skills.

## Bottom Line

Both tools advance AI video in 2026; Runway leads creative generation, Synthesia owns avatar workflows. Test free tiers to match your needs, as outputs vary by prompt and use.

## FAQ

**Which is cheaper?**  
Runway Standard at $15/mo offers more credits for short clips; Synthesia Starter at $29/mo suits longer avatar videos with unlimited exports on paid plans.

**Which has better output quality?**  
Runway for motion and realism in generative scenes; Synthesia for polished, professional talking-head delivery.

**Can I use both?**  
Yes; combine Runway for base clips and Synthesia for voiceovers or avatars in hybrid workflows.
