Zetrix AI and China's CAICT released Avatar this week, a blockchain platform that gives AI agents verified identities and access to digital assets. Think of it as digital passports for AI. Agents can now prove who they are when handling money, credentials, or sensitive transactions. This solves a trust problem. Companies and individuals need confidence that autonomous agents are actually who they claim to be before delegating financial or legal tasks. Avatar addresses that by anchoring agent identity on blockchain. The platform is significant because it treats agent identity as a first-class problem rather than an afterthought. As agents handle more real-world tasks, managing accounts, signing contracts, transferring funds, identity verification becomes critical infrastructure. This is early, but it signals where the industry is heading.
