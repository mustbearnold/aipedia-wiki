**Anthropic and NEC** announced a strategic collaboration to deploy Claude across NEC and build Japan-focused AI products.

## What was announced

- Claude will be made available to about **30,000 NEC Group employees** worldwide.
- NEC becomes Anthropic's first Japan-based global partner.
- The companies will jointly develop secure, industry-specific AI products for Japan.
- Initial sectors: finance, manufacturing, local government, and cybersecurity.

## Internal deployment

NEC plans to establish a Center of Excellence for AI-enabled engineering, with technical enablement and training from Anthropic.

The partnership specifically includes **Claude Code** for NEC engineering work and expanded internal use of **Claude Cowork** through NEC's Client Zero initiative.

## Customer products

Anthropic says Claude, Claude Opus 4.7, and Claude Code will be incorporated into NEC BluStellar Scenario, NEC's consulting, AI tools, security, and digital infrastructure program.

NEC is also integrating Claude into Security Operations Center services and next-generation cybersecurity services.

## Why it matters

This is not just another enterprise seat expansion. It is Anthropic using a major Japanese systems integrator to turn Claude into vertical infrastructure for finance, manufacturing, government, and security.

For the Claude page, this strengthens:

- enterprise distribution
- Japan/APAC footprint
- Claude Code enterprise credibility
- security and regulated-industry adoption
