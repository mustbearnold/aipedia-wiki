**Apple** named **John Ternus** its next CEO on April 21, 2026, effective September 1. **Tim Cook** will step down from the CEO role and remain as executive chairman. The transition comes alongside a simultaneous reorganization of Apple's hardware function under **Johny Srouji**, the new Chief Hardware Officer.

## The transition

- **Effective date:** September 1, 2026.
- **Outgoing:** Tim Cook, CEO since 2011, becomes executive chairman.
- **Incoming:** John Ternus, SVP Hardware Engineering since 2021.
- **Stated rationale:** lead Apple into the AI era via product and silicon integration rather than via standalone model R&D.

## The hardware reorg

Srouji's remit breaks into five divisions:

1. **Hardware Engineering** - core product hardware.
2. **Silicon** - Apple Silicon (M-series, A-series, custom AI accelerators).
3. **Advanced Technologies** - future-looking research.
4. **Platform Architecture** - cross-product system-on-chip platform work.
5. **Project Management** - delivery coordination across divisions.

The reorg tightens the loop between silicon design and AI feature delivery. Apple's position is that frontier LLMs are commodities (partners supply them) while differentiated **on-device inference silicon** is not.

## What it means for AI on Apple devices

Apple's public AI posture since late 2025 has been partnership-heavy: **Gemini powers the reimagined Siri** (shipping with iOS 26.4), specialty tasks route to ChatGPT, and third-party models are bookable via App Intents.

Ternus's CEO appointment doubles down on that model. Expect:

- **Tighter M5/M6 Neural Engine integration** with on-device inference for privacy-sensitive tasks.
- **No Apple-branded frontier LLM.** That bet was made in 2024 and is not being revisited.
- **Faster hardware iteration** on the Vision line as AI-first interface, paired with on-device models.
- **Continued external frontier-model partnerships** (Gemini primary, ChatGPT secondary, Anthropic unconfirmed).

## Why now

Cook's CEO tenure delivered the services-revenue expansion and the Services-plus-Wearables pivot that defined 2015-2025. The next decade's differentiation sits in silicon and AI-era product integration. Ternus's background makes him the natural pick for that cycle.

## Open questions

- Does Apple disclose its **Gemini-for-Siri commercial terms** under Ternus? Current deal is undisclosed.
- Does the Vision Pro line get a dedicated **on-device model** aimed at spatial-compute UX rather than generic chat?
- Does Apple **acquire a frontier-model lab** (perennial rumor, zero evidence) or formalize multi-partner frontier sourcing?
