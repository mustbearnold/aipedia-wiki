# ServiceNow debuts Otto and expands AI Control Tower at Knowledge 2026

ServiceNow issued press releases on May 5, 2026, the opening day of Knowledge 2026 in Las Vegas, outlining a broader “governed autonomous work” story anchored on AI Control Tower, Autonomous Workforce capabilities, and a new unified experience called Otto. The flagship Otto release is described as merging conversational AI, autonomous workflows, and enterprise search so employees can move from intent to completed work across systems.

The announcements also emphasize data visibility: a Data Catalog, lineage, and policy enforcement pitched as prerequisites for agent deployments at scale. ServiceNow names major model providers as ecosystem partners in the visibility layer, reflecting how enterprises now mix Anthropic, Google Cloud, Microsoft Azure, and AWS services behind a single workflow platform.

Financial framing in the same release packet references analyst-day targets, including a 2030 goal of more than $30 billion in subscription revenue with AI representing a large share of annual contract value. Those figures are forward-looking and should be read as investor messaging, not a product roadmap guarantee.

## Why this matters

Enterprises are past the “pilot chatbot” phase. The bottleneck is governance: who can invoke an agent, what data it can touch, and how to prove compliance after an action runs.

ServiceNow is positioning itself as the control plane that sits above siloed SaaS AI features. If that story lands, procurement teams will compare ServiceNow’s governance narrative directly against Salesforce Agentforce, Microsoft Copilot Studio, and vertical SaaS agents.

## Buyer take

Ask for concrete proof points on your own estate: identity and permission models for agents, audit logs for tool calls, separation between draft and committed actions, and how the platform handles model swaps when vendors update foundations.

Otto is a packaging bet. Evaluate whether it reduces sprawl or adds another surface that still needs training and change management.

## What is still unclear

Press materials bundle many modules. Real adoption depends on which SKUs ship in your contract region, integration depth with legacy systems, and whether Autonomous Workforce features match your risk tolerance for delegated execution.
