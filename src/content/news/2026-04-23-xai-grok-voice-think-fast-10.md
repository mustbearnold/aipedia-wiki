**xAI launched Grok Voice Think Fast 1.0**, a new real-time voice-agent model exposed through the xAI API.

## What it is for

xAI targets the model at messy real-world voice workflows:

- customer support
- phone sales
- appointment booking
- restaurant reservations
- multi-step enterprise support flows
- high-volume tool calling

The model is designed to collect and confirm structured data such as addresses, phone numbers, account numbers, and email addresses from spoken conversation.

## Benchmark and language claims

xAI says Grok Voice Think Fast 1.0 ranks first on **tau-voice Bench**, a benchmark for full-duplex voice agents under noisy, accented, interrupted, realistic conversation conditions.

The model supports **25+ languages**.

## Starlink deployment

xAI says the model powers Starlink's phone sales and customer support experience.

Reported operational metrics:

- 20% conversion rate on sales inquiries.
- 70% autonomous resolution rate for support inquiries.
- 28 tools used by the single voice agent.

## Why it matters

Grok has been mostly judged as a chat model and X-integrated assistant. This release gives xAI a stronger enterprise voice story and makes Grok more directly comparable with OpenAI Realtime, ElevenLabs Conversational AI, and enterprise contact-center agents.

The timing is awkward: the launch landed during a week of Grok congestion reports. That makes reliability the main question for buyers.
