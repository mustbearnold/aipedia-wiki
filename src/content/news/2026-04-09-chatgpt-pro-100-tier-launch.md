OpenAI filled the long-standing gap in its pricing ladder on April 9, 2026. The new $100/month ChatGPT Pro tier sits between $20 Plus and $200 Pro Max. Both Pro tiers now share the same model access; the only difference is usage allowance.

**Updated pricing ladder:**
- Free (with ads)
- Go: $8/month
- Plus: $20/month
- **Pro: $100/month (new)**
- Pro Max: $200/month

**What the $100 tier unlocks:**
- 5x Plus usage allowance
- Access to GPT-5.4 Pro (reasoning-first variant)
- Unlimited GPT-5.4 Instant and GPT-5.4 Thinking
- Same feature set as the $200 plan
- Launch promo through May 31: 10x Codex usage vs Plus

**Why OpenAI did this:** Anthropic has offered Claude Max at $100/month for most of 2025 and 2026, and heavy Codex users were either paying $200 for OpenAI or switching. The $100 tier closes the obvious gap.

**Who this targets:** Developers running Codex or GPT-5.4 on coding tasks daily. The Codex allowance is the headline differentiator. Light-to-medium chat users stay on Plus; enterprise buyers go to Business or Enterprise tiers.

**Competitive read:** Claude Max is now priced identically ($100) but Anthropic differentiates on context window (1M tokens on Opus 4.7 vs 400K on GPT-5.4 Pro) and agent-mode quality. OpenAI differentiates on Codex integration and DALL-E image access.
