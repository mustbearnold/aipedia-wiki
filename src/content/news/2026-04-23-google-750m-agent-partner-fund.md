**Google** announced a **$750M partner budget** at Cloud Next 2026 Day 2 (April 23, 2026) to drive distribution of the [Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform](/news/2026-04-23-gemini-enterprise-agent-platform/) through systems integrators and consultancies.

## The allocation

- **Funding recipients:** SIs, management consultancies, and specialist startup resellers.
- **Use cases:** agent design sprints, POC funding, migration incentives, co-sell rebates.
- **Timeframe:** drawdown through 2027 aligned to Gemini Agents adoption milestones.

## Why it matters

Model quality and platform depth are table stakes in 2026 enterprise AI. The actual battle is **distribution**. AWS ships Bedrock Agents through its already-deep AWS partner network; Azure bundles OpenAI into Microsoft's existing CIO-level relationships; Google's weak point has historically been fewer CIO-level enterprise ties.

$750M subsidizes the gap. Expect:

- **Deloitte, Accenture, PwC, KPMG, and EY** to stand up Gemini-Agent delivery practices in 2026-2027.
- **Specialist AI consultancies** to receive POC funding for agent deployments against Gemini Enterprise.
- **Startup resellers** to bundle vertical-specific agents (finance, healthcare, retail) on Vertex AI.

## Competitive framing

| Cloud | Partner budget (disclosed) | Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| **Google** | $750M (new, announced Apr 23) | Catch up on enterprise distribution |
| **AWS** | Not disclosed this year; Bedrock partner program existing since 2023 | Defend and deepen |
| **Azure** | Not disclosed; OpenAI GTM integrated into Microsoft direct sales | Bundle AI into existing CIO relationships |

Google's $750M is the **largest single publicly disclosed AI partner budget** in 2026 so far. Whether it moves the needle depends on whether SIs choose Google over the existing AWS and Azure relationships where their practices are already built.

## Read for enterprise buyers

If you are a Gemini-curious enterprise that has been blocked by "who's going to deploy this for us" cost concerns, the $750M budget directly subsidizes POCs and early-stage deployments through SI partners. Expect aggressive pricing on Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform engagements from Q3 2026.
