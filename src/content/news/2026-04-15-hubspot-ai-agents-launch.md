HubSpot shipped four AI agent products designed to handle real business work. The Prospecting Agent cuts through manual sales work, early customers see 2x better response rates than industry average. The Customer Agent handles customer emails and resolves 70% of cases automatically, reducing support overhead. HubSpot AEO helps content show up when people search ChatGPT or Perplexity instead of Google, addressing the shift in where users find information. These aren't experimental features. HubSpot is positioning agents as production tools that directly impact revenue and efficiency. The company is betting that businesses need agent workflows now, not later. The 70% automation rate on customer support is particularly notable, it suggests the agents are genuinely useful rather than gimmicks.
