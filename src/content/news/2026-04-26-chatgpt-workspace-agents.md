OpenAI introduced **workspace agents in ChatGPT**.

The important shift is product shape. ChatGPT is not just a personal assistant if agents can live inside a workspace and participate in team-level workflows.

## Why it matters

Team agents raise different questions than individual chat. They need permission boundaries, shared memory rules, audit logs, integration scopes, and clear ownership.

## Tool impact

For [ChatGPT](/tools/chatgpt/), workspace agents make the product more directly competitive with enterprise workflow tools and coding agents. The buyer question is no longer just model quality; it is whether the workspace controls are strong enough for real team use.
