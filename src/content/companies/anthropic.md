# Anthropic

Anthropic is an AI safety company founded in 2021 by Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei, and other former OpenAI researchers. Headquartered in San Francisco, Anthropic builds Claude, a family of large language models (Opus, Sonnet, Haiku) designed to be helpful, harmless, and honest. The company has raised over $10 billion in funding, including a $4 billion investment from Amazon, and is valued at more than $60 billion as of early 2026 ([Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/)). Claude supports context windows up to 1 million tokens and competes directly with GPT-4o and Gemini on capability benchmarks while differentiating on safety alignment, reasoning quality, and developer experience ([Anthropic API Documentation](https://docs.anthropic.com/)). Anthropic also offers Claude Code, a CLI-based agentic coding tool, and the Claude Agent SDK for building autonomous AI agents.

## Products
- [Claude](../tools/claude.md): Family of LLMs (Opus, Sonnet, Haiku) available via API and web/app interface. Known for strong reasoning, long context windows, and safety alignment.
- [Claude Code](../tools/claude-code.md): CLI-based agentic coding tool. Runs in the terminal, reads/writes files, executes commands. Competitive with Cursor and GitHub Copilot for developer workflows.
- **Claude Agent SDK:** Framework for building autonomous AI agents powered by Claude. Enables multi-step task execution with tool use.

## Strategy
Anthropic was founded in 2021 by Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei, and other former OpenAI researchers who left over disagreements about safety practices and commercialization speed. The company's core thesis is that AI safety research and commercial AI development are not at odds -- the best way to study AI safety is to build frontier models and apply safety techniques at scale.

The Claude model family has evolved rapidly. By 2026, Claude competes directly with GPT-4o and Gemini on capability benchmarks while differentiating on safety, long context (up to 1M tokens), and developer experience. Anthropic has invested heavily in "Constitutional AI" (RLHF guided by a set of principles) and interpretability research. The company publishes more safety research than any other frontier lab.

Commercially, Anthropic pursues a B2B-heavy strategy. Amazon (via a $4B+ investment) is a key partner, with Claude available through AWS Bedrock ([AWS Bedrock - Claude](https://aws.amazon.com/bedrock/claude/)). The consumer product (claude.ai) competes with ChatGPT but Anthropic's primary revenue comes from API usage by enterprises and developers. Claude Code represents a strategic push into the developer tools market, competing for the lucrative "AI-assisted coding" category.

## Why They Matter
Anthropic is the primary counterweight to OpenAI in the frontier model race. For developers and businesses evaluating AI tools, Claude often wins on tasks requiring careful reasoning, instruction following, and long-document processing. The safety-first positioning makes Anthropic the preferred vendor for regulated industries. Claude Code is emerging as a serious competitor in the AI coding space, particularly for developers who prefer terminal-native workflows. The company's research on interpretability and alignment may prove critical as models become more capable.
