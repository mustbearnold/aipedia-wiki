# Descript

Descript is the AI media-editing company behind [Descript](/tools/descript/), known for letting users edit audio and video by editing a transcript, as easily as a text document. Founded in 2017 by Andrew Mason (co-founder of Groupon), it pioneered text-based editing, overdub voice, and filler-word removal, and has raised about $101 million, including a $50 million Series C led by the OpenAI Startup Fund in 2022, reaching roughly $55 million ARR.

## What They Do

Descript reimagines media editing around text. It transcribes audio and video, then lets you cut, rearrange, and clean up the media by editing the words, plus AI features like Overdub voice, studio-quality sound, filler-word and gap removal, and clip generation. It targets podcasters, video creators, marketers, and teams who want fast, approachable editing without a traditional timeline-first tool.

The strategy is to make professional-grade editing accessible by hiding complexity behind a document-like interface and AI cleanup. Backed early by the OpenAI Startup Fund, Descript has steadily added generative features while keeping its transcript-first workflow as the core differentiator.

## Current Flagship Products

- [Descript](/tools/descript/): The AI-powered audio and video editor built around transcript-based editing, Overdub, and AI cleanup, on free and paid tiers.

## Strategic Position

Descript's moat is its distinctive transcript-first workflow and a loyal creator base, with millions of users and a recognizable product. Its challenges are a crowded creator-tools market, general video models adding editing features, and the need to keep monetizing a freemium base. It competes on ease of use and its unique editing paradigm rather than raw generation.

For AIpedia readers, Descript matters for approachable podcast and video editing with AI cleanup, weighed against timeline editors and avatar or generative-video tools for other jobs.
