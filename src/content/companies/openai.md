# OpenAI

OpenAI is the most visible AI company in the world, headquartered in San Francisco and valued at approximately $300 billion as of early 2026. Founded in 2015, the company builds general-purpose AI systems including ChatGPT (the conversational assistant that brought large language models to mainstream adoption), GPT-4o (its multimodal flagship model), DALL-E 3 (image generation), Whisper (open-source speech-to-text), and Codex (agentic code generation). OpenAI's video generation product Sora 2 was discontinued on April 1, 2026 (see [Sora 2 shutdown page](../dead/sora.md)). OpenAI has raised over $11 billion in funding and employs more than 3,500 people ([OpenAI](https://openai.com/)). ChatGPT reached 100 million users within months of its 2022 launch, making it the fastest consumer technology adoption in history at the time ([ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com/)). The company's API pricing sets market rates for AI inference across the industry ([OpenAI API](https://platform.openai.com/docs)).

## Products
- [ChatGPT](../tools/chatgpt.md): Conversational AI assistant, the product that brought LLMs to mainstream adoption. Free tier + $20/mo Plus + $200/mo Pro.
- [Sora 2](../dead/sora.md): **Discontinued April 1, 2026.** Text-to-video generation model. Released late 2025, shut down after failing to compete with Kling 3.0 and Runway Gen-4.5.
- **GPT-4o:** Multimodal flagship model powering ChatGPT and the API. Text, image, and audio in/out.
- **DALL-E 3:** Image generation model integrated into ChatGPT. Strong prompt adherence compared to earlier versions.
- **Whisper:** Open-source speech-to-text model. Best-in-class accuracy across languages.
- **Codex:** Code generation system powering agentic coding workflows. Originally powered GitHub Copilot.

## Strategy
OpenAI started as a nonprofit research lab in 2015, co-founded by Sam Altman and others. It transitioned to a "capped-profit" structure in 2019 to attract the capital needed for large-scale model training. By 2026, OpenAI has become the default name consumers associate with AI, largely thanks to ChatGPT's explosive growth (100M+ users within months of launch).

The company pursues a vertically integrated approach: build the most capable foundation models, then ship consumer and enterprise products on top. ChatGPT is both the cash cow and the distribution channel. API revenue from developers adds a second revenue stream. The $300B valuation reflects investor belief that OpenAI will capture a significant share of the AI application layer.

Strategically, OpenAI is racing to maintain its lead against Google DeepMind (compute advantage), Anthropic (safety-focused alternative), and open-source models from Meta and Mistral. The company expanded into video with Sora, but both Sora 1 and Sora 2 were shut down by April 2026. OpenAI continues to push into voice and agentic workflows. The transition away from the nonprofit structure has been contentious but signals commitment to scaling as a commercial entity.

## Why They Matter
OpenAI defined the current AI era. ChatGPT created the category of "AI assistant" in the public consciousness. GPT-4 set the benchmark that every competitor measures against. Their API pricing sets market rates for inference. For anyone building with AI tools, OpenAI's releases dictate the frontier of what's possible. However, their closed-source approach and high pricing create opportunities for competitors offering open-weight or cheaper alternatives.
