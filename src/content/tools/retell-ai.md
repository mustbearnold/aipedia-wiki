# Retell AI

Retell AI is a platform for building AI voice agents and AI chat agents. It is aimed at teams that want to automate customer calls, sales qualification, appointment workflows, support routing, and other voice-first business interactions.

The official pricing page positions Retell as "true pay as you go": start at $0, get free credits, and pay only for usage before moving to custom enterprise terms. In May 2026, the important buyer detail is that Retell also exposes component pricing for LLMs, text-to-speech, telephony, speech-to-speech options, and call-agent add-ons.

## System Verdict

> **Pick Retell AI when the workflow is a real phone call, not just a chatbot with voice.** Retell is strongest when you need call analytics, transcripts, simulation testing, webhooks, API access, concurrency, and enterprise escalation.
>
> **Skip it if you want simple fixed-price automation.** Voice agents are usage-based systems, and real cost depends on minutes, model choice, speech stack, telephony, and call complexity.
>
> **Who pays which tier:** Pay-as-you-go is right for builders and pilots. Enterprise is for larger deployments that need SSO, custom agreements, dedicated infrastructure, and higher concurrency.

## What It Actually Is

Retell is voice-agent infrastructure, not a generic transcription app. The pricing page lists pre-built agent templates, call analytics, transcripts, simulation testing, webhooks, API access, and 20 included concurrent calls on pay-as-you-go.

That combination is important. Good voice agents need more than a nice voice. They need interruption handling, fallback behavior, validation, call transfer logic, latency management, transcripts, testing, and post-call data flows. Retell is built around that operational layer.

Retell's docs also show a knowledge-base layer for website URLs, uploaded documents, and custom text, with automatic retrieval during calls. For outbound work, its batch-call docs support CSV uploads, dynamic variables, scheduling, and status metrics, but calls still start as concurrency slots become available.

## When To Pick Retell AI

- **You need phone call automation.** Retell is made for voice agents that talk with customers, not just internal dictation.
- **You need developer control.** Webhooks and API access are key for production workflows.
- **You want to test before committing.** Pay-as-you-go plus free credits lowers the first deployment barrier.
- **You need observability.** Analytics, transcripts, simulation testing, post-call analysis, and QA options are essential for debugging voice agents.
- **You need call-time context.** Retell knowledge bases can ingest URLs, documents, and custom text, then retrieve relevant chunks during a call.
- **You need enterprise escalation later.** Dedicated infrastructure, SSO, custom contracts, role-based access control, and no-cap concurrency are available on Enterprise.

## Failure Modes

- **Cost forecasting is hard.** Minutes, models, TTS, STT, telephony, add-ons, silence time, transfers, and retries all affect real cost.
- **Voice agents need narrow workflow design.** Broad prompts can fail in live calls.
- **Knowledge bases are not magic grounding.** Retell retrieves chunks automatically, but the docs still recommend clear source formatting and prompt constraints when the agent must avoid unsupported answers.
- **Compliance is not automatic.** Healthcare, legal, finance, and HR use cases need careful review of BAA terms, PII removal, recording/transcription opt-outs, and retention settings.
- **Human fallback still matters.** Agents should transfer or escalate when confidence drops.
- **Latency and interruption handling can make or break the experience.** Test with real accents, noisy rooms, and impatient callers.

## FAQ

**What is Retell AI?**
Retell AI is a platform for building AI voice agents and AI chat agents, especially for business phone workflows.

**How much does Retell AI cost?**
The official pricing page lists AI Voice Agents at $0.07-$0.31 per minute, AI Chat Agents at $0.002+ per message, and Enterprise custom pricing. Production costs can be higher when you add specific LLMs, premium voices, telephony, Knowledge Base, Batch Call, Safety Guardrails, PII Removal, or AI Quality Assurance.

**Does Retell AI have free credits?**
Yes. The pricing page lists $10 in free credits.

**Does Retell AI support knowledge bases?**
Yes. Retell's docs say knowledge bases can use website URLs, documents, and custom text, then retrieve relevant chunks automatically during calls. That helps support and FAQ agents, but the source material still needs clean formatting and testing.

**Is Retell AI good for phone support automation?**
Yes, if the workflow is designed tightly and tested. It includes templates, analytics, transcripts, simulation testing, knowledge bases, webhooks, API access, and batch-call workflows.
