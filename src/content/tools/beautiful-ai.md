## What it actually is

A slide tool with a rules-based layout engine and a more guided AI creation lane than the earlier Generate with AI experience. Users can start from a prompt, slide-by-slide instructions, a pasted outline, or a source document, then edit the outline before committing to designed slides.

Create with AI also lets users choose theme, image source, presentation language, and a consistent AI image style before generation. Once the deck exists, slide-level AI can generate alternatives, expose and edit the slide prompt, preserve exact text, regenerate images, or attach a file for slide context.

The moat is still the rules engine. Adding a fourth bullet rescales text; switching from three columns to four reshuffles spacing; swapping an image keeps the composition centered. The April 2026 product update also expanded storytelling layouts with Sequence slides, Connection diagrams, Horizontal funnels, ROI slides, and Map slides.

## When to pick Beautiful.ai

- **You write decks regularly and care about consistent formatting.** Consultants, managers, sales engineers, investor-relations teams.
- **You want AI to preserve your structure.** The Create with AI workflow is best when you can review the outline before generating final slides.
- **You need clean PPTX output that survives review.** Export fidelity beats card-based tools for PowerPoint round-trip.
- **Your team needs centrally-managed brand controls.** Team tier locks fonts, colors, and logos across seats.
- **You build the same deck types repeatedly.** The 300+ Smart Slide library covers most standard business formats.

## When to pick something else

- **Fastest prompt-to-deck generation:** [Gamma](/tools/gamma/) writes the whole first draft from a single prompt faster.
- **Shareable URL as the primary output:** [Gamma](/tools/gamma/) or [Pitch](/tools/pitch/) ship browser-native viewing better.
- **Unlimited free decks for a small team:** [Pitch](/tools/pitch/) free tier supports 5 members with unlimited presentations.
- **Creative, artistic, or social templates:** [Canva](/tools/canva/) library covers a broader non-business range.
- **Design-system depth across a whole product team:** [Figma](/tools/figma/) Slides integrates with the same design tokens the product uses.

## Failure modes

- **Not the fastest prompt-to-deck tool.** Create with AI can start from a prompt, outline, or document, but it is designed for staged outline review and slide-level iteration rather than fastest possible first draft.
- **Business templates only.** The library is strong on timelines, stats, org charts, and comparison tables. Creative or artistic work goes to [Canva](/tools/canva/).
- **Layout rules constrain manual control.** Users who want pixel-level positioning find the reflow behavior rigid.
- **Team pricing is steep for small teams.** $40/user/mo annual adds up fast at 3-5 seats.
- **Trial auto-charge matters.** Pro and Team trials require a credit card and the card is charged if the user does not cancel before the trial ends.
- **Cloud-first editing.** The desktop player and exports support offline presenting, but the core editing workflow remains web-based.
- **Free tier is gone.** The 2026 plan structure starts at Pro; a 14-day trial replaces the old free allowance.

## FAQ

**What is a Smart Slide?**
A Smart Slide is a Beautiful.ai template with a built-in layout engine. Add a bullet and the text resizes; switch columns and the layout reshuffles; swap an image and the composition holds. The design stays correct without manual formatting.

**Does Beautiful.ai generate full decks from a prompt?**
Yes, Create with AI can start from a prompt, detailed prompt, pasted outline, or source document. The difference is workflow: Beautiful.ai pushes users through outline review, theme and image settings, Smart Slide generation, and slide-level AI iteration. It is more controlled than one-shot generation, but [Gamma](/tools/gamma/) remains faster for a rough first draft.

**How does Beautiful.ai compare to Gamma?**
[Gamma](/tools/gamma/) wins on speed of first draft and shareable URL output. Beautiful.ai wins on layout integrity, PPTX fidelity, and brand controls. Repeat deck-builders favor Beautiful.ai; one-off prompt users favor Gamma.

**Is there a free tier?**
No. The 2026 plan structure starts at Pro. A 14-day free trial covers Pro and Team, requires a credit card, and auto-charges if not canceled before the trial ends.

**What security does Custom ship?**
Enterprise includes SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, CCPA, PCI, SSO, SCIM provisioning, audit logs, configurable brand guardrails, priority support, dedicated onboarding, and advanced permissions.
