# Notion AI

Notion AI is an LLM-powered feature layer developed by Notion Labs, Inc. It adds natural language Q&A across all your documents, AI autofill in databases, and a writing assistant for drafting, editing, and summarizing directly into the Notion workspace. Its primary differentiator is deep integration with Notion's existing workspace, databases, and connected apps. As of April 2026, Notion has consolidated AI access: the separate $8-10/month AI add-on is no longer available to new Free or Plus subscribers, and unlimited AI is now bundled into the Business tier at $20/user/month (annual) or $24/user/month (monthly). Existing Plus+AI subscribers are grandfathered at their original $10/member/month add-on pricing. Compared to Obsidian with AI plugins, Notion AI is plug-and-play but cloud-only; compared to NotebookLM, Notion AI is better for team workflows but weaker at focused document research.

## What It Does

Notion AI is an integrated LLM layer within Notion that provides natural language Q&A across all workspace pages and databases with source citations, AI Autofill for computed database properties, and a writing assistant for drafting, editing, summarizing, and translating content ([Notion AI](https://notion.so/product/ai)). AI Autofill in databases lets you create computed properties that use AI (e.g., auto-summarize a linked page, extract sentiment, classify entries). The AI writing assistant helps draft, edit, summarize, translate, and brainstorm within any page. It's not a standalone product — it's an add-on to the existing Notion workspace.

## Who It's For

- **Existing Notion users:** the primary audience. If you already use Notion, the AI add-on is a natural upgrade.
- **Teams with large internal wikis:** Q&A across hundreds of docs saves real time versus manual searching.
- **Knowledge workers:** summarize meeting notes, draft emails, extract action items from docs.
- **Project managers:** AI autofill in databases for status summaries, priority classification, and progress reports.
- **Students and researchers:** summarize research notes, generate outlines, translate sources.

## Pricing

Notion restructured AI access in mid-2025: the standalone $8-$10/member AI add-on is no longer offered to new Free or Plus users. To get Notion AI today, new users must subscribe to the Business tier ([Notion Pricing](https://www.notion.so/pricing)).

## Key Features

- **Q&A:** ask questions in natural language, get answers sourced from your workspace. Searches across pages, databases, and linked documents. The most useful AI feature in Notion.
- **AI Autofill in databases:** create AI-powered properties in any database. Auto-summarize linked pages, classify entries, extract structured data from unstructured text. Useful for content calendars, CRM systems, project trackers.
- **AI writing assistant:** available in any page via `/ai` or space bar. Draft, edit, summarize, translate, change tone, make longer/shorter, fix grammar. Same capabilities as any LLM wrapper but integrated into your workflow.
- **AI blocks:** embed AI-generated summaries that update as source content changes. Living summaries within your wiki.
- **Connected apps search:** Q&A can search across connected apps (Slack, Google Drive, etc.) with integrations. Expands AI coverage beyond Notion ([Notion Integrations](https://www.notion.so/integrations)).

## Limitations

Notion AI is only useful for existing Notion users, with Q&A accuracy that degrades on large or messy workspaces, per-member pricing that scales expensively for teams, and no option for local or offline AI processing of privacy-sensitive material.

- **Only useful if you're already in Notion.** This is an add-on, not a standalone product. If you don't use Notion, there's no reason to start just for the AI features.
- **Q&A accuracy is inconsistent.** On small, well-organized workspaces it works well. On large, messy workspaces with duplicate or outdated pages, it frequently pulls wrong or outdated information. Garbage in, garbage out.
- **AI Autofill has latency.** AI-computed database properties can take several seconds per row. On databases with hundreds of entries, this adds up.
- **Per-member pricing adds up fast.** $20-$24/member/month (Business tier, the only path to Notion AI for new users) for teams of 20+ people is $400-$480/month. The AI features may not justify that for every team member, especially when Claude or ChatGPT sits at $20/month for the individual.
- **No local/offline AI.** Everything goes through Notion's cloud. No option to bring your own model or run locally. Privacy-sensitive workspaces can't opt out.
- **Writing assistant is generic.** It's fine for basic drafting and editing, but it doesn't know your brand voice, writing style, or domain terminology beyond what's in your workspace.

## Bottom Line

Notion AI is a solid AI upgrade for teams already using Notion as their workspace and wiki. The Q&A feature genuinely saves time for teams with large knowledge bases — instead of digging through dozens of pages, you ask and get sourced answers. AI Autofill is clever for structured data workflows. But the AI features alone don't justify switching to Notion from another tool. If you're not already a Notion user, Obsidian with AI plugins offers a more flexible (and private) setup, and NotebookLM does research Q&A better for free.

## Best Alternatives

- **[Obsidian](obsidian.md):** local-first markdown notes with AI plugins. More flexible, more private, no per-user fees. Steeper learning curve.
- **[NotebookLM](notebooklm.md):** Google's free AI research tool. Better for document Q&A on specific source sets. No workspace features.
- **[Coda AI](../categories/ai-notes.md):** similar concept (AI in a doc/database workspace). Smaller community than Notion.
- **[Microsoft Copilot in OneNote](../categories/ai-notes.md):** AI in Microsoft's notebook. Better if your team is in the Microsoft ecosystem.

## FAQ

**Is Notion AI free?**
No. Notion AI requires a paid Notion plan. It is bundled into Plus ($10/mo per member) and Business ($15/mo per member) plans. The free Notion plan does not include AI features.

**Can Notion AI search across connected apps like Slack and Google Drive?**
Yes. Notion AI Q&A can search across connected integrations including Slack, Google Drive, and other linked apps, expanding its knowledge beyond your Notion workspace.

**How does Notion AI compare to ChatGPT for document Q&A?**
Notion AI is better for teams that already store knowledge in Notion, because it searches your workspace without requiring file uploads. ChatGPT is more flexible for general-purpose Q&A and works with any uploaded document, but lacks workspace integration.
