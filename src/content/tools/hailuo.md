# Hailuo AI

Hailuo AI is MiniMax's creator-facing AI video product. As of June 23, 2026, the current MiniMax video story is **Hailuo 2.3** and **Hailuo 2.3 Fast**, with product access through the Hailuo app and developer access through the MiniMax video generation API. The Hailuo site also promotes Hailuo Agent and Unified AI Space, so buyers should separate the creator app, the agent workspace, and the API route when comparing plans.

The buyer frame is simple: Hailuo is a good place to test short, fast, lower-cost clips. It should not be treated as the guaranteed best video model on the market without comparing current outputs against [Seedance](/tools/seedance/), [Kling](/tools/kling/), [Veo](/tools/veo/), and [Runway](/tools/runway/).

## System Verdict

> **Pick Hailuo if you need fast short-form text-to-video or image-to-video tests at sensible cost.** MiniMax says Hailuo 2.3 improves physical action, stylization, character micro-expressions, and prompt adherence over earlier Hailuo models, while Hailuo 2.3 Fast is positioned for lower-cost batch creation.
>
> **Skip Hailuo as your default if raw model quality is the only goal.** In the current AI video market, Seedance, Kling, Veo, and Runway deserve side-by-side testing before you decide. Hailuo's strongest role is value, speed, stylized clips, social concepts, and API experimentation, not a blanket "best video generator" claim.
>
> **Buyer warning:** Hailuo's consumer pricing and credit rules are dynamic. AiPedia can verify the official product, Hailuo 2.3 model page, MiniMax pay-as-you-go rates, MiniMax token plans, MiniMax API package pages, and public Hailuo payment policy on June 23, 2026, but exact live checkout allowances should be checked inside the current account screen before any high-volume purchase.

## What It Actually Is

Hailuo is a web and mobile AI video generator backed by MiniMax video models. The app is the easiest way for creators to test prompts, image-to-video, and stylized scenes. The official site also now pushes Hailuo Agent and Unified AI Space, but the core buying question for this page is still whether Hailuo 2.3/2.3-Fast should be in your video test set.

The MiniMax platform is the route for teams that want programmatic generation and predictable usage accounting. The current official MiniMax API docs support text-to-video, image-to-video, first-and-last-frame, and subject-reference routes, but route support is model-specific. Use Hailuo 2.3/2.3-Fast for the current Hailuo T2V/I2V lanes, Hailuo 02 for the documented first-and-last-frame route, and S2V-01 for subject-reference generation unless MiniMax updates those docs.

## When To Pick Hailuo

- **Short-form social concepts:** You need quick clips for TikTok, Shorts, Reels, ads, thumbnails, or concept decks.
- **Image-to-video tests:** You have a product still, character frame, or style reference and want to see fast motion variants.
- **Stylized or anime output:** MiniMax specifically calls out stronger stylization, anime, illustration, ink-wash, and game-CG support in Hailuo 2.3.
- **Batch ideation:** Hailuo 2.3 Fast is positioned for lower-cost batch creation, so it is useful when you need many rough cuts before choosing a winner.
- **API evaluation:** Developers can test MiniMax's asynchronous video API, task querying, and file download flow.

## When To Pick Something Else

- **Best raw model quality:** Start with [Seedance](/tools/seedance/), [Kling](/tools/kling/), and [Veo](/tools/veo/) before deciding Hailuo is good enough.
- **Production editing workspace:** [Runway](/tools/runway/) is still the stronger workflow product for editing, asset management, and creator operations.
- **Open-weight control:** [Wan](/tools/wan/) is the better research and self-hosting route.
- **Avatar or presenter video:** Use [HeyGen](/tools/heygen/), [Synthesia](/tools/synthesia/), or [Tavus](/tools/tavus/) instead of Hailuo.
- **Locked enterprise procurement:** Teams with strict data, region, contract, or admin requirements should verify MiniMax terms before building around it.

## Pricing And Buying Advice

Do not buy Hailuo only because an old review quotes a low monthly price. The consumer product can show promotional pricing, credit bundles, watermarks, model-specific access, and plan limits that change by account, region, and date.

For developers, the clearer anchors are MiniMax's platform pricing pages. On June 23, 2026, the official docs list pay-as-you-go video rates, video packages, token-plan subscriptions, and separate credit packs.

For Hailuo app users, the 2.3 model page shows example credit costs of 25 credits for a 768p 6-second Hailuo 2.3 clip, 50 credits for 768p 10 seconds, and 50 credits for 1080p 6 seconds. Treat those as example app-credit anchors, not a guarantee that your account, region, or plan exposes the same allowance.

MiniMax pay-as-you-go video rates:

- **Hailuo 2.3-Fast:** $0.19 for 768p 6 seconds, $0.32 for 768p 10 seconds, and $0.33 for 1080p 6 seconds.
- **Hailuo 2.3:** $0.28 for 768p 6 seconds, $0.56 for 768p 10 seconds, and $0.49 for 1080p 6 seconds.
- **Hailuo 02:** $0.28 for 768p 6 seconds, $0.56 for 768p 10 seconds, $0.49 for 1080p 6 seconds, $0.10 for 512p 6 seconds, and $0.15 for 512p 10 seconds.

MiniMax token plans:

- **Plus:** $20/month for 10M tokens.
- **Max:** $50/month for 30M tokens.
- **Ultra:** $120/month for 80M tokens.

The token-plan page also lists credit packs and shared quota windows, but its resource wording can be broader than a simple Hailuo-video allowance. Use pay-as-you-go rates or video package points for first-pass video budgeting unless your MiniMax console confirms a cleaner subscription route.

For video packages:

- **Standard:** $1,000 for 3,760 video points, valid one month.
- **Pro:** $2,500 for 9,920 video points, valid one month.
- **Scale:** $4,500 for 18,900 video points, valid one month.
- **Business:** $6,000 for 26,780 video points, valid one month.
- **Custom:** negotiated video-point volume with priority access and custom limits.

MiniMax also publishes video-point deductions by model, resolution, and duration. Failed jobs and security-review failures do not deduct points, but remaining package points reset to zero when the package expires. If you only need a few clips, start in the app or pay-as-you-go. If you need repeatable production volume, price the API package against the number of 6-second and 10-second clips you expect to generate.

## Hailuo Vs Current Alternatives

**Hailuo vs Seedance:** Seedance is a better first test when you want frontier creator-model output. Hailuo is better when MiniMax pricing, speed, stylized output, or API access is the draw.

**Hailuo vs Kling:** Kling is the stronger default for many buyer comparisons where prompt adherence, cinematic motion, and creator ecosystem matter. Hailuo is a lower-friction short-clip testbed.

**Hailuo vs Veo:** Veo should be tested first for Google ecosystem video work and high-end multimodal generation. Hailuo is more approachable for quick web-app and API iteration.

**Hailuo vs Runway:** Runway is a production workspace. Hailuo is a model surface and creator app. Pick Runway when editing workflow matters more than per-clip cost.

**Hailuo vs Wan:** Wan is the open-weight route. Hailuo is the hosted MiniMax route.

## Failure Modes

- **Stale pricing assumptions:** Consumer plan prices and credits can differ from old reviews. Verify inside the Hailuo account before buying.
- **Short-clip economics:** Hailuo is still a short-form generation tool. Long narrative pieces need stitching, editing, and consistency checks.
- **Quality variance:** Complex physics, hands, crowds, and precise camera choreography still need repeated generations and human review.
- **Workflow gap:** Hailuo is not a full editing suite. Expect to finish clips elsewhere.
- **Compliance review:** MiniMax is a China-based provider, so procurement teams should review data, region, legal, and policy needs directly.
- **Model route confusion:** Hailuo app access and MiniMax API access are related but not identical. Check which model and plan your job will actually use.
- **Route mismatch:** first-and-last-frame and subject-reference controls exist in MiniMax docs, but the current documented model routes are Hailuo 02 and S2V-01 rather than Hailuo 2.3.
- **Payment-policy age:** Hailuo's public payment-policy page lists monthly plan anchors, but it is dated July 14, 2025 and still references Hailuo02, so use it as a public terms signal and confirm live checkout.

## FAQ

**Is Hailuo AI free?**
Hailuo has free app access, but free limits, watermarks, credits, and model access can change. Check the current Hailuo account screen before assuming a specific allowance.

**What is the current Hailuo model?**
MiniMax's current video lineup lists Hailuo 2.3 and Hailuo 2.3 Fast, with Hailuo 02 still present as an older model route.

**Does Hailuo have an API?**
Yes. MiniMax's video generation API supports asynchronous video tasks and includes text-to-video, image-to-video, first-and-last-frame, and subject-reference routes. Do not assume every Hailuo 2.3 route supports the same controls: current docs show first-and-last-frame under MiniMax-Hailuo-02 and subject-reference under S2V-01.

**Is Hailuo better than Kling or Veo?**
Not by default. Hailuo is worth testing for value, speed, and stylized short clips. Kling and Veo should be tested first when your goal is the strongest current video output.

**Which Hailuo plan should I buy?**
Casual creators should start with the app and verify current credits before paying. Developers with small workloads should price pay-as-you-go first; teams with predictable volume should model MiniMax video package points against their expected clip count.
