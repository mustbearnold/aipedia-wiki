# Jimeng AI

ByteDance's consumer creative app. The Chinese-market brand is **Jimeng (即梦)**, accessed at [jimeng.jianying.com](https://jimeng.jianying.com). The international creative route is **Dreamina** on CapCut's domain.

The engine is two ByteDance model families. **Seedream 5.0 Lite** handles image generation with online search and deeper visual reasoning on ByteDance Seed's public model page. **Seedance 2.0** handles video with text, image, audio, and video inputs. Jimeng and Dreamina are the app surfaces; Seedream and Seedance are the model families underneath.

## System Verdict

> **Pick Jimeng if work lives inside the ByteDance creative stack.** Douyin creators, CapCut editors, and supported Dreamina users get a consumer creative workflow with image, video, and canvas tools, plus Seedream 5.0 Lite's online-search image mode. Integration with ByteDance/Jianying/CapCut surfaces is the reason to test it.
>
> **Skip it if compliance, peak aesthetic, or logo text matter most.** US and EU teams should weigh the privacy posture of a Chinese consumer app with uncertain data residency. Midjourney still wins on stylized cinematic output. Ideogram still wins on dense typography. Flux wins on open-weight fine-tuning. Jimeng is a consumer app first, an enterprise tool never.
>
> **Who pays which tier:** start free, then verify the exact Jimeng/Dreamina route in-app before paying. Historical third-party pricing has cited low RMB and $18-$84/mo Dreamina ranges, but the June 25 official public-page check did not expose a durable crawlable rate card.

## What it actually is

A consumer creative app, not a developer platform. The surface is a web and mobile client where users type a prompt, pick a model, and generate an image or short video. The model stack is shared across Jimeng and Dreamina.

Seedream 5.0 Lite is the image pipeline. ByteDance Seed describes it as a unified multimodal image generation model with online search, deeper reasoning, style transfer, image editing, and complex multi-subject generation. The important buyer point: do not confuse the app surface with the model name.

Seedance 2.0 is the video pipeline. ByteDance's official launch page describes targeted video modifications, video extension, two-channel audio, synchronized sound, and broad commercial/scenario adaptability. For buyer work, compare it against Kling, Veo, and Runway on the exact route you can access.

The moats: ByteDance owns the creative distribution surface, not just the model. A generated image or clip can move through Jimeng, Dreamina, Jianying, or CapCut-style workflows with less glue than a standalone model lab. The weakness is access and procurement clarity: China accounts, Dreamina regional availability, watermark rules, export rights, and exact credit economics can change by route.

## When to pick Jimeng

- **Workflow is already on CapCut.** Native pipeline beats any export-import loop. Image and video flow into the same editor.
- **China-market creator or Douyin operator.** Jimeng is the default creative tool for the ByteDance ecosystem. Western tools have friction.
- **Budget might matter, but verify first.** Historical third-party pricing makes Jimeng/Dreamina look cheap, but June 25 official public pages did not expose a durable rate card. Confirm plan, credits, rollover, watermark removal, and region in the app.
- **Prompt needs current reference.** Seedream 5.0 Lite online search can ground images in newer context instead of relying only on training data.
- **You are in a supported Dreamina market.** Dreamina is the route to test outside mainland China, but availability and feature gates can vary.

## When to pick something else

- **Peak aesthetic on stylized cinematic shots:** [Midjourney](/tools/midjourney/) still leads on mood, lighting, and composition.
- **Open-weight fine-tuning or self-hosting:** [Flux](/tools/flux/). Seedream is closed-weight.
- **Logo text and dense typography:** [Ideogram](/tools/ideogram/). Seedream handles signage-level text but trails Ideogram on poster work.
- **ByteDance's other consumer assistant:** [Doubao](/tools/doubao/). Chat-first, different use case.
- **Pure video generation on the same model stack:** [Seedance](/tools/seedance/) direct. Skip the Jimeng shell if only video is wanted.

## Failure modes

- **China signup requires Chinese phone and payment.** International users cannot use the full Jimeng app directly. Dreamina is the path.
- **Dreamina international rollout is phased.** Supported markets launched first (Brazil, Indonesia, Malaysia, Mexico, Philippines, Thailand, Vietnam); other regions see friction.
- **Compliance posture is a consumer app's posture.** Data residency, training-on-user-content, and prompt logging defaults are not enterprise-aligned. US and EU legal teams will flag this.
- **Pricing screenshots age fast.** Jimeng/Dreamina plan names, credits, watermark rules, and route availability can move faster than static public pages.
- **Some features were paused on privacy concerns.** AI-generated voice from facial images was temporarily suspended after public pushback. Feature availability shifts with policy updates.
- **Model churn is fast.** Seedream has moved through multiple major labels inside one year; Seedream 5.0 Lite and Seedance 2.0 are the June 7 public model anchors. Workflows built on a specific version may need re-tuning each cycle.
- **Do not treat every Seedance site as official.** Independent Seedance-branded wrappers can be useful or risky depending on disclosure. Trace the route back to ByteDance Seed, Dreamina/Jimeng, BytePlus, or a clearly disclosed provider.
- **Language friction remains.** Jimeng's native prompts are optimized on Chinese; some English prompts produce weaker output than equivalent Chinese phrasing. Dreamina narrows the gap.

## FAQ

**What is the relationship between Jimeng, Dreamina, Seedream, and Seedance?**
Jimeng (即梦) is the Chinese-market consumer app. Dreamina is the international CapCut-domain route. Seedream 5.0 Lite is the June 7 public image-model anchor, and Seedance 2.0 is the public video-model anchor. CapCut/Jianying/Dreamina are the editing and app surfaces where outputs land.

**Can I use Jimeng outside China?**
The full Jimeng app at jimeng.jianying.com is China-oriented. International users should start with Dreamina on CapCut's domain, then verify whether their country, account, and target feature are supported before building a workflow around it.

**What does Seedream 5.0 Lite add over earlier versions?**
ByteDance Seed says Seedream 5.0 Lite adds online search, deeper reasoning, precise style transfer, advanced image editing, and stronger complex multi-subject generation. The buyer value is current-context image creation and better instruction following, not enterprise governance.

**How does pricing compare to Midjourney?**
Do not compare from old screenshots. Midjourney publishes a public plan table; Jimeng/Dreamina pricing is region/account-gated on the public pages AiPedia checked on June 25. Historical third-party coverage has cited low Jimeng/Dreamina entry prices, but the safer comparison is the actual in-app credit math you see in your region.

**Does Jimeng have an API?**
Treat Jimeng/Dreamina as consumer app routes, not clean developer platforms. For programmatic work, verify ByteDance Seed, BytePlus, ModelArk, or another disclosed official route rather than relying on a wrapper that only says "Seedance" or "Seedream."

**Is there a free tier?**
Yes, but exact free credits, seconds, watermarks, and model access are route-specific. Test the free path first, then verify paid plan economics in the live account before subscribing.
