# Semrush

Semrush is an all-in-one SEO and digital marketing platform developed by Semrush Inc., a publicly traded company (NYSE: SEMR). It provides keyword research, backlink analysis, technical site auditing, rank tracking, competitor intelligence, PPC research, and AI search visibility tracking in a single platform. Its key differentiator is breadth: no other SEO tool covers as many marketing channels. As of April 2026, Semrush maintains a database of 26 billion+ keywords and 43 trillion+ backlinks, with plans starting at $140/month. Compared to Ahrefs, Semrush covers more ground but has slightly weaker backlink data.

## What It Does

Semrush is an all-in-one SEO and digital marketing platform with the largest keyword database at 26 billion+ keywords and 43 trillion+ backlinks, covering keyword research, backlink analysis, site auditing, rank tracking, competitor analysis, PPC research, and AI Search Visibility tracking ([Semrush](https://semrush.com)). In 2025-2026 it added AI Search Visibility — tracking how often your brand appears in AI-generated answers from ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. It has one of the largest backlink databases in the industry.

## Who It's For

- **SEO professionals and agencies:** the industry-standard platform. Most client reports are built in Semrush.
- **Digital marketing teams:** SEO, PPC, content, and social in one dashboard.
- **Enterprise companies:** site audit at scale, API access, multi-user workflows.
- **Competitor researchers:** reverse-engineer any competitor's organic strategy, paid ads, and top pages.
- **Content strategists:** topic research, content gap analysis, SEO Writing Assistant.

## Key Features

- **Keyword research:** 26B+ keyword database. Keyword Magic Tool finds long-tail opportunities. Keyword difficulty, search volume, intent classification, SERP features analysis.
- **Backlink analytics:** crawls 43T+ backlinks. Backlink gap analysis shows where competitors have links you don't. Backlink Audit identifies toxic links.
- **Site Audit:** crawls up to 100K pages. Finds technical SEO issues (broken links, duplicate content, page speed, schema markup, Core Web Vitals). Prioritized fix recommendations.
- **Position tracking:** daily rank tracking for your target keywords. Local, national, and device-level granularity.
- **AI Search Visibility:** tracks brand mentions in AI-generated answers (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews). This is a unique feature no other SEO tool does as well ([Semrush AI Search Visibility](https://www.semrush.com/features/)).
- **Competitor analysis:** enter any domain and see their top keywords, traffic estimates, top pages, ad copies, and backlink profile. The core of most SEO audits.
- **SEO Writing Assistant:** content optimization with SEO scoring, readability, tone of voice, and plagiarism checking. Less detailed than Surfer SEO but integrated into the suite.

## Limitations

Semrush is the most expensive mainstream SEO platform at $140-$500 per month, with an overwhelming interface of 50+ tools, rigid report limits on lower tiers, and a backlink database that still trails Ahrefs in freshness and completeness.

- **Expensive.** $140/mo for the base plan is a significant investment. For solo bloggers or small sites, Ahrefs Starter ($29/mo) covers the basics at a fraction of the cost.
- **Overwhelming interface.** 50+ tools in one platform. New users get lost. The learning curve is real; plan for weeks, not days.
- **Backlink database is second to Ahrefs.** Semrush has improved massively, but Ahrefs still has the edge in backlink data freshness and completeness.
- **Report limits are rigid.** Pro plan's 10K results per report is limiting for large sites. Upgrading to Guru doubles the cost.
- **Feature bloat.** Includes PPC, social media, local SEO tools that many users never touch but pay for. You're subsidizing features you may not need.
- **AI Writing Assistant is basic.** Not competitive with Surfer SEO for content optimization. Fine for quick checks, not for serious on-page work.

## Bottom Line

Semrush is the most complete SEO platform available. If you can only pick one SEO tool, pick Semrush — it covers more ground than anything else. The AI Search Visibility feature is forward-looking and increasingly important as AI answers eat into traditional SERP clicks. The main downsides are price (it's the most expensive option) and the fact that Ahrefs slightly beats it on backlink data. For agencies and professionals, Semrush is the default. For solo operators, consider Ahrefs Starter or pairing a cheaper tool with Surfer SEO for content.

## FAQ

**Is Semrush worth $140/month?**
For SEO professionals and agencies, yes. Semrush replaces multiple tools (keyword research, backlink analysis, site audit, rank tracking, and competitor analysis) in one platform. Solo bloggers with small sites should consider Ahrefs Starter at $29/month or SE Ranking at $52/month as cheaper alternatives.

**How does Semrush compare to Ahrefs?**
Semrush is broader (SEO, PPC, social, AI visibility); Ahrefs has better backlink data. If you need an all-in-one marketing suite, choose Semrush. If your priority is link building, choose Ahrefs. Many agencies use both.

**What is Semrush AI Search Visibility?**
It is a feature that tracks how often your brand appears in AI-generated answers from ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. No other SEO tool does this as comprehensively. It helps you measure and optimize your visibility in the growing AI search channel.
