# Ahrefs

Ahrefs is an SEO toolset developed by Ahrefs Pte. Ltd., a Singapore-based company founded by Dmitry Gerasimenko. It provides the industry's largest and most frequently updated backlink index, with 35 trillion+ known links crawled and refreshed every 15-30 minutes. Ahrefs also covers keyword research, site auditing, rank tracking, and content discovery. Its primary differentiator is backlink data depth and freshness, which no competitor matches. As of April 2026, pricing starts at $29/month with the Starter plan, making it the most affordable entry point for professional-grade SEO data. Compared to Semrush, Ahrefs has stronger backlink data but narrower feature coverage.

## What It Does

Ahrefs maintains the industry's largest backlink index at 35 trillion+ known links, crawled and refreshed every 15-30 minutes, with comprehensive keyword research, site auditing, rank tracking, and content discovery tools built on top of that backlink foundation ([Ahrefs](https://ahrefs.com)). You can see every backlink pointing to any domain, page, or URL — who links there, with what anchor text, when the link appeared, and whether it's followed or nofollowed. The Starter plan at $29/mo opened up Ahrefs to solo bloggers for the first time in 2024.

## Who It's For

- **Link builders:** the primary use case. Ahrefs' backlink data is unmatched for finding link opportunities, analyzing competitor link profiles, and monitoring new/lost links.
- **SEO professionals:** comprehensive enough for full SEO workflows, especially teams that prioritize off-page SEO.
- **Content marketers:** Content Explorer finds the most linked-to and shared content on any topic. Great for content ideation.
- **Affiliate site owners:** analyze competitor backlinks, find link-building targets, track keyword rankings.
- **Solo bloggers:** Starter plan at $29/mo makes Ahrefs accessible for small sites.

## Pricing

Ahrefs offers four tiers from $29 per month for the Starter plan to $449 per month for Advanced with API access, making it the most affordable entry point for professional-grade SEO data with annual billing discounts of approximately 17% ([Ahrefs Pricing](https://ahrefs.com/pricing)).

| Plan | Price | Key Limits |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Starter | $29/mo | 750 tracked keywords, limited reports, 1 user |
| Lite | $129/mo | 5 projects, 750 tracked keywords, full backlink data |
| Standard | $249/mo | 20 projects, 2,000 tracked keywords, SERP history |
| Advanced | $449/mo | 50 projects, 5,000 tracked keywords, API access |

*Prices verified 2026-04-13. Annual billing saves ~17%. Check [ahrefs.com/pricing](https://ahrefs.com/pricing) for current rates.*

## Key Features

- **Backlink Index:** the largest and most frequently updated backlink database available. 35T+ known links. Updated every 15-30 minutes for active crawl targets. The gold standard for backlink research ([Ahrefs Backlink Checker](https://ahrefs.com/backlink-checker)).
- **Site Explorer:** enter any domain and see its full backlink profile, organic keywords, top pages, and traffic estimates. The core tool most users open first.
- **Keywords Explorer:** keyword difficulty scores, search volume, click metrics, SERP overview with backlink counts for ranking pages. Covers 10+ search engines including Google, YouTube, Amazon, Bing.
- **Content Explorer:** search a topic and find every page that ranks or gets shared for it. Filter by traffic, backlinks, publish date. Unmatched for content research and link prospecting.
- **Site Audit:** crawls your site and identifies technical SEO issues. Less polished than Semrush's audit tool but covers the essentials.
- **Rank Tracker:** daily tracking with desktop/mobile split. SERP feature tracking. Shareable reports.
- **Free Webmaster Tools:** limited free access to Site Audit and Site Explorer for sites you verify ownership of. Good entry point.

## Limitations

Ahrefs is an SEO-only platform with no PPC research, social media tools, AI Search Visibility tracking, or built-in content optimization, and its Starter plan restricts report depth enough that heavy users must jump to $129 per month.

- **Narrower than Semrush.** Ahrefs is SEO-only. No PPC research, social media tools, or AI Search Visibility tracking. If you need an all-in-one marketing suite, Semrush covers more.
- **Content optimization is weak.** No Content Editor equivalent to Surfer SEO. The SEO toolbar gives basic suggestions but nothing like real-time content scoring.
- **Keyword data differences.** Ahrefs' keyword difficulty metric is purely backlink-based (how many referring domains do top-10 results have). This is useful but doesn't account for content quality, brand authority, or topical relevance. Semrush's difficulty score considers more factors.
- **Starter plan is limited.** $29/mo gets you in the door but with restricted report depth. Heavy users hit limits quickly and need to jump to $129/mo.
- **No AI features.** As of April 2026, Ahrefs has not added meaningful AI/LLM features beyond basic AI writing assistance. No AI search visibility tracking, no AI-generated content briefs. This is a gap.
- **UI can be dense.** The interface is powerful but information-heavy. Newer users find Semrush's UI more approachable.

## Bottom Line

Ahrefs has the best backlink data in the industry, and that's not close. If your SEO strategy is link-building-focused, Ahrefs is the right tool. The Starter plan at $29/mo is the best low-cost entry point for serious SEO data. Where Ahrefs falls short is breadth — Semrush covers PPC, social, AI visibility, and content optimization that Ahrefs doesn't. The ideal setup for serious SEOs is Ahrefs for backlinks + Surfer SEO for content optimization, but that's $158-$348/mo combined. If you can only pick one, Semrush covers more ground; if backlinks matter most, pick Ahrefs.

## Best Alternatives

- **[Semrush](semrush.md):** broader feature set including PPC, social, AI Search Visibility. Slightly weaker backlink data. Starts at $140/mo. The main competitor.
- **[Surfer SEO](surfer-seo.md):** content optimization that Ahrefs lacks. $79/mo. Use alongside Ahrefs, not instead of it.
- **[Moz Pro](../categories/ai-seo.md):** simpler, cheaper ($99/mo). Domain Authority metric is industry-standard despite some limitations. Less data depth.
- **[Majestic](../categories/ai-seo.md):** backlink-focused like Ahrefs. Trust Flow / Citation Flow metrics. Less feature-rich overall but cheaper for pure link research.

## FAQ

**Is Ahrefs Starter plan enough for beginners?**
Yes. The $29/month Starter plan includes Site Explorer, Keywords Explorer, and basic backlink data. It covers keyword research and competitor analysis for small sites. Heavy users doing link building at scale will need the Lite plan at $129/month for full backlink data access.

**Does Ahrefs have AI features?**
As of April 2026, Ahrefs has not added meaningful AI or LLM features. There is no AI search visibility tracking, no AI-generated content briefs, and no AI writing assistant comparable to Semrush's tools. This is a notable gap in their product.

**Should I choose Ahrefs or Semrush?**
If backlinks and link building are your priority, choose Ahrefs. If you need an all-in-one platform covering SEO, PPC, social, and AI search visibility, choose Semrush. Many professional SEOs use both tools for different purposes.
