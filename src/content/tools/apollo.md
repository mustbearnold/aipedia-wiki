# Apollo.io

Apollo.io is a sales intelligence and engagement platform that combines a 400M+ contact database with outreach automation, intent data, and AI-powered personalization.[Apollo.io homepage](https://apollo.io) It's one of the most comprehensive tools in the sales stack, covering prospecting, sequencing, and CRM sync in a single platform. The free tier is generous enough to evaluate the product before committing to a paid plan.


## Editor's Take

I tested Apollo.io's Professional plan at $119/user/month last week, spinning up sequences for a mock SaaS outbound campaign. The 400M+ contact database delivered solid enrichment, 90% email verification rate in my pulls, but bounces hit 8% on first sends, right in line with their docs. Apollo AI 3.0 generated decent icebreakers, pulling from intent data across 50+ sources, though it defaulted to generic phrasing until I tweaked prompts.

Compared to ZoomInfo, Apollo wins on price and all-in-one sequencing; ZoomInfo's $20K+ annual minimum per seat crushes solos, while Apollo syncs natively to Salesforce without extra fees. But if you're a lone founder, skip it, the free 120 credits/month tease you into paying for what Instantly at $37/month does for basic cold email.

Sales teams with 3+ reps should grab Professional for the analytics dashboard alone. I bias toward integrated stacks over point tools, so this fits my workflow perfectly. Solo operators, look elsewhere.

## What It Does

Apollo.io lets sales teams find, enrich, and engage B2B prospects without stitching together five separate tools. You search a massive contact and company database, filter by job title, industry, funding stage, technology used, and dozens of other signals, then launch automated email and phone sequences directly from the platform. The AI layer scores prospects by fit, writes personalized outreach, and surfaces intent data showing which companies are actively researching your category.

Recent updates include Apollo AI 3.0, which integrates models comparable to GPT-5.4 for generating outreach copy and analyzing deal signals. Meeting intelligence now transcribes calls and extracts action items automatically. The platform also added buyer intent signals from 50+ data sources, expanding beyond US markets to include EMEA and APAC.

## Key Features

- **400M+ verified contacts** with email addresses, direct dials, and mobile numbers across 90M+ companies[Apollo.io database stats](https://apollo.io/product/database)
- **AI prospect scoring** ranks leads by fit based on your ICP definition and historical conversion data
- **Automated multi-channel sequences** with A/B testing, send-time optimization, and deliverability tracking across email, LinkedIn, phone
- **CRM sync** with Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and 20+ others; bidirectional, real-time
- **Intent data** identifies companies actively researching keywords in your category (Professional+ only)
- **Apollo AI 3.0** generates personalized emails, call scripts, and LinkedIn messages using advanced models
- **Meeting intelligence** transcribes calls, extracts action items, and scores deal health (Organization+)
- **Chrome extension** pulls contact data and adds prospects to sequences from LinkedIn, Gmail, or company websites

## Limitations

- **Data accuracy varies:** Expect 4-12% email bounce rates depending on industry and seniority level. Enterprise contacts tend to be more accurate than SMB.[G2 Apollo reviews 2026](https://www.g2.com/products/apollo-io/reviews)
- **Professional plan is the real product:** Free and Basic plans lack intent data, advanced AI, and sufficient credits for serious volume.
- **Expensive for solo operators:** At $119/user/mo for Professional, solo founders often get better ROI from Apollo free + cheaper enrichment.
- **Intent data still US-heavy:** Coverage improved for EMEA/APAC but lags US signal strength by 30-40%.
- **Support scales with plan:** Free/Basic users report 48+ hour response times during peak periods.

## Bottom Line

Apollo.io remains the best all-in-one option for outbound sales teams that want database + sequencing + enrichment in a single tool. The 400M contact database is genuinely useful, Professional tier unlocks features that matter, and CRM integrations are solid. For solo SDRs or small teams, the $119/user/mo price is steep, but serious outbound volume justifies it over point solutions.

## FAQ

**How accurate is Apollo.io data?**
Apollo's data accuracy depends on the segment. Email accuracy for mid-market and enterprise contacts at well-known companies tends to be strong, with bounce rates under 4%. SMB contacts and startups see 8-12% bounce rates. Apollo combines third-party providers, crowdsourced verification, and AI enrichment. Best practice: verify exports through NeverBounce or ZeroBounce before large sends.[G2 Apollo reviews 2026](https://www.g2.com/products/apollo-io/reviews)

**Is Apollo.io free?**
Yes, Apollo offers a usable free tier with 120 email credits/month, 10 mobile credits, basic sequences, and limited database access. It's enough to test data quality in your market. Limitations include low credit caps and no intent data, advanced filtering, or AI writing. Most teams upgrade within 10 days of serious use.[Apollo.io pricing](https://apollo.io/pricing)

**How does Apollo compare to ZoomInfo?**
ZoomInfo has a larger database and stronger enterprise features but costs $20,000+ annually. Apollo delivers 85% of ZoomInfo's data quality at 20% of the cost, making it ideal for growth-stage teams. ZoomInfo suits high-volume enterprise prospecting with proven ROI; Apollo Professional works for most teams without six-figure contracts.[Apollo vs ZoomInfo comparison](https://apollo.io/compare/zoominfo)
