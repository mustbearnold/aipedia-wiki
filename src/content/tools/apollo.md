# Apollo.io

Apollo.io is a sales intelligence and engagement platform that combines a 275M+ contact database with outreach automation, intent data, and AI-powered personalization. It's one of the most comprehensive tools in the sales stack, covering prospecting, sequencing, and CRM sync in a single platform. The free tier is generous enough to evaluate the product before committing to a paid plan.

## What It Does

Apollo.io lets sales teams find, enrich, and engage B2B prospects without stitching together five separate tools. You search a massive contact and company database, filter by job title, industry, funding stage, technology used, and dozens of other signals, then launch automated email and phone sequences directly from the platform. The AI layer scores prospects by fit, writes personalized outreach, and surfaces intent data showing which companies are actively researching your category.

## Who It's For

- SDRs and AEs running outbound prospecting at SaaS companies
- Sales ops teams building enriched lead lists for campaigns
- Founders doing sales themselves and needing one tool instead of five
- Agencies running lead generation campaigns for clients
- Recruiters who use the database for candidate sourcing (unofficial but common)

## Key Features

- **275M+ verified contacts** with email addresses, direct dials, and mobile numbers across 70M+ companies
- **AI prospect scoring** ranks leads by fit based on your ICP definition and historical conversion data
- **Automated email sequences** with A/B testing, send-time optimization, and deliverability tracking
- **CRM sync** with Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and others — bidirectional, near real-time
- **Intent data** identifies companies actively researching keywords in your category (Professional+ only)
- **LinkedIn extension** lets you pull contact data and add prospects to sequences directly from LinkedIn profiles
- **AI-written outreach personalization** generates first lines and full emails based on prospect data

## Limitations

- **Data accuracy varies:** Expect 5-15% email bounce rates depending on industry and seniority level. Enterprise contacts tend to be more accurate than SMB.
- **Professional plan is the real product:** The free and Basic plans are limited enough that most serious users end up at $99/user/mo minimum.
- **Expensive for solo operators:** At $99/user/mo for Professional, solo founders often get better ROI from Apollo free + a cheaper enrichment tool.
- **LinkedIn prospecting is restricted:** Apollo's Chrome extension walks a line with LinkedIn's ToS. Aggressive use risks LinkedIn account restrictions.
- **Intent data is US-centric:** The buyer intent signals are significantly weaker for non-US markets.
- **Support quality scales with plan:** Free and Basic users report slow response times and limited troubleshooting help.

## Bottom Line

Apollo.io is the best all-in-one option for outbound sales teams that want database + sequencing + enrichment in a single tool. The 275M contact database is genuinely useful, the Professional tier unlocks the features that matter, and the CRM integrations are solid. For solo SDRs or small teams, the $99/user/mo price is steep — but if you're running serious outbound volume, it replaces three or four point solutions and earns its keep. If you only need a contact database and already have a sending tool, Clay + a cheaper data source may give you better data quality for less.

## Best Alternatives

- **ZoomInfo** — larger database, significantly more expensive, better for enterprise
- **Clay** — enrichment-focused, connects 50+ data sources, no built-in sending
- **Instantly** — sending-focused, unlimited accounts, weaker database
- **Lusha** — simpler, phone number focus, better GDPR compliance for EU
- **Hunter.io** — email finding only, much cheaper, limited to one signal

## FAQ

**How accurate is Apollo.io data?**
Apollo's data accuracy depends heavily on the segment you're prospecting. Email accuracy for mid-market and enterprise contacts at well-known companies tends to be strong, with bounce rates under 5%. SMB contacts and rapidly growing startups see higher bounce rates — 10-15% is common. Apollo uses a combination of third-party data providers, crowdsourced verification, and AI enrichment to keep data fresh, but no B2B database achieves 100% accuracy. Best practice: always run Apollo exports through a dedicated email verification tool (NeverBounce, ZeroBounce) before large sends to protect sender reputation.

**Is Apollo.io free?**
Yes — Apollo has a genuinely usable free tier that includes 50 email credits per month, 5 mobile credits, basic sequence automation, and access to the contact database with limited filters. It's enough to evaluate whether the data quality suits your market before paying. The main limitations are the low monthly credit cap and the absence of intent data, advanced filtering, and AI writing features. Most teams outgrow free within a week of serious prospecting.

**How does Apollo compare to ZoomInfo?**
ZoomInfo has a larger, more frequently refreshed database and stronger enterprise-grade features, but it costs significantly more — often $15,000-$30,000+ per year for meaningful access. Apollo offers around 80% of ZoomInfo's data quality at a fraction of the cost, making it the default choice for growth-stage and mid-market sales teams. ZoomInfo makes sense when you're running high-volume enterprise prospecting and can justify the price on closed deal ROI. For most teams, Apollo Professional gets you there without the six-figure contract.
