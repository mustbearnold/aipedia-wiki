# LOVO (Genny)

LOVO, branded around its flagship product Genny, is an AI voice generation and video production platform built for content creators, e-learning developers, and marketers. It offers a library of 500+ AI voices across 100+ languages, combined with a browser-based video editor and screen recorder — making it one of the few tools that lets you go from script to finished video without leaving the platform.

The key differentiator is the all-in-one workflow. Where [ElevenLabs](../tools/elevenlabs.md) focuses purely on voice quality and API access, LOVO bundles a video editor, royalty-free media library, and AI script writer alongside its TTS engine. The result is a more accessible tool for non-technical creators, at the cost of some voice quality at the top end.

LOVO targets the same audience as tools like [Murf](../tools/murf.md) but leans harder into video production. If you need high-volume API access or the best raw voice quality, look elsewhere. If you need a self-contained workflow for producing voiceover videos — course content, explainers, social ads — LOVO is a strong option.

## What It Does

LOVO generates AI voiceovers from text using a library of 500+ voices, with controls for pacing, emphasis, and pronunciation. The Genny editor lets users assemble videos with the generated voiceover, stock media, and on-screen text in a drag-and-drop interface. A built-in AI writer can generate scripts from prompts. The screen recorder enables product demos and tutorial recordings with AI narration added in post.

## Who It's For

- **E-learning developers** — build narrated course modules without recording studios or voice actors
- **YouTube creators** — produce voiceover videos using the integrated editor and stock media library
- **Marketers** — create product explainers and ad content from a script in one session
- **Corporate trainers** — generate multilingual training content across 100+ languages
- **Small agencies** — produce client video content at scale without hiring voice talent

## Key Features

- **500+ voices** — broad library covering 100+ languages and a wide range of styles and ages
- **Genny video editor** — drag-and-drop timeline editor, stock media library, on-screen text, and transitions built in
- **AI script writer** — generate scripts from a topic prompt before converting to voiceover
- **Voice cloning** — upload 10-30 seconds of audio to clone a voice (Basic plan and above)
- **Screen recorder** — record your screen and layer AI narration on top
- **Pronunciation editor** — custom dictionary for brand names, technical terms, and acronyms
- **Emphasis and pacing controls** — adjust speaking rate, pitch, and emphasis at the word level

## Limitations

- **Voice quality below ElevenLabs** — LOVO's voices are good but don't match the naturalness of [ElevenLabs](../tools/elevenlabs.md) or [Murf](../tools/murf.md) at the top end, particularly for emotional range
- **Limited API access** — LOVO's API is not as well-documented or developer-friendly as ElevenLabs; it's primarily a UI-driven product
- **Video editor is basic** — the built-in editor covers simple use cases but isn't a replacement for a dedicated tool like CapCut or DaVinci Resolve
- **Monthly audio caps** — the Basic plan's 2-hour monthly ceiling is easy to hit for active creators
- **Free plan is restrictive** — 5 downloads per month and watermarked video output make the free tier more of a trial than a usable product

## Bottom Line

LOVO scores 7/10 on utility and value for creators who want an end-to-end voiceover video workflow without juggling multiple tools. The 500-voice library and video editor combination is genuinely useful. It loses points on moat (6/10) because the all-in-one video+voice space is crowded, and on longevity (7/10) because stronger competitors like ElevenLabs are expanding into video. Best for e-learning and explainer video production; not the right choice if raw voice quality or API access is the priority.

## FAQ

**Does LOVO have a free plan?**
Yes. The free plan provides 5 downloads per month and up to 20 minutes of audio generation, but video exports include a watermark. It is sufficient for evaluating voice quality and the editor, but not for production use.

**How many voices does LOVO have?**
LOVO offers 500+ AI voices across 100+ languages as of April 2026. The library includes a range of ages, accents, and styles, including voices optimized for e-learning, advertising, and narration.

**Can LOVO clone my voice?**
Yes, starting from the Basic plan ($24/mo). Voice cloning requires 10-30 seconds of clean audio and produces a usable clone within minutes. The Pro plan includes up to 3 custom voice clones.
