# NeuronWriter

NeuronWriter is an NLP-driven SEO content optimizer from a Polish software company. It scrapes the top 30 SERP results for a keyword, extracts the semantic terms and entities ranking pages use, and scores drafts in real time inside a web editor.

Five subscription tiers: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond. Pricing runs $19 to $97 per month on annual billing.

## System Verdict

> **Pick NeuronWriter if budget is the constraint and content scoring is the job.** Silver at $37/month delivers roughly 90% of Surfer's editor quality at half the price. Bronze works for solo affiliate writers on one or two projects.
>
> **Skip it for anything beyond the content editor.** No native keyword research. No backlink index. No rank tracker on the cheaper tiers. Pair with [Ahrefs](/tools/ahrefs/) or [Semrush](/tools/semrush/) for the upstream work.
>
> **Who pays which tier:** Bronze $19 for freelancers on two projects, Silver $37 for most working SEO writers, Gold $57 for small agencies needing plagiarism and WordPress integration, Platinum $77 and Diamond $97 for larger teams with API access and custom branding.

## What it actually is

A browser-based content editor with a SERP scraper bolted to the front. The scraper pulls the top 30 results for a keyword, extracts NLP terms and entities, and feeds them to a live scoring panel beside the draft.

Scores run 0 to 100. Writers add flagged terms until the score matches top competitors. The AI writer generates outlines and paragraphs anchored to the same entity list, so drafts land closer to the ranking profile than general-purpose LLM output.

The moat is thin. The core scoring engine is commodity NLP applied to SERP scrapes. Competitors replicate the approach. What NeuronWriter does well is ship that approach at the lowest sustainable price point in the category.

## When to pick NeuronWriter

- **Budget forces a sub-$50 tool.** Silver at $37/month undercuts Surfer Essential ($79-$99) by a material margin.
- **You write one or two articles per week.** Bronze's 25 queries handle solo affiliate work cleanly.
- **You already own keyword research upstream.** Pair with Ahrefs or Semrush for topic discovery; NeuronWriter handles the editor work.
- **You run a small agency on consistent scoring.** Silver or Gold scales to dozens of articles per month without per-seat surcharges.
- **You want WordPress publish-in-place.** The Chrome extension writes directly into the WP editor.

## When to pick something else

- **Full SEO suite (keyword + backlinks + rank tracking):** [Semrush](/tools/semrush/) or [Ahrefs](/tools/ahrefs/). NeuronWriter covers content only.
- **Polished editor with deeper SERP analyzer:** [Surfer SEO](/tools/surfer-seo/). Costs more, ships more intelligence beside the score.
- **Topic modeling and content planning at scale:** [MarketMuse](/tools/marketmuse/). Enterprise-tier topic authority mapping NeuronWriter does not attempt.
- **Brief-first workflow for teams:** [Frase](/tools/frase/). Faster from keyword to draft, lighter on granular scoring.

## Failure modes

- **AI drafts still need editing.** SERP-calibrated output beats blank-page GPT, but nothing on-tier ships production-ready copy.
- **SERP analyzer is thinner than Surfer's.** No backlink graph, no in-app competitive intelligence. Entities and word counts only.
- **Query limits bite agencies.** 25 queries on Bronze fills a week. 50 on Silver caps at roughly 12 articles per month with revisions.
- **No native keyword research.** Topic and volume data must come from a separate tool.
- **Currency fluctuation on historical EUR pricing.** Older reviews cite euro figures; the current pricing page lists USD. Budget against the live page, not cached copy.
- **Interface is dense.** The editor packs SERP data, entity lists, scoring, and AI controls on one screen. New users need a few sessions to calibrate.

## FAQ

**How many plans does NeuronWriter have?**
Five: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond. Annual pricing runs $19, $37, $57, $77, and $97 per month respectively ([NeuronWriter pricing](https://neuronwriter.com/pricing-neuron/)).

**How does NeuronWriter compare to Surfer SEO?**
Both analyze SERP content and score writing against top-ranking pages. NeuronWriter Silver at $37/month lands near Surfer Essential at $79/month on core scoring. Surfer ships deeper SERP intelligence, a polished editor, and a better-known brand. For budget-bound users, NeuronWriter hits most of the value.

**Does NeuronWriter include keyword research?**
No. The tool scores content against an existing keyword. Pair with [Ahrefs](/tools/ahrefs/) or [Semrush](/tools/semrush/) for volume, difficulty, and topic discovery.

**Can NeuronWriter publish to WordPress?**
Yes. The Chrome extension writes directly into the WordPress editor and Google Docs. Gold and above add native integrations with Shopify and Google Search Console.

**Is there a free trial?**
Yes. A 7-day Gold trial exposes plagiarism checks, integrations, and team collaboration before billing starts.
