# NeuronWriter

NeuronWriter is an NLP-based content optimization and AI writing tool developed by a Polish software company. It analyzes the top-ranking SERP results for any keyword and generates semantic content recommendations — terms, entities, and structural guidelines — to help writers produce content that matches the ranking profile. Its key differentiator is strong NLP content scoring at a lower price point than [Surfer SEO](../tools/surfer-seo.md), making it particularly popular among budget-conscious SEO professionals and agencies in European markets. As of April 2026, NeuronWriter's plans are priced in euros: €19/month for Bronze (25 queries), €37/month for Silver (75 queries), and €97/month for Gold (unlimited). Note: prices are in euros — the dollar equivalent varies with exchange rates.

## What It Does

NeuronWriter is an SEO content editor that performs semantic SERP analysis for a target keyword, extracts the NLP terms and entities used by top-ranking pages, and presents them as a real-time content scoring framework as you write or edit ([NeuronWriter](https://neuronwriter.com)). The content editor highlights terms to include, tracks word count against the SERP average, and provides a content score (0-100) that correlates with on-page optimization depth. It also includes an AI writing assistant that generates content outlines, paragraph drafts, and full article sections using the NLP terms as guidance — the AI output is anchored to what the SERP already rewards, making it more likely to produce structurally competitive content than general-purpose AI writers.

## Key Features

- **NLP content scoring:** Semantic term analysis identifies the concepts and entities that top-ranking pages use, translated into a checklist and a live content score as you write.
- **SERP competitor analysis:** Breaks down the top 30 ranking pages per keyword — word counts, headings, NLP term usage, and structural patterns.
- **AI writing assistant:** Generates content outlines, introductions, paragraph drafts, and full sections using the NLP data as a constraint. More SEO-calibrated than using ChatGPT directly.
- **Content planner:** Organizes keywords and content assignments across projects. Useful for agencies managing multiple client campaigns.
- **Google Search Console integration:** Connects to GSC to surface optimization opportunities from your existing published content.
- **Internal linking recommendations:** Suggests internal linking opportunities between articles in your content set.
- **Chrome extension:** Access the content editor from within WordPress or Google Docs via the Chrome extension without switching to the NeuronWriter interface.

## Limitations

- **Priced in euros.** For US-based users, the effective cost fluctuates with exchange rates. This is a minor but real planning inconvenience.
- **Query limits on lower plans.** 25 queries on the Bronze plan ($19) fills up quickly for agencies. Silver at €37 (75 queries) is the practical minimum for regular production work.
- **SERP analysis depth is competitive but not best-in-class.** Surfer SEO's SERP Analyzer provides more granular backlink and competitive data alongside content optimization. NeuronWriter focuses on NLP terms and content scoring rather than full competitive intelligence.
- **AI writing quality is functional, not excellent.** The AI-generated content needs editing. It is better than a blank page and more SEO-calibrated than generic AI writers, but it is not production-ready without revision.
- **Smaller English-language community.** Documentation, tutorial content, and community support are thinner than Surfer SEO or Frase. European SEO communities are better served.
- **Interface feels less polished than competitors.** The UI works but lacks the refinement of Surfer SEO's Content Editor.

## Bottom Line

NeuronWriter is the best value AI SEO content optimization tool for writers and small agencies who want solid NLP-based content scoring without paying Surfer SEO's $79/month minimum. At €19-€37/month, it delivers most of what Surfer SEO does for content optimization at roughly half the price. The Gold plan at €97 is competitive with Surfer Scale at $129 for high-volume use. The 30% affiliate commission is strong, making it worth featuring for SEO-focused content creators who review tools. The main limitation is that it is not a full SEO suite — it handles on-page content optimization only, and must be paired with [Ahrefs](../tools/ahrefs.md) or [Semrush](../tools/semrush.md) for keyword research and backlinks.

## FAQ

**Why are NeuronWriter prices in euros?**
NeuronWriter is developed by a Polish company. All prices are listed in euros, which means the effective dollar cost fluctuates with the EUR/USD exchange rate. At most common exchange rates, the plans cost between approximately $20 and $105 USD.

**How does NeuronWriter compare to Surfer SEO?**
Both tools analyze SERP content and score your writing against top-ranking pages using NLP terms. NeuronWriter costs roughly half as much as Surfer SEO and has a comparable content scoring engine. Surfer SEO has a more polished interface, a better-known brand, and deeper competitive SERP analysis. For budget-conscious users, NeuronWriter delivers most of the same value at lower cost.

**Does NeuronWriter have an affiliate program?**
Yes. NeuronWriter offers a 30% commission affiliate program with a 30-day cookie window, paid directly. This is one of the stronger commission rates in the AI SEO tools category.
