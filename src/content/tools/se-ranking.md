# SE Ranking

SE Ranking is an **all-in-one SEO platform**: rank tracking, site audit, backlink checker, competitor research, on-page SEO, and local marketing tools bundled into one subscription. Founded in 2013, it has grown from a single rank tracker into a suite of 30+ tools used by agencies and in-house teams.

The current buyer story is the **AI Search Toolkit**, which tracks brand mentions and citations across Google AI Overviews, AI Mode, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity, and native **MCP (Model Context Protocol) access** included in every plan, connecting the platform's data directly into Claude, Cursor, VS Code, and other AI-assistant tools.

## What it actually is

A single-account SEO suite built around the classic workflow: keyword rank tracking, website audit, backlink checker and monitor, competitor analysis, on-page SEO checker, and local rank tracking for Google Business Profile visibility. Content tools (Content Editor, AI Writer) and reporting round out the agency-facing side.

The differentiator layered on top is the **AI Search Toolkit**, which treats AI-answer visibility as a first-class metric next to traditional rankings. It tracks whether a brand is cited or mentioned across Google's AI Overviews and AI Mode, plus ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity, with competitor comparison and source-level analysis of which domains get cited.

**MCP access** is the other current differentiator: rather than treating AI-assistant integration as a premium add-on, SE Ranking bundles it into every subscription tier, along with seven pre-built Claude Skills that turn MCP calls into finished content briefs, share-of-voice reports, and competitor analyses.

## When to pick SE Ranking

- **You want classic SEO and AI-search visibility in one account.** Rank tracking, backlinks, audits, and AI Overviews/ChatGPT/Gemini/Perplexity tracking share the same dashboard and project setup.
- **You want MCP access without paying extra for it.** Every plan includes MCP, unlike suites that gate AI-assistant integration behind a higher tier or separate API purchase.
- **You run an agency on a tighter entry budget than Semrush or Ahrefs.** Core at $129/mo (or $103.20/mo annual) undercuts Semrush Pro ($139.95/mo) and sits close to Ahrefs Lite pricing while including AI-search tracking out of the box.
- **You need white-label agency reporting.** The Agency Pack add-on covers client seats and white-label reports for a flat $69/mo on annual billing.

## When to pick something else

- **Deepest backlink index and freshness:** [Ahrefs](/tools/ahrefs/). Still the default when link-building depth is the primary job.
- **Broadest multi-channel marketing suite:** [Semrush](/tools/semrush/). Adds PPC, social, and AI PR Toolkit alongside SEO for agencies running more than search.
- **Content optimization as the core product:** [Surfer SEO](/tools/surfer-seo/). Content Editor and scoring are the primary workflow, not a side feature.
- **AI-visibility monitoring as a standalone specialist:** [OmniSEO](/tools/omniseo/). Purpose-built for AI-channel tracking without the full SEO suite around it.
- **Budget semantic content optimization:** [NeuronWriter](/tools/neuronwriter/). Lower entry price if AI visibility is a secondary concern.

## Recent developments

- **AI Search Toolkit expansion.** SE Ranking's AI visibility tracking now covers Google AI Overviews, AI Mode, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity, with a standalone SE Visible dashboard for teams that want AI-answer monitoring separated from the core SEO project workflow.
- **Native MCP rollout.** MCP access shipped as a standard inclusion across all subscription tiers rather than a premium add-on, alongside pre-built Claude Skills for content briefs, share-of-voice reports, audits, and competitor analyses.
- **Tier restructure.** The older Essential/Pro/Business plan structure was retired in late 2025 in favor of the current two-tier Core/Growth self-serve structure plus custom Enterprise.

## System verdict

> **Pick SE Ranking if you want classic SEO and AI-search visibility tracking in one affordable account with MCP access included.** Core at $129/mo (or $103.20/mo annual) undercuts Semrush's Pro floor while bundling AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity tracking, plus MCP access that competitors often gate behind separate purchases.
>
> **Skip it if backlink-index depth is the deciding factor, or if bundled AI content generation at no extra cost matters.** [Ahrefs](/tools/ahrefs/) still leads on link data depth for many teams. AI Writer sits behind a separate add-on whose current price needs confirmation at checkout.
>
> **Who pays which tier:** Core $129/mo for solo SEO pros and small agencies needing 10 projects and baseline AI-search tracking, Growth $279/mo for larger teams needing 30 projects, 3 seats, and deeper AI-prompt allowances, Enterprise custom for agencies needing full API access and custom limits.

## FAQ

<details>
<summary>Is SE Ranking free?</summary>

No. SE Ranking is a paid platform with a 14-day free trial and no permanent free tier. Plans start at $129/month for Core ($103.20/month billed annually).

</details>

<details>
<summary>Does SE Ranking track AI Overviews and ChatGPT visibility?</summary>

Yes. The AI Search Toolkit tracks brand mentions and citations across Google AI Overviews, AI Mode, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity. Core and Growth include a baseline daily AI-prompt allowance, and the AI Search add-on at $89/month expands that volume and unlocks the standalone SE Visible dashboard.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Does SE Ranking include MCP access?</summary>

Yes, on every subscription tier. MCP access connects SE Ranking's keyword, backlink, domain, and AI-search data to Claude, Cursor, VS Code, Windsurf, Codex, Zed, Gemini CLI, and other MCP-compatible AI tools, gated by the plan's API credit allowance rather than sold as a separate feature.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How does SE Ranking compare to Semrush and Ahrefs?</summary>

SE Ranking undercuts Semrush's Pro tier ($129 vs $139.95) while bundling AI-search visibility tracking that Semrush sells through a separate Semrush One or AI Visibility Toolkit path. Ahrefs remains the stronger default for teams whose primary job is backlink depth and freshness. SE Ranking's edge is bundling classic SEO, AI-search tracking, and native MCP access into one mid-priced account.

</details>
