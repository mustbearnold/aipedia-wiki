# Mistral AI

Paris-based AI lab founded in 2023 by former DeepMind and Meta researchers. Ships frontier-class closed models (Mistral Large 3) alongside permissively licensed open weights (Mistral Small 4 under Apache 2.0).

EU-incorporated, EU-datacenter hosted, and governed under French law. For any European organization under GDPR Article 44 or the EU AI Act, Mistral is the default credible alternative to U.S.-based providers.

The March 2026 product cycle was Mistral's busiest ever. Mistral Small 4 (March 16), Forge (March 17), Voxtral TTS (March 23), Leanstral, and Moderation 2603 all shipped within two weeks.

## System Verdict

> **Pick Mistral if EU data sovereignty is non-negotiable or API cost must stay low without sacrificing frontier capability.** Mistral Large 3 ($0.50/$1.50 per M) competes with GPT-5.4 at ~60% lower cost. Mistral Small 4 (Apache 2.0, 256K context) unifies reasoning, vision, and agentic coding in a single openly-licensed model.
>
> **Skip it for the deepest ecosystem.** [ChatGPT](/tools/chatgpt/) has the largest plugin marketplace. [Gemini](/tools/gemini/) wins Google Workspace. [Claude](/tools/claude/) takes long-form reasoning. Mistral has none of those moats. Le Chat Pro at €14.99/mo is cheaper than ChatGPT Plus but the consumer UI is less polished.
>
> **Who pays which tier:** Free for le Chat casual use, Pro €14.99/mo for individuals wanting unlimited Large 3 access and image generation, la Plateforme API for developers at published per-token rates. Forge is contact-sales for enterprise fine-tuning.

## What it actually is

Two product surfaces on one model family. la Plateforme is the commercial API for developers. le Chat is the consumer product at [chat.mistral.ai](https://chat.mistral.ai).

Small 4 (March 16, 2026) merges three prior specialized models into a single Apache 2.0 release: Magistral for reasoning, Pixtral for multimodal vision, Devstral for agentic coding. 256K context, 128-expert Mixture-of-Experts, 40% lower latency and 3x higher throughput than Small 3.

Large 3 remains the closed frontier-tier model for workloads where Small 4's capability ceiling is hit. Codestral handles dedicated code workloads. Voxtral TTS is an open-weights multilingual speech model (CC BY-NC 4.0, not permissive enough for most commercial products).

The moat is jurisdiction, not raw capability. EU-incorporated, EU-datacenter, French-law-governed. That pairing matters when GDPR Article 44 third-country transfer rules kick in, which they do for almost every European enterprise workload above a minimal threshold.

## When to pick Mistral

- **EU enterprise with GDPR or EU AI Act requirements.** EU-incorporated, EU-datacenter, French-law jurisdiction. No other frontier-class provider matches this posture.
- **Low-cost API with open-weight escape.** Small 4 API is $0.15/M input; the same model is free to self-host under Apache 2.0. You can move the workload in-house if pricing changes.
- **Multimodal reasoning at commodity cost.** Small 4 handles text + vision + coding at $0.15/M input, below DeepSeek V3.2's $0.28/M.
- **OpenAI-compatible migration path.** la Plateforme ships an OpenAI-API-compatible endpoint. Existing SDK integrations swap in with a base-URL change.
- **Multilingual TTS for internal tools.** Voxtral TTS is open-weight and multilingual. CC BY-NC 4.0 limits commercial use but internal tools are clear.

## When to pick something else

- **Largest ecosystem and plugin marketplace:** [ChatGPT](/tools/chatgpt/). GPT Store has no Mistral equivalent.
- **Google Workspace integration:** [Gemini](/tools/gemini/). Native Docs / Sheets / Gmail hooks.
- **Strongest long-form reasoning and writing:** [Claude](/tools/claude/). Opus 4.7 beats Large 3 on most reasoning benchmarks.
- **Cheapest per-token API with open weights:** [DeepSeek](/tools/deepseek/) V3.2 at $0.28/M input for text-only workloads where vision is not needed.
- **Video generation:** Mistral has none. [Veo 3](/tools/veo/) via Gemini or [Kling](/tools/kling/).
- **Commercial TTS with permissive license:** Voxtral is CC BY-NC 4.0. For commercial products use [ElevenLabs](/tools/elevenlabs/) or [OpenAI TTS](/tools/chatgpt/).

## Failure modes

- **Output-token pricing for Small 4 not fully public.** $0.15/M input is confirmed; output rate varies by source. Verify at [docs.mistral.ai](https://docs.mistral.ai/deployment/ai-studio/pricing) before budgeting.
- **Voxtral license is CC BY-NC 4.0.** Non-commercial only. Using it in a commercial product (even internally) requires a commercial license from Mistral.
- **Smaller plugin ecosystem.** la Plateforme is OpenAI-compatible, so most tools work, but Mistral-first integrations are thin compared to ChatGPT or Claude.
- **le Chat UX trails competitors.** Functional but less polished than ChatGPT, Claude.ai, or Gemini web. Code interpreter, canvas, and GPT-Store-equivalent features are limited or absent.
- **Multimodal vision is Small 4 only.** Large 3 is text-only. For image input workloads, Small 4 is the required model regardless of whether its capability ceiling fits.
- **Mistral Medium 3.1 status unclear.** No 2026 release notes confirmed. Assume superseded by the Small 4 / Large 3 pair.
- **Smaller capital base than U.S. frontier labs.** ~$1B raised vs tens of billions at OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google DeepMind. Compute ceiling is a real constraint on frontier model cadence.
- **No native video generation.** For multimodal output including video, workflow needs a second tool.

## FAQ

**Is Mistral free?**
Yes, in two ways. The open-weight models (Mistral 7B, Mixtral 8x7B, 8x22B, Small 4) are free to self-host under permissive licenses. Small 4 specifically is Apache 2.0 and permits commercial use. [chat.mistral.ai](https://chat.mistral.ai) also has a free consumer tier.

**What is Mistral Small 4?**
A single Apache 2.0 model released March 16, 2026 that merges reasoning (Magistral), multimodal vision (Pixtral), and agentic coding (Devstral). 256K context, 128-expert Mixture-of-Experts, 40% faster latency and 3x higher throughput than Small 3. Available via API, HuggingFace, AI Studio, and NVIDIA NIM/NeMo.

**How does Mistral compare to GPT-5.4?**
Mistral Large 3 is broadly competitive on text benchmarks at roughly 60% lower API cost (~$0.50/M vs ~$2.50/M input). GPT-5.4 wins on ecosystem, image generation, and multimodal breadth. For EU-regulated workloads Mistral is the default pick regardless of benchmark numbers because GDPR Article 44 third-country transfer rules apply.

**Why choose Mistral over OpenAI or Anthropic?**
Three concrete reasons: lower per-token API cost, EU-native data residency for GDPR compliance, and open-weight self-hosting fallback via Apache 2.0 Small 4. U.S. developers without compliance constraints should weigh those against OpenAI's ecosystem or Anthropic's reasoning lead.

**What is Forge?**
Mistral's enterprise fine-tuning platform launched March 17, 2026. Enables custom-model training on proprietary data under a license-fee model. Contact Mistral sales for specifics.

**Is Voxtral safe for commercial products?**
Not under the default license. CC BY-NC 4.0 restricts commercial use. Internal tools are clear; consumer products require a paid commercial license from Mistral.
