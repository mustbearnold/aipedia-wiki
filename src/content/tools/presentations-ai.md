# Presentations.AI

Presentations.AI is an AI presentation platform for business decks. It turns prompts, documents, PDFs, URLs, data, and existing presentations into editable decks, then adds brand controls, PowerPoint/PDF export, analytics, and enterprise paths for API or large-volume generation.

The important June 2026 correction: Presentations.AI should not be described as the old Public Beta annual bargain anymore. The current English pricing page lists Starter at $0, Pro at $20/month billed annually, and Gold at $100/month billed annually. Regional and localized pages may lag or vary, so quote the live checkout when buying.

## System Verdict

> **Pick Presentations.AI if you need business-ready AI decks with PowerPoint export and brand controls.** Pro is the practical buyer tier because it includes 5,000 credits, high-quality PowerPoint export, presentation tracking and analytics, and brand customization.
>
> **Skip it if you need an interactive presenter funnel.** [Decktopus AI](/tools/decktopus/) is stronger for forms, voice recording, webhooks, custom domains, lead capture, and training/sales delivery. [Pitch](/tools/pitch/) is stronger when team collaboration and deck operations matter more than AI generation.
>
> **Who pays which tier:** Starter for free testing, Pro for individual business presenters, Gold for higher-stakes presentation quality and shared-knowledge workflows, and Enterprise when API, data integrations, SSO, audit logging, private deployment, or white-label generation matter.

## What It Actually Is

Presentations.AI is a business presentation generator, not a generic slide toy. The official site says users can turn ideas, documents, and data into presentation-ready decks, apply brand styling, and export editable PowerPoint files. The platform also positions itself around refresh, scale, API/webhook generation, data-connected updates, and white-label use cases.

The moat is not model exclusivity. The moat is workflow: source material in, structured business deck out, with brand sync, PowerPoint compatibility, analytics, and enterprise controls around it. That makes Presentations.AI most interesting for teams that produce recurring sales decks, QBRs, board updates, client proposals, investor updates, and executive reviews.

The watch-out is that the product can look more complete than the argument is. Presentations.AI's own FAQ says important facts, numbers, and claims should be reviewed before external sharing. Treat generated decks as strong drafts, not as verified analysis.

## When To Pick Presentations.AI

- **You need editable PowerPoint output.** Pro explicitly includes high-quality PowerPoint export.
- **You present from source material.** The site supports prompts, PDFs, documents, URLs, existing presentations, research reports, meeting notes, and business plans.
- **Brand consistency matters.** Pro includes brand customization; Gold adds advanced brand customization and custom templates.
- **You want analytics.** The pricing table lists views and slide engagement analytics.
- **You need higher AI usage.** Gold's 50,000-credit bucket is materially larger than Starter or Pro.
- **You are evaluating enterprise generation.** Enterprise positioning includes API, white-label, SSO, data integrations, audit logging, and large-volume generation.

## When To Pick Something Else

- **Interactive presenter funnels:** [Decktopus AI](/tools/decktopus/) is stronger for forms, voice recording, embeds, custom domains, webhooks, and lead/response capture.
- **Team deck collaboration:** [Pitch](/tools/pitch/) is stronger for roles, guests, comments, pitch rooms, version history, and collaborative deck operations.
- **Broad deck/doc/web publishing:** [Gamma](/tools/gamma/) is still the safer default when one idea needs to become a deck, doc, website, and shareable page.
- **Layout discipline:** [Beautiful.ai](/tools/beautiful-ai/) is stronger when the story is already known and the main problem is governed business-slide polish.
- **Visual inserts:** [Napkin AI](/tools/napkin-ai/) is better when the bottleneck is diagrams and slide-ready visuals rather than a full deck.

## Failure Modes

- **Stale pricing confusion.** Some older or localized pages still surface Public Beta language. Use the current English pricing page and live checkout for buyer guidance.
- **Generated decks need fact review.** Important claims, numbers, charts, and sourcing need human verification before client or board use.
- **Starter is only a trial path.** 100 credits and up to 20 slides are enough to test style, not to run a regular deck workflow.
- **Gold can be expensive for occasional decks.** $100/month billed annually only makes sense when quality, credits, shared knowledge, and brand controls matter.
- **Collaboration is not Pitch-level.** Presentations.AI can support sharing and guests, but it is not the deepest team editing workspace.
- **Interactive delivery is not Decktopus-level.** If the deck needs forms, Q&A, webhooks, voice recording, and response capture, use Decktopus instead.
