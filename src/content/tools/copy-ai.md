# Copy.ai

Copy.ai is an AI writing and go-to-market (GTM) automation platform built by Copy.ai Inc. (San Francisco, founded 2020). It started as a template-based copywriting tool and now focuses on sales and marketing workflows that connect AI generation to CRM data and outreach sequences. The free trial offers limited access; Pro starts at $49/month, Team at $249/month where workflow features activate ([Copy.ai](https://copy.ai)).

## What It Does

Copy.ai generates sales emails, ad copy, social posts, and blog content using over 100 templates and custom prompts. Users input details like product info or audience; the tool outputs formatted copy ready for use.

On Pro and higher plans, it builds workflows that automate multi-step tasks: pull prospect data from HubSpot or Salesforce, generate personalized emails or LinkedIn messages, and send for approval or directly to tools like Outreach. Integrations handle data flow without manual steps ([Copy.ai Pricing](https://copy.ai/pricing)).

Generation uses GPT-5.4, Claude Opus 4.6, and Gemini 3.1 Pro; the platform routes prompts to the best model per task. Brand voice training applies consistent style across outputs ([Copy.ai Blog](https://www.copy.ai/blog)).

## Who It's For

- **Sales teams:** CRM-integrated workflows for personalized outreach; automates email and LinkedIn sequences from prospect lists
- **Marketing teams:** batch generation of ads, emails, and social content; templates for Google Ads, Meta, and LinkedIn formats
- **Growth agencies:** client workspaces with usage limits per brand; team permissions for collaborative editing
- **Content teams:** long-form blogs and landing pages with SEO structure; voice-trained outputs for brand consistency
- **Small businesses:** Pro plan for unlimited generation without per-word costs

## Pricing

Copy.ai offers four plans from free trial to custom enterprise. Pro at $49/month unlocks unlimited words; Team at $249/month adds workflows and five seats. Enterprise includes custom integrations and support ([Copy.ai Pricing](https://copy.ai/pricing)).

## Key Features

- **100+ templates:** Formats for emails, ads (Google, Meta, LinkedIn), product pages, and blog posts; fills from bullet-point inputs ([Copy.ai](https://copy.ai))
- **Workflow builder:** Drag-and-drop automation chains generation with CRM actions; example: enrich lead → write email → log in Salesforce
- **CRM integrations:** Native connections to HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive; Zapier for 6,000+ apps
- **Brand voice:** Train on 10-50 examples; applies tone across all generations account-wide
- **Multi-model routing:** Uses GPT-5.4 for creative copy, Claude Opus 4.6 for analysis, Gemini 3.1 Pro for factual content
- **Team workspaces:** Role-based access, content approval flows, usage analytics per user
- **Multilingual support:** 25+ languages with native templates for non-English markets

## Limitations

- **Workflows require Team plan:** Core automation sits behind $249/month tier; Pro lacks CRM chaining, limits to single generations
- **Templates constrain creativity:** Structured formats speed routine copy but rigid for custom needs; advanced users prefer raw GPT-5.4 prompts
- **No native research:** Lacks web search or data enrichment; prospect research needs external tools like Apollo or Clay first
- **Model access trails direct APIs:** Uses slightly older model versions than ChatGPT Teams or Anthropic API; adds convenience layer at higher cost
- **Small team pricing jumps:** Team plan assumes five users; two-person teams overpay for unused seats

## Bottom Line

Copy.ai suits sales and marketing teams on Team plan ($249/month) where workflow automation saves hours on personalized outreach. The template system and brand voice add efficiency for routine copy. Solo writers or small teams find better value in ChatGPT Plus ($20/month) for similar generation quality.

## Best Alternatives

- **[Jasper](./jasper.md):** Template-focused for marketing; stronger long-form and SEO features
- **ChatGPT Teams ($25/user/month):** Direct GPT-5.4 access; custom GPTs replicate most Copy.ai templates
- **[Clay](./clay.md):** Sales automation with better data enrichment; pairs with Copy.ai for full GTM stack

## FAQ

**Does Copy.ai have a free plan?**  
The free trial lasts two weeks with template access and limited word count. No credit card needed. Pro ($49/month) required for unlimited use ([Copy.ai Pricing](https://copy.ai/pricing)).

**What models power Copy.ai?**  
GPT-5.4, Claude Opus 4.6, and Gemini 3.1 Pro. The platform selects per task; outputs match direct model quality with added formatting ([Copy.ai Blog](https://www.copy.ai/blog/ai-models)).

**Is Team plan worth $249/month?**  
Yes for sales teams using workflows (3+ hours/week saved on outreach). Skip for content-only; Pro or ChatGPT covers generation needs.
