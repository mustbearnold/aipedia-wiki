# Fireflies.ai

Fireflies.ai is an AI meeting assistant that records, transcribes, and summarizes meetings across Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, and Webex. Its defining feature beyond basic transcription is AskFred — a search interface that lets you query your entire meeting history in natural language ("what did we decide about the Q3 budget?"). Native CRM integrations with Salesforce and HubSpot make Fireflies a natural choice for sales teams who want meeting notes pushed directly into their deal pipeline without manual data entry.

## What It Does

Fred, the Fireflies bot, joins meetings automatically via calendar sync and begins recording and transcribing in real time. After each meeting, Fireflies generates an AI summary with action items, decisions, and key topics. The transcript and recording are stored in the Fireflies workspace, indexed for full-text and semantic search. AskFred lets users ask questions across their entire meeting library rather than scrolling through individual transcripts. Conversation intelligence features surface speaker talk-time ratios, sentiment trends, and topic frequency across meetings over time.

## Who It's For

- **Sales teams** who need meeting notes synced to Salesforce or HubSpot automatically
- **Revenue operations teams** tracking conversation patterns across hundreds of sales calls
- **Engineering and product teams** who want a searchable record of decisions across sprints and planning cycles
- **Anyone who runs recurring meetings** and wants to quickly answer "wait, what did we agree on last time?"
- **Remote teams** where async sharing of meeting summaries is a core workflow

## Key Features

- **Auto-joins all platforms** — Zoom, Teams, Meet, Webex via calendar sync; no manual setup per meeting
- **AI summaries** — action items, decisions, and key topics extracted automatically post-meeting
- **AskFred** — natural language search across your entire meeting library; ask questions, get cited answers
- **Speaker diarization** — labels each speaker's turns in the transcript
- **Conversation intelligence** — talk-time percentages, sentiment analysis, topic tracking across meetings
- **CRM integration** — push notes and summaries to Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive automatically
- **Channels** — shared meeting libraries for teams or projects, allowing collaborative review
- **API access** — retrieve transcripts and meeting data programmatically (Pro and above)

## Limitations

- **Free storage cap of 800 minutes** (~13 hours) fills quickly for anyone in several meetings per week; old recordings must be manually deleted to free space
- **$18/seat/month** is expensive for full-team deployment compared to Fathom, which is free
- **AI summaries miss nuance** with heavy accents, fast speech, or poor audio — the transcript quality ceiling is the summary quality ceiling
- **Meeting bot visibility** — Fred joining as a named bot participant can be awkward in external client calls or sensitive discussions
- **No video recording on Pro** — Business plan required at $29/seat; this is a notable feature gap relative to Otter.ai Business
- **Conversation intelligence metrics** are interesting but lack benchmarks — knowing your talk time is 65% is only useful if you know what good looks like

## FAQ

**Is Fireflies.ai free?**
Yes. The free plan provides unlimited transcription up to an 800-minute storage cap (approximately 13 hours of total recorded meetings). Basic AI summaries and limited AskFred queries are included. The storage cap — not a monthly usage limit — means you can keep transcribing as long as you stay under 800 minutes total stored, or delete old recordings to free space. For most occasional users, the free plan is sufficient.

**How does Fireflies compare to Otter.ai?**
Both tools auto-join meetings and produce AI summaries at similar accuracy levels. Fireflies has a stronger edge for sales teams via CRM integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot) and the AskFred cross-meeting search. Otter.ai has a stronger free tier (300 min/month vs 800 min total), better mobile app for in-person transcription, and a longer product history. If CRM sync and conversation intelligence matter to you, choose Fireflies. If you need a general-purpose meeting transcription tool, either works.

**Can Fireflies search across past meetings?**
Yes — this is Fireflies' primary differentiator. AskFred indexes all stored transcripts and lets you ask natural language questions like "what features did the customer ask about?" or "what did we decide about the API timeline?" It returns cited excerpts from specific meetings. The quality of answers depends on transcript accuracy and storage — meetings on the free plan that exceed the 800-minute cap are not searchable unless the plan is upgraded.
