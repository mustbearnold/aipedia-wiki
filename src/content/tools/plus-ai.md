# Plus AI

Plus Docs, Inc.'s **native AI add-in** for Google Slides and Microsoft PowerPoint. There is no separate Plus AI editor; the product installs from the Google Workspace Marketplace or Microsoft AppSource and runs inside the deck you already have open, generating slides from a prompt or an uploaded PDF, Word doc, or text file.

The company was formerly branded **PlusDocs** and now operates as **Plus Docs, Inc.** at plusai.com. It is unrelated to the autonomous-trucking company also called "PlusAI" or "Plus" (a separate, much larger business); buyers searching for pricing should confirm they are on plusai.com.

Founded in 2021 and backed by roughly $8M from Madrona Venture Group, Positive Sum, BoxGroup, and angel investors from Plaid, Zapier, Smartsheet, Auth0, and Asana, Plus AI has over 1 million installs and a 4.6-star Google Workspace Marketplace rating.

## System Verdict

> **Pick Plus AI if your organization is committed to Google Slides or PowerPoint and wants AI drafting without leaving that editor.** Files stay in Google Drive or OneDrive with native collaboration, version history, and permissions intact. No export/import round-trip, because there is nothing to export from.
>
> **Skip it if you want a standalone generator or need heavy chart work.** [Gamma](/tools/gamma/) and [Beautiful.ai](/tools/beautiful-ai/) build decks (and in Gamma's case, docs and web pages) from scratch in their own editor with stronger design automation. Plus AI does not support waterfall, Mekko, or Gantt charts, which rules it out for chart-first consulting and finance work.
>
> **Who pays which tier:** Basic $10/mo for light, occasional generation; Pro $20/mo once you need document uploads, longer prompts, and AI images (the plan most buyers land on); Team $30/mo when a team needs shared branding and templates; Max $200/mo only for heavy, credit-unconstrained daily use; Enterprise for SSO and white-glove rollout.

## What it actually is

Plus AI installs as an add-on inside an existing Google Slides file or as a PowerPoint add-in inside the desktop or web app. There is no separate canvas or file format. Users describe a presentation in a sentence or two, pick a preset, or upload a PDF, Word doc, or text file, and Plus AI generates slides directly in the host document.

Beyond initial generation, the tool handles slide-level edits: rewriting text, remixing a slide into a different layout with one click, inserting single AI-generated slides into an existing deck, and generating AI images. Team and higher tiers add custom branding (logo, fonts, colors) so generated output matches a company's existing template rather than a generic theme.

A companion "Plus AI for Google Docs" extends the same generation and editing model to documents, and "AI Presentation Narrator" is a separate, smaller Plus AI product listed on the Google Workspace Marketplace.

## When to pick Plus AI

- **You will not leave Google Slides or PowerPoint.** No new file format, no export step, no separate login for collaborators. Everything stays in Drive or OneDrive.
- **You need document-to-deck conversion for standard business content.** Upload a PDF or Word doc and get a draft deck, though embedded images and charts in the source do not carry over automatically.
- **A team needs consistent branded output.** Team tier locks in logo, fonts, and colors so every generated deck matches the company template without manual cleanup.
- **You want incremental AI help, not a full rebuild.** Remix, rewrite, and single-slide insertion fit editing an existing deck rather than starting from zero.

## When to pick something else

- **Chart-heavy consulting or finance decks:** neither Plus AI nor most AI generators natively build waterfall, Mekko, or Gantt charts; specialist tools or manual chart work are still required.
- **Standalone prompt-to-deck speed with a shareable web output:** [Gamma](/tools/gamma/) generates decks, docs, and public web pages from one prompt in its own editor.
- **Design-forward automation with cleaner PPTX export:** [Beautiful.ai](/tools/beautiful-ai/) leans harder into design rules and layout automation.
- **Brand-kit control across a large non-designer org:** [Canva](/tools/canva/) has the deeper brand-kit and asset-library tooling.
- **A dedicated slide-quality narrator or rapid outline tool:** compare against other entries in [AI Presentation Tools](/categories/ai-presentation/) before committing to an add-in model.

## Pricing

Verified live on [plusai.com/pricing](https://plusai.com/pricing/) on July 2, 2026. Pricing is per user, billed monthly or annually; annual billing is materially cheaper per seat.

| Plan | Annual (per user/mo) | Monthly (per user/mo) | Credits/month | Who it's for |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | $10 | $15 | 1,500 | Light, occasional slide generation in Slides or PowerPoint |
| Pro | $20 | $25 | 3,000 | **Most users land here**, document uploads, 100K+ character prompts, AI image generation |
| Team | $30 | $40 | 6,000 | Teams needing shared branding, presets, and templates |
| Max | $200 | $240 | Unlimited | Heavy daily use with priority email support |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Custom | SSO, advanced templates, asset libraries, white-glove onboarding |

A **7-day free trial** includes 1,000 AI credits and access to both the Google Slides and PowerPoint add-ins, but requires a credit card up front; the company states it does not issue refunds on subscription payments. Credits are tied to the individual user, not pooled across a team, and reset on the billing date. A typical AI task (generation, remix, or edit) consumes roughly 40 to 170 credits depending on complexity.

## System verdict

Plus AI's whole pitch rests on staying inside the editor you already use. That is a real advantage for IT-governed Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 environments where a new standalone app means a new procurement and security review. The tradeoff is that Plus AI inherits the ceiling of the host app: it cannot build chart types Slides and PowerPoint do not natively support, and per-user, non-pooled credits mean a single heavy user on a low tier can hit a wall mid-project while teammates on the same plan have headroom to spare. For teams that have already decided they are not leaving Slides or PowerPoint, Plus AI is a reasonable default. For teams still choosing an editor, the standalone generators are worth a side-by-side trial first.

## FAQ

<details>
<summary>Is Plus AI a standalone app?</summary>
No. Plus AI is a native add-in that installs into Google Slides (via Google Workspace Marketplace) or Microsoft PowerPoint (via Microsoft AppSource) and generates and edits slides inside that existing document. There is no separate Plus AI editor or file format.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Is plus.ai the same company as plusai.com?</summary>
No. The presentation add-in covered on this page is Plus Docs, Inc. at plusai.com, formerly branded PlusDocs. There is a separate, unrelated autonomous-trucking company that has also used the "Plus" or "PlusAI" name. Confirm you are on plusai.com before purchasing.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How much does Plus AI cost?</summary>
Annual per-user pricing verified July 2, 2026: Basic $10/month (1,500 credits), Pro $20/month (3,000 credits), Team $30/month (6,000 credits), Max $200/month (unlimited credits). Monthly billing is higher across every tier. Enterprise is custom-quoted.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Does Plus AI work in PowerPoint or only Google Slides?</summary>
Both. Plus AI for Google Slides launched first; Plus AI for PowerPoint launched as a separate add-in in July 2024. Third-party reviews note the PowerPoint version has historically felt less responsive than the Google Slides version.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Is there a free trial?</summary>
Yes, 7 days with 1,000 AI credits and access to both the Google Slides and PowerPoint add-ins. A credit card is required to start the trial, and the company states subscription payments are not refunded.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can Plus AI convert a PDF or Word doc into a deck?</summary>
Yes on Pro and higher tiers. Conversion pulls text content into slides; embedded images, charts, and diagrams in the source document are not automatically carried over and must be added manually.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Does Plus AI support advanced charts like waterfall or Gantt?</summary>
No. Plus AI supports basic chart types but does not natively build waterfall, Mekko, or Gantt charts, which limits it for consulting and finance-heavy decks.
</details>
