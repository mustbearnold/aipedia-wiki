# Writesonic

Writesonic is now a GEO-first stack. The 2026 lineup centres on the AI Search Visibility tracker (monitoring ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews answers about a brand), the Action Center (on-page and off-page fixes for AI search rankings), AI article generation, and site audits. Chatsonic remains the conversational research surface, Botsonic remains the chatbot builder, but the marketing story is GEO, not template-based copywriting.

Pricing now runs $79 to $399/mo plus custom Enterprise. The old free tier and the $1,499 enterprise cap are both gone.

## Recent changes

- **2026-06-04:** Reverified the four-tier pricing structure (Starter $79, Basic $199, Growth $399, Enterprise custom) and the GEO-first feature gating. The legacy six-tier ladder (Free / Lite / Standard / Professional / Advanced / Enterprise) remains retired. Annual billing saves 20%.

## System Verdict

> **Pick Writesonic if AI search visibility tracking, the Action Center, and AI article generation all need to live in one dashboard.** The Growth plan at $399/mo is the most popular tier and unlocks the Action Center trial plus sentiment analysis across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews; Enterprise opens the full 10-platform tracker, SOC 2, HIPAA, and GDPR.
>
> **Skip it if a single frontier model already covers the job.** Direct [ChatGPT](/tools/chatgpt/) Plus or [Claude](/tools/claude/) Pro at $20/month beats Writesonic on raw drafting quality and costs a small fraction. For per-article SEO work, [Frase](/tools/frase/) is cheaper, and for site-wide strategy, [MarketMuse](/tools/marketmuse/) goes deeper. For competitor keyword research, [Ahrefs](/tools/ahrefs/) or [Semrush](/tools/semrush/) remain the category default. For pure GEO at lower entry cost, evaluate [Profound](https://www.tryprofound.com/) and Semrush AI Search.
>
> **Who pays which tier:** Starter $79/mo for marketers running a single ChatGPT visibility track, Basic $199/mo when Gemini and Google AI Overviews coverage matters, Growth $399/mo when the Action Center plus sentiment analysis is the reason to buy, Enterprise when the full 10-platform tracker and compliance posture are non-negotiable.

## What it actually is

A GEO and AI-search-visibility platform that wraps third-party frontier models. No proprietary base model. The 2026 rebuild centres on tracking how brands surface in LLM answers and pushing Action Center fixes back into the rankings loop.

The AI Search Visibility tracker monitors prompts and answers across ChatGPT, Gemini, Google AI Overviews, and (on Enterprise) seven more AI platforms, then attributes movement to specific content and links. The Action Center is the operational layer: on-page suggestions (schema, headings, citations) and off-page outreach to fix what the tracker surfaces. Site audits, AI article generation, Chatsonic, and Botsonic sit alongside.

The moats remain narrow. Writesonic owns no frontier model. What it does own is a GEO-first integrated dashboard with a real Action Center, which most general SEO platforms have not shipped at the same depth.

## When to pick Writesonic

- **AI search visibility tracking is the job to be done.** Starter at $79/mo tracks ChatGPT, Basic at $199/mo adds Gemini and Google AI Overviews. Few competitors carry that breadth at that price.
- **Action Center workflow needed.** Growth at $399/mo unlocks the trial; Enterprise opens the full operational layer for on-page and off-page fixes.
- **Mid-market marketing team.** Dashboards, not API keys. The whole content team can run it without engineering help.
- **Need a chatbot builder attached.** Botsonic on the same subscription removes the overhead of a separate FAQ-bot deployment.
- **Compliance-sensitive buyer.** Enterprise carries SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA, and GDPR, which most cheap GEO tools do not.

## When to pick something else

- **Raw writing quality at the lowest price:** [ChatGPT](/tools/chatgpt/) Plus or [Claude](/tools/claude/) Pro at $20/month. Direct frontier access beats routed third-party for most drafts.
- **Pure GEO at a lower entry price:** [Profound](https://www.tryprofound.com/) and Semrush AI Search compete on price for visibility tracking only.
- **Single-article SEO optimization:** [Frase](/tools/frase/) handles brief-to-optimized-article at a fraction of Writesonic's Growth tier.
- **Site-wide topical authority:** [MarketMuse](/tools/marketmuse/) models cluster-level coverage Writesonic does not touch.
- **SERP-grounded on-page scoring:** [Surfer SEO](/tools/surfer-seo/) is the on-page specialist.
- **Keyword and backlink research:** [Ahrefs](/tools/ahrefs/) or [Semrush](/tools/semrush/). Writesonic's traditional SEO data layer is thinner.

## Failure modes

- **Entry price tripled.** Starter is now $79/mo and only tracks ChatGPT. The old $0 free tier and $20-tier drafting plans are gone.
- **Action Center gates at $399/mo.** Buyers expecting the headline feature on a cheap tier hit a hard wall.
- **No proprietary model.** Writesonic routes OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google APIs. Output ceiling tracks the underlying call.
- **Article output needs editing.** Factual accuracy drops on niche topics. Every draft needs human review before publishing.
- **Tracker breadth gated at Enterprise.** Full 10-platform coverage (TikTok AI, Perplexity, You.com, Copilot, etc.) only opens on a custom contract.
- **Helpful-content risk.** AI-heavy output without human editing still trips Google's helpful-content signals. AI Article Writer does not fix that; it accelerates drafts.
- **Workflow caps are aggressive.** Agentic workflows are trial-run capped on every tier below Enterprise (10, 50, then full).

## FAQ

**Is there still a free Writesonic plan?**
No. As of 2026-06-04 the lowest published tier is Starter at $79/mo. Annual billing saves 20% ([Writesonic pricing](https://writesonic.com/pricing)).

**Which models power Chatsonic and the AI Article Writer?**
Routed third-party frontier APIs from OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google. No proprietary base model. Model quality tracks whatever underlying API handles the call on the day.

**When does the Action Center unlock?**
A trial (5 off-page plus 5 on-page actions per month) starts on Growth at $399/mo. The full Action Center is Enterprise-only.

**Which AI platforms does Writesonic track?**
Starter tracks ChatGPT only. Basic and Growth add Gemini and Google AI Overviews. Enterprise covers all 10 supported platforms.

**Writesonic or ChatGPT for content?**
Writesonic wins on AI search visibility tracking and the Action Center. [ChatGPT](/tools/chatgpt/) Teams wins on raw model quality and lower seat cost. Teams optimizing for GEO usually pick Writesonic; teams focused purely on drafting pick ChatGPT.
