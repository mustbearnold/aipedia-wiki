# Frase.io

Frase.io is an AI-powered SEO content platform developed by Frase Inc. It combines SERP research, automated content brief generation, and AI writing into a single workflow, and is primarily used by content teams and SEO agencies who need to move quickly from keyword to publishable article without losing optimization rigor. Its key differentiator is the speed of brief creation — Frase can generate a research-backed content brief from a keyword in under a minute, aggregating data from the top SERP results into a structured template a writer can follow immediately. As of April 2026, Frase offers plans from $14.99/month (Solo, 4 articles) to $44.99/month (Basic, 30 articles) to $114.99/month (Team, unlimited). This is one of the most accessible price points in AI SEO tools.

## What It Does

Frase.io is a content research and optimization platform that crawls the top-ranking pages for any target keyword, extracts the topics, headings, statistics, and FAQs those pages cover, and assembles them into a structured content brief — then lets you write and optimize your own article in an integrated editor that scores your content as you work ([Frase.io](https://frase.io)). The brief generation feature is the strongest part of the product: a one-click analysis of a keyword produces a document showing what all the top-10 results cover, the average word count and structure, the most-cited sources, and the questions people ask related to the topic. Writers can draft directly inside Frase using the brief as a guide, with the AI writing assistant available for generating sections or entire drafts from the research data.

## Who It's For

- **Content managers and editors** who need to produce consistent, research-backed briefs for writers quickly
- **SEO agencies** managing content production across multiple clients who need a scalable brief workflow
- **Freelance writers** who want to produce better-optimized content without spending hours on manual SERP research
- **Solo content operators** (bloggers, affiliate marketers) who want an affordable content optimization tool with AI writing included
- **SaaS and B2B content teams** building topical authority through structured, research-driven content programs

## Key Features

- **Automated content briefs:** One-click SERP analysis generates a structured brief with headings, topic coverage gaps, key questions, competitor word counts, and recommended sources. The core value proposition.
- **Content optimization scoring:** Real-time topic coverage score as you write, comparing your article's topical depth against the top-ranking competitors for your keyword.
- **AI writing assistant:** Generates paragraphs, headings, and full sections from your brief. Output is anchored to the SERP research rather than generic AI training data.
- **SERP data visualization:** Side-by-side view of what each competitor covers, making it easy to identify content gaps or opportunities to differentiate.
- **Answer engine optimization:** Frase identifies the questions people ask related to a topic (from Google's People Also Ask and related searches) and formats them for FAQ sections and featured snippet targeting.
- **Team collaboration:** The Team plan supports multiple users working on the same documents and brief library.
- **Integrations:** Connects to Google Search Console for importing keyword data, and exports to Google Docs and other writing tools.

## Limitations

- **Article limits on lower plans are restrictive.** 4 articles/month on the Solo plan and 30/month on Basic force agencies to upgrade to Team quickly. The jump from $44.99 to $114.99 is steep.
- **Content scoring depth is less granular than Surfer SEO.** Frase's optimization scoring is solid but does not match the keyword density analysis and NLP entity tracking depth of [Surfer SEO](../tools/surfer-seo.md).
- **AI writing quality requires editing.** Like all AI writing tools in this category, Frase's AI-generated content is a useful starting point rather than finished copy. Brand voice and factual accuracy need human review.
- **SERP analysis timeliness.** Frase's competitor data may not reflect the very latest SERP changes for highly dynamic keywords. Fast-moving news or trending topics benefit from a manual refresh.
- **No backlink data or keyword research.** Frase handles content optimization only. A full SEO workflow requires pairing it with [Ahrefs](../tools/ahrefs.md) or [Semrush](../tools/semrush.md) for keyword discovery and competitive backlink analysis.
- **Interface can feel cluttered.** The combination of brief view, optimizer, and AI tools in one screen can be overwhelming for first-time users.

## Bottom Line

Frase.io is the best AI SEO tool for content teams who need fast, SERP-backed brief generation at an affordable price. The brief creation workflow is genuinely faster than doing manual SERP research, and the integrated optimization scoring keeps writers on track throughout the drafting process. At $14.99/month, the Solo plan is the cheapest entry point in AI SEO tools — appropriate for evaluating the product or for very light publishing schedules. Agencies producing significant content volume will need the Team plan at $114.99/month. Compared to [Surfer SEO](../tools/surfer-seo.md), Frase is better for brief generation and overall workflow; Surfer is better for granular on-page content scoring.

## FAQ

**Is Frase.io worth it for solo bloggers?**
At $14.99/month for 4 articles, Frase is the cheapest AI SEO tool available. If you publish 1-4 SEO articles per month, it is an excellent value for the brief generation and optimization scoring alone. For higher publishing volumes, the Basic plan at $44.99 covers 30 articles per month.

**How does Frase compare to Surfer SEO?**
Frase is better at content brief generation — aggregating what competitors cover and turning it into a writing template. Surfer SEO is better at granular content scoring — tracking keyword density, NLP terms, and on-page structure in real time. Many serious SEO content teams use both together: Frase for briefs, Surfer for final optimization.
