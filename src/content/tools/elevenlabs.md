# ElevenLabs

ElevenLabs is the leading AI voice generation and text-to-speech platform developed by ElevenLabs, Inc. It converts text into human-quality speech and is primarily used for voice cloning, multilingual dubbing, audiobook narration, and conversational voice agents. ElevenLabs consistently ranks first in blind quality tests against competitors. As of April 2026, pricing ranges from free (10K characters/month) to $330/month (2M characters/month), with voice cloning available from the $5/month Starter plan. Compared to its closest competitor Fish Audio S2, ElevenLabs offers superior voice quality and a more polished platform, but at roughly double the API cost.

## What It Does

ElevenLabs generates human-quality speech from text, handling voice cloning, multilingual synthesis in 32+ languages, voice design, and conversational AI voice agents, with output indistinguishable from human speech in most contexts ([ElevenLabs](https://elevenlabs.io)). Voice cloning requires as little as 30 seconds of source audio to produce a usable clone ([ElevenLabs Docs](https://elevenlabs.io/docs)). The platform also supports entirely new voice creation via its Voice Design feature.

## Who It's For

- **YouTube creators:** faceless channels using AI narration (Creator plan = 6-10 videos/month)
- **Podcast producers:** AI narration, audio translation, clip generation
- **App developers:** voice agents, IVR systems, in-app TTS via API
- **Audiobook authors:** narrate a full book without a recording studio
- **Content localizers:** translate and dub content into 32+ languages

## Pricing

ElevenLabs costs $0-$330/month across five tiers, with voice cloning available on all paid plans starting at $5/month and API access included from the Starter tier ([ElevenLabs Pricing](https://elevenlabs.io/pricing)).

## Key Features

- **Voice quality:** best in class, consistently ranking first in blind quality tests with natural pacing, emotion, and emphasis ([ElevenLabs](https://elevenlabs.io)). The benchmark everyone else chases.
- **Instant Voice Cloning:** upload 30 seconds of audio, get a usable clone. Professional Voice Cloning (with consent verification) is even better ([ElevenLabs Docs](https://elevenlabs.io/docs)).
- **32+ languages:** same voice, different language. The dubbing quality is remarkable.
- **Voice Agents:** launched early 2026. Build conversational AI phone agents with natural speech. Competes with Bland.ai, Vapi.
- **Projects:** long-form editor for audiobooks and podcasts. Chapter management, pronunciation controls.
- **API:** well-documented REST and WebSocket APIs. Python, Node, and community SDKs.

## Limitations

ElevenLabs' main trade-off is cost at scale: API pricing runs $0.030/1K characters, roughly double competitors like Voxtral at $0.016, and it offers no self-hosted option ([ElevenLabs Pricing](https://elevenlabs.io/pricing)).

- **Price at scale.** $0.030/1K characters via API. Voxtral is $0.016. For high-volume API use, ElevenLabs is 2x more expensive.
- **Voice cloning ethics.** Easy to clone any voice from 30 seconds of audio. ElevenLabs has consent verification for Professional Cloning, but Instant Cloning is minimally gated.
- **Occasional artifacts.** Long passages can occasionally produce odd pauses or mispronunciations. Rare but noticeable in audiobook-length content.
- **No offline/self-hosted option.** Cloud-only. If you need on-premise TTS, look at [Fish Audio S2](../categories/ai-voice.md) or open-source alternatives.

## Bottom Line

ElevenLabs is the best choice for content creators and developers who need the highest-quality AI voice output with minimal setup, but Fish Audio S2 wins if self-hosting, privacy, or API cost savings are the priority. The Creator plan at $22/mo is the sweet spot for YouTube creators. Only skip ElevenLabs if you need API pricing under $0.02/1K chars (use Voxtral) or self-hosting (use Fish Audio S2).

## FAQ

**Is ElevenLabs free?**
Yes, ElevenLabs has a free plan that provides 10,000 characters per month (approximately 7 minutes of audio). The free plan does not include voice cloning or API access. It is sufficient for testing voice quality and generating short clips, but most serious users will need a paid plan.

**How much does ElevenLabs cost?**
ElevenLabs costs $5/month (Starter, 30K characters), $22/month (Creator, 100K characters), $99/month (Pro, 500K characters), or $330/month (Scale, 2M characters). API usage is billed at approximately $0.030 per 1,000 characters. All paid plans include voice cloning and API access.

**What are the best alternatives to ElevenLabs?**
Fish Audio S2 is the best alternative if you need open-source, self-hostable TTS at lower cost. Voxtral by Mistral offers cloud API at 47% less per character, though quality is lower. OpenAI TTS is a good option if you are already in the OpenAI ecosystem.
