## Overview

General-purpose AI chatbots (large language models, or LLMs) are the foundation layer of the AI tool economy . they handle writing, coding, reasoning, research, analysis, and conversation in a single interface. ChatGPT kicked off the mainstream era in November 2022, and by 2026 the category has consolidated around five serious players: **ChatGPT** (OpenAI), **Claude** (Anthropic), **Gemini** (Google), **Grok** (xAI), and **Mistral** (Mistral AI).

These tools aren't interchangeable. ChatGPT has the deepest feature surface . DALL-E image generation, voice mode, plugins, code interpreter. Claude wins on long-context reasoning with up to 1M tokens on Max plans and produces more thoughtful, less formulaic writing. Gemini integrates natively with Google Workspace and offers the cheapest price-per-token for raw API access. Grok differentiates on real-time X/Twitter integration. Mistral leads the open-weight commercial space with models you can self-host.

For most people, the winning move is to subscribe to **one** chatbot at Pro tier ($20/mo) . typically Claude for writers and reasoners, ChatGPT for general-purpose users who value features. API access for developers is priced per token and differs by model generation.

## Our Picks

- **Best overall:** Claude Pro ($20/mo). Best writing, best reasoning, 200K context on Pro / 1M on Max, best developer tool (Claude Code CLI).
- **Best features:** ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo). DALL-E image generation, voice mode, code interpreter, plugins. The broadest feature set.
- **Best for Google users:** Gemini Advanced ($20/mo). Deep Workspace integration, browsing, and long-context work.
- **Best open-weight:** Mistral. Self-hostable models at zero marginal inference cost.

## When to subscribe to more than one

Most people should only pay for one chatbot. The exceptions:

- **Developers** often keep both Claude Pro (for reasoning and Claude Code) and ChatGPT Plus (for DALL-E and plugin ecosystem). Total: $40/mo.
- **Content creators** may want Claude for writing + Gemini for Google Workspace integration.
- **Researchers** benefit from Claude (deep reasoning) + Perplexity (see [AI Search](/categories/ai-search/)) for web-grounded citations.
