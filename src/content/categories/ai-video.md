## Overview
AI video generation exploded in 2025-2026 and now includes six major tools spanning text-to-video, image-to-video, and AI avatar creation. Kling 3.0 by Kuaishou holds the number one ELO benchmark position, delivering the best overall video quality and value starting at $10 per month ([Kling AI](https://klingai.com)). Seedance 2.0 by ByteDance is a serious free-tier contender with strong motion quality, particularly for dance and rhythmic content ([Seedance](https://seedance.ai)). Runway Gen-4.5 maintains the most polished cinematic aesthetic and enterprise features at $15-95 per month ([Runway](https://runwayml.com)). OpenAI's entire Sora product line is now dead: the original Sora shut down March 24, 2026, and Sora 2 was discontinued on April 1, 2026 (see [Sora 2 shutdown page](../dead/sora.md)). The market has split into generation tools for creating video from text or images, and avatar tools like HeyGen and Synthesia for talking-head and corporate training content. For most creators, Kling 3.0 is the default recommendation for quality and value.
