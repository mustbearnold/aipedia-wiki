# May 2026 News Coverage Gap Implementation Plan

### 1. Objective

Cover material AI and AI tools news published after AiPedia's latest existing news items, keeping the `/news/` archive current, source-backed, image-backed, and useful for buyers deciding which AI tools to trust, buy, or administer.

### 2. Current state

The latest existing news records in `src/content/news/` are dated `2026-05-06`. News pages are generated by `src/pages/news/[slug].astro`, the archive is generated by `src/pages/news/index.astro`, and RSS is generated by `src/pages/news/rss.xml.ts`. News social cards and thumbnails are generated from frontmatter by `scripts/generate-og-news.mjs` into `public/og/news/` and `public/og/news/thumbs/`.

The news schema in `src/content.config.ts` supports `title`, `date`, `severity`, `summary`, `categories`, `affects`, `related_tools`, `sources`, and optional `hero`. The `affects` field is audited by `scripts/audit-news-xrefs.mjs`; any affected tool page must reference the news item.

### 3. Target state

AiPedia has source-backed articles for the material May 7-8, 2026 updates from OpenAI and GitHub, with generated OG images, current verification dates, and affected tool pages updated so tool readers see the new product and policy changes.

### 4. Scope

Included:
- Add new news markdown files under `src/content/news/`.
- Generate matching OG images and thumbnails.
- Update affected tool pages for `chatgpt`, `codex`, `github-copilot`, `claude`, and `grok`.
- Verify `/news/` top-layer archive, homepage latest-news modules, RSS, and LLM manifests are data-driven and do not require manual edits beyond content.
- Run news, link, content, build, and ship checks.

Excluded:
- Full redesign of news templates.
- Paid newsletter or external syndication.
- Claims that cannot be verified against source pages available on May 9, 2026.

### 6. Data model impact

No schema changes. New records use the current `news` collection fields. `affects` creates tool cross-reference obligations; `related_tools` controls visible tool rails.

### 7. SEO impact

Each new article gets a unique title, date, summary, canonical route, NewsArticle schema from `ArticlePlusLayout`, source citations, and archive visibility through `/news/`, RSS, homepage latest-news, site search JSON, and LLM full manifest.

### 8. Conversion impact

No affiliate CTAs are added. Revenue impact is indirect: fresher tool pages and news reinforce buyer trust around ChatGPT, Codex, GitHub Copilot, Claude, and Grok, improving confidence before visitors click through to tool pages and comparisons.

### 9. Mobile UX impact

News templates use generated 1200x630 imagery with responsive cards and the article layout's mobile-friendly source and impact boxes. No wide tables are added to the article bodies.

### 10. Implementation steps

1. Confirm latest covered date and existing news schema.
2. Research May 7-8, 2026 source-backed AI tools news from primary/vendor sources first.
3. Create one concise article per material source-backed item.
4. Add affected tool-page freshness bullets and update visible verification dates.
5. Generate OG images.
6. Run `npm run check:news`, `npm run check:links`, `npm run check:content`, and `npm run ship:check`.
7. Mobile-smoke `/news/` and representative new article at 390px if the dev server is available.

### 12. Acceptance criteria

- New articles only cover currently verified news after `2026-05-06`.
- Every article has sources, date, severity, categories, and buyer/admin analysis.
- Every affected tool page references the new article slugs.
- `/news/` and homepage are current through data-driven latest-news modules.
- Generated OG images exist for every new article.
- Checks pass or failures are documented with exact commands and causes.

### 13. Risks and mitigations

- Risk: too many low-value news snippets thin out the archive. Mitigation: only include material product, model, security, admin, or buyer-impact changes.
- Risk: source pages shift after publication. Mitigation: cite primary URLs and keep claims narrow.
- Risk: `affects` audit fails. Mitigation: update affected tool pages in the same checkpoint.
