  - Pitch vs Presentations.AI: Pitch for collaborative team deck operations; Presentations.AI for simple annual prompt-to-deck generation.
- Each page uses `last_updated: 2026-05-08` and `last_verified: 2026-05-08`.
- Pages avoid unsupported model names, fake context windows, and stale third-party ranking sources.
- Sources are official vendor pages checked on May 8, 2026.
- Each page includes quick verdict, winner by use case, pricing guidance, what to avoid, FAQ, and sources.

- Risk: Pages become too similar.
  - Mitigation: Make each page answer a distinct buyer job: brand-governed decks, interactive presentation delivery, multi-format publishing, and team deck operations.
- Risk: Gamma prices are not visible in the text extraction of the official pricing page.
  - Mitigation: Use official Gamma plan names and features verified from the pricing page, and avoid publishing exact Gamma prices unless currently visible from an official source.
- Risk: Presentations.AI pricing labels include early-bird discounts that can change.
  - Mitigation: Mention the May 8, 2026 observed pricing and tell buyers to check the live pricing page before purchase.
